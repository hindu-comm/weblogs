## Rites for pustaka-s

A pUja can be performed for pustaka-s expounding the shaivAgama, as per
the laid out procedure. When performing abhiSheka, it should be done to
the reflection in a mirror.

If a book which expounds the shaiva-siddhAnta is damaged, it should be
covered in ghee and consigned to fire as an oblation, followed by 100x
performance of the bahurUpi.
