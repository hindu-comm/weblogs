## Elements of the pAshupatavrata

The vAyavIya saMhita of the shiva purANa has a chapter on the pAshupata
vrata with many interesting details. Some notes from the same below:

mukha gandha & dhUpa:

To vAmadeva sandal paste is to be offered. Yellow orpiment to tatpuruSha
& bhasma to IshAna.

To aghora mukha dhUpa of white and black agaru should be shown. To
tatpuruSha fragrant gum resin. To saumya (vAmadeva?) perfumes are to be
offered. ushIra dhUpa to IshAna.

All the above are part of sAmAnya pUja.

The special feature of this vrata is to invoke rudra in a li\~Nga made
of specific gems for each month of the year. In vaishAka mAsa – vajra,
jyeSTha – Emerald, AShADa – Pearl, shrAvaNa – Sapphire, bhAdrapada –
Ruby, Ashvina – Onyx, kArthIka – Coral , mArgashIrSha – Lapis Lazuli,
puShya – Topaz, mAgha – Sun Stone, phAlguna – Moon Stone and chaitra –
Sun Stone. If gems are not available gold should be substituted every
month.

If Gold is not available, Silver, copper, rock or clay or even with Lac.
The performer should wear matted hair and hold a staff for the time
period of the vrata.
