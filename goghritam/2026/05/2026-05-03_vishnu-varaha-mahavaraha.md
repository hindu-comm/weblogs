devAmR^ita-varShiNI – देवामृत वर्षिणी

## viShNu-varAha! mahAvarAha!

[![](https://goghritam.wordpress.com/wp-content/uploads/2026/03/gemini_generated_image_2wwlww2wwlww2wwl.png?w=582)](https://goghritam.wordpress.com/wp-content/uploads/2026/03/gemini_generated_image_2wwlww2wwlww2wwl.png)

mahAvarAha mAlA mantra:

shiva uvAcha:

Hear, O auspicious one (*Kalyani*), I shall speak the great and powerful *Mala Mantra* of Lord Vishnu in His boar form (*Krodarupa*), which destroys all sins.

It is the cure for incurable diseases like consumption (tuberculosis), and it is capable of removing the afflictions caused by unparalleled enemies.

It drives away ghosts, spirits, demons, epilepsy, and madness, O *Shive* (Parvati).

Having meditated thus and chanted this mantra a hundred times, one effortlessly wards off ghosts, spirits, demons, and other such entities.

By chanting it fifty times, one destroys the secret plots of enemies in an instant. By chanting it twenty-five times, one destroys the harmful spells or black magic (*Parakrutyam*) cast by others.

O Goddess! By chanting it ten times, one can remove a multitude of diseases. If one energizes sacred ash (*Bhasma*) with this mantra and places it on the heart of a sick person…

…while touching them and reciting this mantra twelve times, O slender-waisted one (*Tanumadhyame*), their body becomes light and free from suffering in an instant!

If one energizes pure sand with this mantra and throws it secretly into an enemy’s army, that army will be paralyzed (rendered inactive) by that act.

Just as a bridge of sand dissolves quickly in water, the enemy’s strength dissolves. Whatever desire a person holds in their heart while chanting this mantra…

…that desire is surely attained by that man or woman, O Dear one! Thus, O Devi, I have told you the Great Mala Mantra of the immensely radiant Lord Vishnu in His Mahavaraha form.

varAha dhyAna bhedAH:

## Different Meditations and Applications of Varaha

**Lord Shiva said:**

1.  Hear, O Devi, I shall describe the various forms of meditation and
    the different ritual applications (*Prayoga*) of the great Lord
    Varaha, O fair-faced one (*Varānanē*). 2.  By which ancient sages, O Devi, attained their desired fruits on
    this earth. (Meditation on the Cosmic Body): On His **tusk** rests
    the Earth with its mountains, cities, forests, and rivers. His
    **grunt/roar** is the sound of the Vedas (*Vagisham*). His
    **breath** is the wind (*Anilam*). 3.  The **Sun and Moon** are His arms. The **Vasus** are His joints. The
    **Directions** are His ears. The **Ashwini Kumaras** are His eyes.
    Padmottha i.e. **Lord Brahma** is His feet. **Lord Hari** is in His
    heart, and **Lord Shankara** (Shiva) resides in His face/mukha. 4.  One who meditates on this Boar-formed sustainer of the Earth in this
    manner becomes equal to all the gods; of this, there is no doubt. 5.  Meditation on Him in the **Earth element** brings peace and destroys
    enemies. In the **Fire element**, it brings subjection/attraction
    (*Vashya*). In the **Wind element**, it is used for driving away
    enemies (*Ucchatana*). 6.  In the **Ether element**, it protects against spirits and planetary
    afflictions. In war, in chariots, or in battle, Lord Varaha grants
    victory to those who meditate on Him. 7.  One should meditate on the self as being non-different from the
    Boar-formed Lord, the enemy of Hiranyaksha, and chant the supreme
    mantra. 8.  O Devi! One who chants the **Bhu-Varaha mantra** a hundred times
    with focus will surely and suddenly achieve victory over enemies in
    battle. 9.  On a Tuesday (*Angaravare*), one who knows the mantra should take
    mud from a disputed field and perform a hundred circumambulations
    (*Pradakshina*); that field will then become theirs. 10. One who daily touches the earth while chanting the mantra a thousand
    times will obtain great land, free from rivals and thorns
    (obstacles). 11. If one sits in a disputed field on their birthday and chants the
    mantra 1008 times, O Dear one, they will never face a land dispute
    again in that life. 12. One who performs a *Laksha-Homa* (100,000 fire oblations) with
    milk-rice (*Payasam*) mixed with ghee will obtain the seven islands
    (the whole world). There is no need to deliberate further on this. 13. Thus, O Devi, I have told you the various meditations and
    applications of the Boar-form of Lord Vishnu, who possesses immense
    splendor.

varAha gAyatrI mantra:

तीक्ष्णदंष्ट्राय विद्महे महाकायाय धीमहि । तन्नो वराहः प्रचोदयात् ॥

*tīkṣṇadaṃṣṭrāya vidmahe mahākāyāya dhīmahi \|* *tanno varāhaḥ pracodayāt \|\|*

## The Varaha Gayatri Mantra

**Lord Shiva said:**

1.  Hear, O Devi! I shall proclaim the **Gayatri of the Boar-formed**
    (*Krodarupina*) Vishnu, which destroys all sins and removes all
    miseries. 2.  It breaks the spells, mantras, and yantras cast by others. It grants
    sovereignty to those desiring a kingdom and liberation (*Mukti*) to
    those seeking moksha. 3.  **The Mantra:Om Tikshna-damshtraya Vidmahe Mahakayaya Dhimahi \|**
    **Tanno Varahah Prachodayat \|\|** *(Translation: We know the one
    with sharp tusks; we meditate upon the one with the great body. May
    that Varaha inspire/illumine us.)* 4.  Thus is the Gayatri spoken by the knowers of the Vedas. The Sage
    (*Rishi*) is **Bhargava**, the Meter (*Chhandas*) is **Gayatri**,
    and the Deity is **Varaha**. The application (*Viniyoga*) is
    according to one’s desire. 5.  I shall now speak of the method of meditation (*Dhyana*), which
    grants all desires: 6.  In the Earth element (*Parthive Mandale*), meditate on Him as
    **golden** (*Hema-prabham*). In the Water element, as **white**
    (*Nihara-abham*). In the Fire element, as **shining like a red
    lotus**. In the Wind element, meditate on the Boar as **black**
    (*Krishnam*) and filling the sky, seated upon a lotus. 7.  One who meditates thus and chants this Gayatri a hundred times will
    never fear death, thieves, enemies, or spirits. 8.  O fair-faced one (*Varanane*)! If one energizes water by chanting
    this mantra a hundred times and gives it to a sick person to drink,
    they will surely be cured of their disease. 9.  If one energizes food with this mantra and feeds it to a barren
    woman for three months (or half that time), she will surely conceive
    a child. 10. If one energizes ash (*Bhasma*) with this Gayatri and sprinkles it
    on the head of someone afflicted by a “Graha” (negative planetary
    spirit/seizure) every evening… 11. …within a fortnight, that spirit will leave and go elsewhere. If a
    man drinks ghee energized by this mantra… 12. …even if he is consumed by sins, he becomes free from all sins
    within a fortnight, O Beautiful one (*Bhamini*)! 13. Thus, O Devi, I have told you the purifying and sin-destroying
    Gayatri of Varaha, which I have kept secret.

shiva R^iShiH, bR^ihatI chandaH, varAho devatA  
oM bIjaM, svAhA shaktiH.

dhyAnaM:*kālameghasamaprabhaṃ kanakāmbaraṃ karapaṅkajaiḥ \|* *śaṅkhacakragadāvarān paribibhrataṃ vanamālinam \|\|* *hāranūpurakuṇḍalāṅgadabhūṣitaṃ bhuvanādhipam \|* *potrirūpadharaṃ hariṃ hṛdi bhāvayāmi dharādharam \|\|*

I meditate in my heart upon Lord Hari, the sustainer of the Earth (*Dharadharam*), who has taken the form of a Boar (*Potri-rupa-dharam*). He has the complexion of a dark rain-cloud (**Kalamegha-samaprabham**) and wears golden garments (**Kanakambaram**). In His lotus-like hands, He holds the Shankha (conch), Chakra (discus), Gada (mace), and the Varada (boon-granting) mudra. He is adorned with a forest garland (*Vanamala*), anklets, earrings, and armlets, and is the Lord of the worlds.
