devAmR^ita-varShiNI – देवामृत वर्षिणी

## nR^isiMha AvR^itIshArchanaM

![](https://goghritam.files.wordpress.com/2022/08/image.png?w=645)

Brief note from a late pA\~ncharAtrika text: The AvaraNa pUja with 8 AvaraNa-s to be done as below for the mantrarAja.

1 a\~NgAH

2 dikShu: vainateya, sheSha, sharva, kamalasaMbhava vidikShu: shrI, hrI, tuShTi, puShTi

3 aShTa nR^isiMha mUrtayaH in prAgAdi krama: stambhkArI, vashyakArI, mAraNakArI, vidveShakArI, AkarSha(na)kArI, puShTiprada, nidhiprada, vidyAprada

4 devasya daxiNe: chakra, khaDga, mahApadma, musala. vAme: sha\~Nkha, kheTa, gadA, shAra\~Nga.

5 laxmI – daxiNe, tuShTi , kaustubha – vAme shrIvatsaM daxiNe, vanamAlAM – madhye, pItAmbaraM, puShpasUtraM, nAbhipadmaM, kirITa – puro bhAge

6 aShTa dixu: shraddhA, medhA, kAmA, bhImA, mAyA, ArdrA, dIpti in pUrvAdi krama

7 & 8 aShTadikpAla /lokapAla & Ayudha.

The aShTa nR^isiMha mUrti are to be meditated as below:

stambhakArI nR^isiMha: Of golden from with sha\~Nkha chakra wearing black clothes

vashyakArI nR^isiMha: Of reddish hue holding the sha\~Nkha chakra gadA and padma

mAraNakArI nR^isiMha:With AntramAla, sha\~Nkha chakra gadA khaDga and of black color, three eyes and is to be meditated as tearing the daitya apart

vidveShaNakArI nR^isiMha: Of the color of nIlotpala, with sha\~Nkha chakra gadA, chApa and daNDa

AkarShaNa nR^isiMha: Of the color of japa puShpa with sha\~Nkha chakra khaDga & pAsha along with his shakti(? text has shakti yuktaM)

puShTiprada mUrti: Of various hues, with sha\~Nkha chakra gadA abhaya and is three eyed.

nidhiprada nR^isiMha: Is to be meditated as of green color, holding the pA\~nchajanya, sudarshana, gada and nidhi in his four hands with laxmI on his side.

vidyAprada nR^isiMha: He is to be seen as white as milk with pItAmbara and is holding the sha\~Nkha chakra pAsha & a\~nkusha.
