SONDEKOPPA RAMACHANDRA SASTRI RAMASWAMY departed yesterday just as he had lived. In serenity. In peace. And left behind a chasm that can never be filled. Writing about him in the past tense feels surreal for he endures as a profound Presence.

At 88, his mind was still as sharp as it had been when he was in his prime but for the fact that age had taken it…
