THE FIRST RECORDED ALIEN INVASION OF INDIA began with Alexander and ended with Ahmad Shah Adbali (Durrani). From faraway Greece to neighbouring Afghanistan. Throughout this prolonged timepsan, India had been subjected to scores of such alien invasions and finally ended up losing her freedom first to Turkic Muslim invaders and then to the colonial British.

However, during the same period,not one alien rule managed to permanently damage Bharatavarsha's civilisational core enconsced within her Vedantic kernel.

**In just about half an hour**, **this podcast narrates the full story of India's eventual civilisational triumph.** It covers a broad sweep of this history, giving rare anecdotes and precious insights generally not found in mainstream narratives.
