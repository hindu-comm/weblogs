CHANNAPATTANA VASUDEVAIAH WAS ONCE A HOUSEHOLD NAME in Karnataka, affectionately renowned for his elementary schoolbooks teaching the basics of Kannada grammar and composition to children. Titled *Kannaḍa bāla bōdhe* (a rough translation is, *Kannada Lessons for Children*), he wrote them out of a deep spirit of service, which in turn reflected the zeitgeist…
