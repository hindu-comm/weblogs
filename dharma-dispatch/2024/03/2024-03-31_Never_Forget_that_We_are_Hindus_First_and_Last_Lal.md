IN 1916, THE EDITOR of the *Hindusthanee Student*, invited Lala Lajpat Rai to write a short message of advice to the Indian students studying and living in America.

More than a century after he wrote it, it remains more relevant than ever. If anything, large sections of Hindus — both students and otherwise — in America have become more thoroughly de-Hindu…
