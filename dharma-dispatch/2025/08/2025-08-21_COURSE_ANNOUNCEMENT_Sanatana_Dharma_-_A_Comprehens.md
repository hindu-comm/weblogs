VIBHU ACADEMY announces the launch of an online course titled **Sanatana Dharma: A Comprehensive Study.**

The course covers all the major aspects of Hindu Dharma including fundamental concepts such as Veda,-Agama-Darshana, Dharma, Varna and Ashrama, social, cultural and environmental dimensions, folk traditions and contemporary challenges.

The course is offered in English is spread over 21 sessions with a total duration exceeding 30 hours. It is open to anyone aged 13+.

Details of the course are as follows:

Start Date: August 28 - 13 October, 2025

Total Sessions: 21

Days: Tuesday, Thursday, Saturday

Time: 7 P.M. - 8:30 P.M., India Time

Tutors: **Dr Arathi V B,** **Arjun Bharadwaj** and **Sandeep Balakrishna**

**LAST DATE FOR REGISTRATION**: August 24, 2023

**To register:**

Send an email to: vibhuacademygurukula@gmail.com

or

Contact: 9480318113 or 9611131760

Registrations are currently open.

Hurry up and book your slot!

For full details of the course including the topics covered, see the poster below.

For those who wish to get a comprehensive understanding of Sanatana Dharma, this unique course provides a clear and accurate exposition drawn from primary sources and led by experts in each domain.
