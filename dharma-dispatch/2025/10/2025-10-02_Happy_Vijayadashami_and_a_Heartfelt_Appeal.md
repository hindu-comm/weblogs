ON THIS SACRED Vijaya Dasami festival, The Dharma Dispatch would like to make a heartfelt appeal: If you find inspiration, joy, and value in the content published on The Dharma Dispatch, **please consider supporting it financially**.

We run this publication out of a sense of sacred civilisational duty to Sanatana Dharma and as a labour of love and commitment. A lot goes on behind the scenes. It takes us hundreds of hours each month to research, travel, write, shoot, interview, edit, and significant costs to sustain this publication. Since inception, we’ve made a conscious decision to *not* accept corporate funding or advertising so that our work remains independent *forever.* We’d like to keep it that way so that no compromise comes in the way of our sustained fight against Asuric forces and for the restoration of Sanatana Dharma in troubled times.
