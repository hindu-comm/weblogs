# Lessons from D.V. Gundappa’s 1959 Letter to B.D. Jatti and G.B. Pant

### In January 1959, DVG wrote two acerbic letters to the then Mysore Chief Minister B.D. Jatti and sent its copies to the Home Minister, G.B. Pant. It triggered a chain of govt correspondence.
