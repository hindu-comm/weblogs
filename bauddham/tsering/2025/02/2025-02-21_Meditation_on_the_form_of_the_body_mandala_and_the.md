## Visualisation of the base, structure of the mandala in your body

- Crown: *Viśvavajra* (Double crossed-vajra)

- Soles of the feet: *Vajrabhūmi* (Vajra-ground)

- Ribcage: *Vajraprākāra* (Vajra-fence)

- Skin: *Vajrapañjara* (Vajra-canopy)

- Body hairs: *Sarajāla* (Net-of-arrows)

- Finger and toe nails: *Jvālānalārka* (Blazing circle of flames)

- Soles of the feet: *Vāyu* (Sphere of air, shape of bow when sitting
  with cross-legged)

- *Śṛṅgāṭaka* (place where three channels meets-four inches below the
  navel, shape of groin): Agni (sphere of fire triangular shape)

- Belly: *Udaka* (Sphere of water, round shape of belly)

- Chest: *Pṛthivī* (Sphere of earth, square shape of chest)

- Backbones: *Sumeru* (Mount *Sumeru*)

- Head: Top/peak of of Mount *Meru*

- Four *Nāḍī*-petals of the four directions of the heart chakra (four
  gates): are the four doors of the body (mouth, nose, genitals and
  anus).

- Two shanks, two thighs, two forearms, two upper arms: These eight are
  eight pillars (of mandala’s four gates)

- Eyes: *Pañcarekhā* (Five-lines)

- Nose: architrave \[of mansion\]

- Teeth and intestines: Fringe networks

- Tongue and lips: *kāmaguṇa* (terrace of sense pleasure)

- Ears: *toraṇa* (archway)

## Visualising the deities of the mandala in your body

Buddha Akshobhya

- **Heart**: The place of the mind or consciousness is the heart
  \[chest\] region when the human body is first formed. So visualise the
  Deities beginning from the heart. From the syllable *Hūm* (ཧཱུཾ) of the
  heart chakra, visualise the vajra mind Akshobhya, blue in complexion,
  holding a vajra in his right hand and his left arm hanging down,
  holding a bell in his left hand. Visualise the mother Māmakī
  (consort), of the same complexion as the father \[Akshobhya\], sitting
  in the manner of embracing \[the father\], holding a crooked knife
  (*kartrī*) in her right hand and a skull cup (*kapāla*) in her left
  hand. And visualise them surrounded by eight goddesses \[on the eight
  *Nāḍī*-petals\].

- **Navel**: From the syllable *Aṃ* (ཨཾ) of navel chakra, visualise the
  father Ratnasambhava, yellow in complexion (symbolising earth),
  holding *ratna* (precious gem) in his right hand and his left arm
  hanging down holding a bell in his left hand. Visualise the mother
  Locanā (consort), of the same complexion as the father
  \[Ratnasamabhava\], sitting in the manner of embracing \[the father\],
  holding a crooked knife (*kartrī*) in her right hand and a skull cup
  (*kapāla*) in her left hand. And visualise them surrounded by
  sixty-four goddesses \[on the sixty-four *Nāḍī*-petals\].

- **Perineum-loins**: From the syllable *Ha* (ཧ)of perineum chakra,
  visualise the father Amoghasiddhi, green in complexion, holding a
  sword in his right hand and his left arm hanging down holding a bell
  in his left hand. Visualise the mother Samaya-tārā (consort), of the
  same complexion as the father \[Amoghasiddhi\], sitting in the manner
  of embracing \[the father\], holding a crooked knife (*kartrī*) in her
  right hand and a skull cup (*kapāla*) in her left hand. And visualise
  them surrounded by thirty-two goddesses.

- **Throat**: From the syllable *Oṃ* (ཨོཾ) of throat chakra, visualise the
  vajra speech father Amitabha, red in complexion, holding a lotus in
  his right hand and his left arm hanging down holding a bell in his
  left hand. Visualise the mother Pāṇḍara­vāsinī (consort), of the same
  complexion as the father \[Amitabha\], sitting in the manner of
  embracing \[the father\], holding a crooked knife (*kartrī*) in her
  right hand and a skull cup (*kapāla*) in her left hand. And visualise
  them surrounded by sixteen goddesses \[on the sixteen *Nāḍī*-petals\].

- **Crown**: From the syllable *Haṃ* (ཧཾ) of the crown chakra, visualise
  the vajra body father Vairocana, white in complexion, holding a wheel
  in his right hand and his left arm hanging down holding a bell in his
  left hand. Visualise the mother Dhātvīśvarī (consort), of the same
  complexion as the father \[Vairocana\], sitting in the manner of
  embracing \[the father\], holding a crooked knife (*kartrī*) in her
  right hand and a skull cup (*kapāla*) in her left hand. And visualise
  them surrounded by thirty-two goddesses \[on the thirty-two
  *Nāḍī*-petals\].

- **Additional visualisation**: Visualise the deities of the twelve
  sense bases (*Āyatana*) and the ten wrathful deities of the syllable
  *Hūm*. Visualise the form of the channel as *Nirmāṇakāya*, the vital
  air as *Svābhāvikakāya*, the syllables in the channels as
  *Sambhogakāya*, the Amrita in the channel as *Dharmakāya*. In this
  way, visualise the four bodies of the Buddha as spontaneously existing
  in your body.
