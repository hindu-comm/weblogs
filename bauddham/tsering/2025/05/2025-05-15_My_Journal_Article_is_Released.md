Hello everyone,

I'm pleased to share that my peer-reviewed article, titled *"Yoga Posture and Forceful Yoga: Tibetan Amṛtasiddhi Text by Amoghavajra,"* has been published in the *Journal of Yoga Studies*. This article may be of interest to those studying or practicing yoga, particularly with a focus on yoga postures. The findings presented in the article suggest that the *Amṛtasiddhi* tradition of Vajrayāna and the Brahmanical Haṭha Yoga tradition not only share key practices such as (Haṭha yogic) mudrās and bandhas but may also exhibit similarities in certain yoga postures.

Below is the link and abstract of the article. <https://journalofyogastudies.org/index.php/JoYS/article/view/106/JoYS.V6.002>
