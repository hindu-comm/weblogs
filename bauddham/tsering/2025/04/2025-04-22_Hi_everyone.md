**Hi everyone!**

It’s been almost three years since I started this Substack page. I’ve aimed to share a new post each week, and there are now around 165 posts — primarily focused on *Amṛtasiddhi Yoga*, the *Shadangayoga* practices of *Kalachakra Tantra*, and various other Vajrayana yoga practices such as *Tummo*, *Lu Jong*, *Trulkhors,* etc.

Lately, I’ve been wondering if there are any specific topics you’d like me to cover, or any questions you might have about Vajrayana yoga within the Tibetan tradition that I could explore in future posts. If so, feel free to reach out via email at **tseringdorjinewsletter@outlook.com** — I may be able to share insights based on the original texts through my translations.

**Have a good day!**  
Tsering Dorji
