``` text Visualise one’s body, hollow inside. The air in the centre of the navel, Is a fine, bluish, a size of a mustard seed. Yogi should attend one's mind to that. 

Yogi, your very mind will emanate. 
Imagine the mind merging into the body’s empty space, Like a cloud into the sky. 

Yogi, the moment the (wild and rough) male vital-air (in the belly) stirs, Such that, the (hot) air of the fire (at the navel) will cause the fall of nectar. 
Visualise a blue circle of wind, At the meeting point of three channels, On the spinal joints of the back. 

Fire at the navel and water at the heart, Vapour rises from the earth at the throat. 
Fire fills the nondual Avadhuti channel. 

Imagine the bee of mind, Sucking nectar from the nectaries, Of the full grown fiery flower of banana. 

As the wind kindles the fire and boils the water, 
Heat generated will cause a shower of nectar from the sky. 
And imagine the nectar filling the hollow body. 
This will bring a great bliss in the body.

The bow of wind and the arrow of mind. From the crown's great bliss chakra's bindu HUM, The elements from top to bottom will merge into one another in stages. Attend one's mind to the rainbow coloured, From the (navel's) earth element to the (crown's) expanse of space. 
Both outer and inner bodhi will manifes to you. 
The nondual pristine consciousness will arise by itself. I, Sutili, am imcomprehensible (acintya), Have attained the Siddhi by the grace of Guru.

```
