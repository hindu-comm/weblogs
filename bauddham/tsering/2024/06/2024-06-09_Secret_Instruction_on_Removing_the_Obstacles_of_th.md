This text, 'The Secret Instruction on Removing the Obstacles of the Vital Air', appears to be popular and is available in various Tibetan Buddhist traditions such as Nyingma, Sakya, Kagyu and Jonang. The translation of this text is based on a Tibetan text associated with the Kagyu tradition. This text is also included in the Collected Works of Marpa Choekyi Lodoe. This post contains the translation of the five primary *vāyu* sections. I will post the translation of the five secondary *vāyu* in my next article.

### Translation of the Text

I bow down to the true gurus (sadguruḥ).

From (Nagarjuna’s) Pañca krama (text):

The vital air (vāyu), which is the life force (prāṇa) of all sentient beings, is the doer of all the activities of both samsara (through the karmic air - karma-vāyu) and nirvana (the vital air of wisdom - jñāna-vāyu). It is the vehicle on which (the six) consciousness rides (and moves towards its objects). There are five primary vāyu and five secondary vāyu, ten in all.

The meaning of this verse is:

One should know these two aspects, the method of controlling the vāyu and how to overcome it when the vāyu is being controlled in the wrong way. One should know this with the five primary vāyu and five secondary vāyu, ten in all.

### Five primary *vāyu* (རྩ་བའི་རླུང་ལྔ)

Each of the five primary vāyu is explained in five aspects: its location, function, yogic technique, signs of success and failure in conquering the vāyu, and method of overcoming if the vāyu is controlled in the wrong way.

#### I. *Apāna vāyu* (ཐུར་སེལ་གྱི་རླུང)

##### (i) Location (གནས)

The location is: Apāna vāyu resides in the genital area.

##### (ii) Function (བྱེད་ལས)

Its function is to expel faeces and urine downwards.

##### (iii) Technique (སྒོམ་ཐབས)

The technique is to contract and relax the anal sphincter. If you want pleasure (bliss), practise this.

##### (iv) Signs of success and failure in conquering the *apāna vāyu* (ཟིན་རྟགས་ལོག་ཟིན་མ་ལོག་ཟིན)

If the *apāna vāyu* is controlled in the right way, it will bring benefits. If it is controlled in a wrong way, it will cause physical ailments such as pain in the lower openings and stoppage of faeces and urine.

##### (v) Methods to overcome these harmful effects (ལོག་གནོན)

The method is: rub the ears with oil. While keeping your body in an inverted position (upside down, balancing on your hands without letting your head touch the ground), and shake your lower body. (Let your companion beat the soles of your feet, and gently beat the vertebral joints from the coccyx with his fists. (In that position) shake your lower body by bending your knees and stretching out legs. Focus your mind on the air rushing down through the earholes. At times, focus your mind at the cranial aperture.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

#### II. *Samāna vāyu (མཉམ་གནས་སམ་མེ་མཉམ་གྱི་རླུང)*

##### (i) Location (གནས)

The location is: Samānavāyu resides in the abdomen.

##### (ii) Function (བྱེད་ལས)

(It generates heat) and helps digest both solid and liquid food.

##### (iii) Technique (སྒོམ་ཐབས)

The yogic technique is to fix the ocean (belly) to the back (spine) and from time to time twist the waist left and right. If you wish to have great heat, practise this.

##### (iv) Signs of success and failure in conquering the *Samānavāyu* (ཟིན་རྟགས་ལོག་ཟིན་མ་ལོག་ཟིན)

If the samānavāyu is controlled in the right way, it will bring benefits. If it is controlled in the wrong way, it will cause physical ailments such as pain in the kidneys, bloating of the stomach and diarrhoea, mental sinking (drowsiness), and difficulty in digesting food and drink.

##### (v) Methods to overcome these harmful effects (ལོག་གནོན)

With a warm oil sprinkled with a Tibetan beer from your mouth, turn and twist your waist, fixing the ocean (the belly) to the cliff (the spine), massage the abdomen with your hands.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

#### III. *Prāṇa vāyu* (སྲོག་འཛིན་གྱི་རླུང)

##### (i) Location (གནས)

Prāṇa vāyu resides in the heart region (chest).

##### (ii) Function (བྱེད་ལས)

Breathing in and breathing out is its function. It holds life in the body.

##### (iii) Technique (སྒོམ་ཐབས)

Relax your mind in an artless way, and always press down the prāṇa vāyu. If you want bliss to be infused into your body, practise this over and over again.

##### (iv) Signs of success and failure in conquering the *prāṇa vāyu* (ཟིན་རྟགས་ལོག་ཟིན་མ་ལོག་ཟིན)

If the prāṇa vāyu is controlled in the right way, it will bring benefits. If it is controlled in the wrong way, it will cause physical afflictions such as mental anguish, blurred memory, anger, restlessness of mind, and insanity.

##### (v) Methods to overcome these harmful effects (ལོག་གནོན)

Massage your body first. (Next) Hold the air, shake and twist your upper body, and when you release the air, exhale with the sound Ha. (Next) Jump into the water or immerse your body in the water, stay on the waves and do various methods to create fear.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

#### IV. *Udāna vāyu* (གྱེན་རྒྱུའི་རླུང)

##### (i) Location (གནས)

Udāna vāyu resides in the throat region.

##### (ii) Function (བྱེད་ལས)

Its function is to propel saliva and mucus upwards.

##### (iii) Technique (སྒོམ་ཐབས)

Close your nose and centre your mind and air on your forehead. If you wish for emptiness, practise this.

##### (iv) Signs of success and failure in conquering the *Udāna vāyu* (ཟིན་རྟགས་ལོག་ཟིན་མ་ལོག་ཟིན)

If the udāna vāyu is controlled in the right way, it will bring benefits (such as unblocking the throat channels). If it is controlled in the wrong way, it will cause physical ailments such as throat pain and becomes mute, swollen larynx, extreme difficulty in breathing in and out, and can be a danger to life.

##### (v) Methods to overcome these harmful effects (ལོག་གནོན)

Focus your mind on your navel. Tighten your body parts, spread your lips and eyes wide and make your body tremble. Let your companion, without letting your feet touch the ground, beat your shoulders with their palms, hold your upper body in their arms and chest and shake your body, pulling your head in turns to the right and left.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

#### V. *Vyāna vāyu* (ཁྱབ་བྱེད་ཀྱི་རླུང)

##### (i) Location (གནས)

It resides in the joints of your body.

##### (ii) Function (བྱེད་ལས)

It performs walking, sitting and other body movements.

##### (iii) Technique (སྒོམ་ཐབས)

The technique is the Lion Liberation Mudra (blocking the nine doors of the body with the fingers). If you wish miraculous power, practise this.

##### (iv) Signs of success and failure in conquering the vyana vāyu (ཟིན་རྟགས་ལོག་ཟིན་མ་ལོག་ཟིན)

If the vyāna vāyu is controlled in the right way, it will bring benefits. If it is controlled in the wrong way, it will cause physical ailments such as pain in the vertebral joints, contractures of the limbs, difficulty in bending and stretching the limbs.

##### (v) Methods to overcome these harmful effects (ལོག་གནོན)

Massage your body first. (Next) rotate your waist and throw the lasso. Wherever the pain is, visualise a hollow sieve-like shape (with many holes) and focus on the twenty channel openings (visualise the pain going out through these holes). (Next) Hold the air and do running and jumping. Then do a lot of bending and stretching of the limbs.
