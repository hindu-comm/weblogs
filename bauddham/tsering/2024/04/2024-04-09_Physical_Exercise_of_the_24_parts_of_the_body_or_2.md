### Location of 24 sacred places in the human body according to Taranatha's text

#### I. Pīṭha/གནས།

1.  Pullīramalaya (head) མགོ་བོ

2.  Jālandhara (crown of the head) སྤྱི་གཙུག

3.  Uḍḍiyāna (right ear) རྣ་བ་གཡས་

4.  Arbuda (back bones) རྒྱབ་ཀྱི་ཚིགས་

#### II. Upapīṭham/ཉེ་བའི་གནས

5.  Godāvarī (left ear) རྣ་བ་གཡོན་

6.  Rāmeśvara (spot between the eyebrows) སྨིན་མའི་དབུས

7.  Devīkoṭa (two eyes) མིག་གཉིས་

8.  Mālava (shoulders) དཔུང་མགོ

#### III. Kṣetra/ཞིང་

9.  Kāmarūpa (armpits) མཆན་ཁུང

10. Oḍra (breasts) ནུ་མ་

#### IV. Upakṣetram/ཉེ་བའི་ཞིང་

11. Triśakuni (navel) ལྟེ་བའི་དབུས་

12. Kośala (nose-tip) སྣ་རྩེ་

#### V. Chandoha/ཚན་དོ་ཧ་

13. Kaliṅga (mouth) ཁ་

14. Lampāka (throat) མགྲིན་དབུས་

#### VI. Upacchandoha/ཉེ་བའི་ཚན་དོ་ཧ་

15. Kāñcī (chest) སྙིང་

16. Himālaya (perineum) གསང་རྩ་

#### VII. Melāpakam/འདུས་པ་ཅན་

17. Pretapurī (genitals) མཚན

18. Gṛhadevatā (anus) བཤང་ལམ

#### VIII. Upamelāpakam/ཉེ་བའི་འདུས་པ་ཅན་

19. Saurāṣṭra (thighs) བརླ་གཉིས་

20. Suvarṇadvīpa (shanks) རྗེ་ངར་གཉིས་

#### IX. Śmaśānakam/དུར་ཁྲོད་

21. Nagara (fingers, toes) སོར་མོ་

22. Sindu (ankles) རྐང་བོལ་

#### X. Upaśmaśānakam/ཉེ་བའི་དུར་ཁྲོད་

23. Maru (thumbs) མཐེ་བོང་གཉིས་

24. Kulatā (knees) པུས་མོ་གཉིས་

I.

> Rotate and massage the *Uḍḍiyāna* (right ear) and the *Godāvarī* (left
> ear).

II\.

> Rotate *Devīkoṭas* (eyes) and turn them in all four directions (to
> left, to the right, up and down).

III\.

> Massage the *Kośala* (nose-tip).

IV\.

> Draw the *Rāsi (?)*

V. & VI

> Shake and rotate the *Pullīramalaya* (head).

VII\.

> Tap and massage the *jālandhara* (crown of the head).

VIII\.

> Massage *Arbuda* (spine) with both hands.

IX\.

> Rub *Rāmeśvara* (spot between the eyebrows)

X.

> Rotate *Mālava* (shoulders)

XI\. & XII.

> Massage, press and twist *Kāmarūpa* (under arms/armpits) and *Oḍra*
> (breasts).

XIII\.

> Stir *Triśakuni* (navel).

XIV\.

> Open wide *Kaliṅga* (mouth)

XV\.

> Twist *Lampāka* (throat)

XVI\.

> Twist and beat *Kāñcī* (heart/chest)

XVII\.

> *Himālaya* (perineum) spread apart and twist.

XVIII\.

> Bind and beat *Gṛhadevatā* (anus).

XIX\.

> *Saurāṣṭra* (thighs), make supple.

XX\. & XXI

> Massage *Suvarṇadvīpa* (shanks) and *Sindhu* (ankles).

XXII\. & XXIII

> Hold the *Kulatā* (knees) with your hands. Move the abdomen as you
> stand up and sit down.

XXIV\.

> Beat the back and massage your whole body.
