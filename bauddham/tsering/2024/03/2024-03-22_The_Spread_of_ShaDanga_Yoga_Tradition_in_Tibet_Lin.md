### I. Well-known spread of the practice of Shadanga Yoga system in Tibet

#### 1. Dissemination of the Shadanga Yoga practice system in the early period by four individuals/lotsawas)

\(i\) Lotsawa Gyi jo Dawa’i Woeser (Candrabhanu?).

\(ii\) Dro Lotsawa Sherab Drakpa (passed down from Kashmiri Pandita Somnatha).

\(iii\) Ra Lotsawa Dorje Drakpa (received from Pandita Samanta Shri).

\(iv\) Dingri Choekyi Drakpa’s tradition.

#### 2. Dissemination of the Shadanga Yoga practice system in the middle period by five individuals

\(i\) Kashmiri Pandita Shakya Shri.

\(ii\) Menlung pa (Menlung Guru).

\(iii\) Vibhuticandra.

\(iv\) Tharlo Nyima Gyaltsen.

\(v\) Orgyen pa (Rinchen Pal).

#### 3. Dissemination of the Shadanga Yoga practice system in the later period by two individuals

\(i\) Pandita Vanaratna.

\(ii\) Sangye Baspa Gonpo (Buddha Gupta Natha).

### II. Seventeen different traditions of Shadanga Yoga in Tibet

- 1\. Maha Lotsawa Gyi-jo Dawa Woeser’s Shadanga yoga instruction.

- 2\. rMa Lotsawa Geway Lodoe’s Shadanga yoga system.

- 3\. Khrom Lotsawa Pema Woeser’s Shadanga yoga.

- 4\. Atisha’s Shadanga yoga received from Lama Nagpo

- 5\. Dro Lotsawa Sherab Drakpa’s Shadanga yoga received from Pandita
  Somnatha.

- 6\. Ra Lotsawa Dorje Drakpa’s Shadanga yoga received from Pandita
  Samanta Shri.

- 7\. Tradition of Tsa mi Lotsawa Sangye Drak’s composition of series of
  shadanga yoga.

- 8\. Rechung Dorje Drakpa’s (dagpo Kagyu) Shadanga yoga received from
  Amoghavajra.

- **Three traditions passed down from Ga lotsawa:**

\`9. He transmitted Kalachakra tantra’s Shadanga yoga to Lama zhang Tshudue Drakpa/Dakpo Kagyu).

10\. He transmitted Mother tantra’s He Vajra Shadanga yoga to Sachen Kunga Nyingpo/Sakya.

11. He transmitted Father tantra’s Guhya Samaja tantra’s Shadanga yoga
    to Kyura A-kyab.

- **Two traditions passed down from Mahapandita Shakya shri:**

\(12\) Shakya Shri transmitted it to Caelo Choezang (Geluk)

\(13\) Shakya Shri transmitted it to Sakya Panchen.

- **Vibhutiracandra two tradition**:

  14. One received it from Ratna Rakshita who in turn received it from
      Anupamarakshita.

  15. Second received it from Shabari in Nepal.

  16. Chag Lotsawa Choeje Pal Shadanga yoga received from Nepal Pandita
      Nyiwang Srung and from Indian Pandita Rahula Shri bhadra.

  17. Menlung Guru

------------------------------------------------------------------------

### III. Other Shadanga Yoga traditions

- **Ko-brag-pa tradition**: Ko-brag-pa received close transmission from
  Vibhuticandra in Nepal. Shadanga yoga transmitted from Drupchen
  Golungpa is Ko-brag-pa tradition.

- **Rong lugs (Rong tradition**): Rong-pa Ga lo’s tradition passed down
  through his studend Rong-pa Sherab Senge and from him to his student
  Dorje Gyaltsen.

- **Bodong tradition**: Bodong Rinchen Tsemo received it from Drubthob
  Semo chepa and passed down to Senge Gyaltsen, Shonglo Dorje Gyaltsen
  to Kunkhyen Jigdrel.
