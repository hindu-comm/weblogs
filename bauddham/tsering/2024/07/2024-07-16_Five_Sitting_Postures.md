### Five sitting postures

1.  **Jaina asetic (naked one) posture**:

    Sit in a squatting position with your arms crossed over your chest.

2.  **Powerful athlete/wrestler posture**:

    Keep your heels on your buttocks. Plant your knees and hands on the
    ground.

    ------------------------------------------------------------------------

3.  **Rishi stability posture**:

    Sit with the vajra cross-legged posture (full lotus), pressing the
    hands down with the buttocks.

    ------------------------------------------------------------------------

4.  **Low caste siting posture**:

    Sit down in a squatting position with your arms wrapped around your
    knees (like a yoga strap).

    ------------------------------------------------------------------------

5.  **Graceful sitting posture**:

    (First) stand up. (Then) place your shins on the floor and place
    your hands under your armpits.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

**Note**: This is called the five sitting postures. It is taken from the text written by Taranatha, a practice associated with the Shangpa Kagyu tradition.
