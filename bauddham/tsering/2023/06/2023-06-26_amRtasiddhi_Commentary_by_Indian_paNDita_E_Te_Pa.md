#### Homage

Text within this block will maintain its original spacing when published

``` text There dwells a moon 
On the stainless lotus in the circle/mandala of the navel.  
From that arises the three realms and the path of dharma. 
In the middle of it, there is the third path,  
Which is pierced upto the great bliss chakra, 
I pay homage to her the pure one.  
```
