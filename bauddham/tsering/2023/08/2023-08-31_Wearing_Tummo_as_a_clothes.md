Four types of tummo are mentioned in the tummo practice of the Niguma tradition. They are,

1.  Eating Tummo as food.

2.  Wearing Tummo as clothes.

3.  Using Tummo as a sitting mat.

4.  Using Tummo as a horse to ride.

This post contains the translation of a short instruction on the practice of 'wearing tummo as clothes'.
