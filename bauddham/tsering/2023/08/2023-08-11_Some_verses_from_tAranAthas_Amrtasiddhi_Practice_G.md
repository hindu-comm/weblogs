### Translator’s note

The three main mudras of *Amṛtasiddhi* are *Mahāmudra*, *Mahāvedha* and *Mahābandha*. In the Shangpa teaching cycles (Taranatha's *Amṛtasiddhi* teaching is also based on this tradition) *Mahāvedha* is called བེ་ཏ (*be-ta* or *vedha*), *Mahābandha* is called བྷནྡྷ་ (*bandha*) and *Mahāmudra* is called *Am-grol* (which has the same steps as *Mahāmudra*).

The air of *rāhu* (eclipse maker) or the air of pristine consciousness moves through the central channel. There are three types of air moving in the upper three channels (ཉི་ཟླ་སྒྲ་གཅན་གྱི་རླུང་གསུམ་), Sun in the right, Moon in the left and *rāhu* in the central channel.

I believe the term མཉེན་བཅུག་ (*mnyen bcug*) in the line རྩ་རྣམས་དག་ཀྱང་མཉེན་བཅུག་འགྱུར། is actually མཉེན་ལྕུག (*mnyen lcug*). The meaning of མཉེན་ལྕུག (mnyen lcug) is flexible, pliant. There are four types of flexibility (མཉེན་ལྕུག) - the body, the letters, the channels and the *bindu*. རྩའི་མཉེན་ལྕུག (*rtsa'i mnyen lcug*) The flexibility of the channels is that when all the knots in the channels are released, the channels wind slightly around themselves with extreme flexibility.

Following text is extracted from one of the Tāranātha’s *Amṛtasiddhi* text.

[](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!0pvw!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F436c5389-cfed-429a-9e76-c9b0778085b3_1600x400.png)

![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!0pvw!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F436c5389-cfed-429a-9e76-c9b0778085b3_1600x400.png)

In the yoga of the three mudras,

*Rāhu* is the indispensable helper.

Am-grol destroys the city of raudra (wrathful).

Vedha destroys the city of Hari (Ravisher/Vishnu).

Bandha destroys the city of Fetter.

The three Mudra lay siege to the city,

But the one who destroys them is the *rāhu*.

[](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!KGvd!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F62c555ba-d3e2-4c8b-8e40-4a1846e9eb9f_1600x400.png)

![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!KGvd!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F62c555ba-d3e2-4c8b-8e40-4a1846e9eb9f_1600x400.png)

If one practises these three techniques for three years,

Will achieve complete victory over the vital air,

All the nāḍīs becomes extremely flexible,

Bindus are purified and gains control over it,

Boundless higher perception of the knowledge-holder will be revealed.

[](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!Mh2e!,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2Fe7e668f3-b280-41ec-bf8b-10054eaeddc4_1600x400.png)

![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/$s_!Mh2e!,w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fsubstack-post-media.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2Fe7e668f3-b280-41ec-bf8b-10054eaeddc4_1600x400.png)

By using one's own body as a method for the path of liberation,

To develop the primordial wisdom higher and higher,

Is the main pillar of this teaching.

Without mixing this practice with other teachings,

Adhering to the practice of this very tradition,

Will accomplish (result) simultaneously with the mudra of one’s own body.
