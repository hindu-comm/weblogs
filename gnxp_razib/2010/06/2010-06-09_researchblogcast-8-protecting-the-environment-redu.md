ResearchBlogCast #8: Protecting the Environment Reduces Poverty?

It’s up, [listen here](http://researchblogging.org/news/?p=1446), or, subscribe on iTunes to “ResearchBlogCast.” We highlight Ed Yong’s post [Protect biodiversity, alleviate poverty: the surprise benefits of protected areas](http://blogs.discovermagazine.com/notrocketscience/2010/05/24/protect-biodiversity-alleviate-poverty-the-surprise-benefits-of-protected-areas/), which reviews a new *PNAS* paper, [Protected areas reduced poverty in Costa Rica and Thailand](http://www.pnas.org/content/107/22/9996).
