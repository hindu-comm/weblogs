Yes, Biblical fundamentalists are not bright

[Tom Rees](https://bhascience.blogspot.com/2010/01/bible-done-be-word-of-god.html?utm_source=feedburner&utm_medium=feed&utm_campaign=Feed:+ResearchBloggingSocialScienceEnglish+(Research+Blogging+-+English+-+Social+Science)&utm_content=Google+Reader) reports on an analysis of the GSS which point to the correlation between low verbal skills and a Biblical literalist outlook. Well, I’ve talked about this at length. Religion & IQ, Biblical literalism or low IQ: which came first?, Pentecostals are stupid? Unitarians are smart? and Educational levels & denomination. Poking around the [GSS](http://sda.berkeley.edu/cgi-bin/hsda?harcsda+gss08) the powerful correlates of belief in the literal truth of the Bible (variable “BIBLE”) jump out at you.
