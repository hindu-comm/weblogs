The Human Edge (on NPR)

*NPR* has a series on *Morning Edition* titled “The Human Edge,” which explores human evolution and genetics. The first episode is up, [Finding Our Inner Fish](http://www.npr.org/templates/story/story.php?storyId=127937070). They focus on [Neil Shubin](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Neil_Shubin)‘s work (also, some reporting [on what fish can tell us](http://www.npr.org/templates/story/story.php?storyId=128084031) about human skin color on *All Things Considered*).

As a constructive criticism, I wonder if NPR could do something with science like [Planet Money](http://www.npr.org/blogs/money/).
