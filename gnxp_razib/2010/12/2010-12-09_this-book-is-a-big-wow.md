This book is a big *wow*

[![](https://i0.wp.com/www.gnxp.com/wp/wp-content/uploads/2010/12/0199262136-199x300.jpg?resize=199%2C300 "0199262136")![](https://i0.wp.com/www.gnxp.com/wp/wp-content/uploads/2010/12/0199262136-199x300.jpg?resize=199%2C300 "0199262136")](https://www.amazon.com/exec/obidos/ASIN/0199236631/geneexpressio-20/)[War in Human Civilization](https://www.amazon.com/exec/obidos/ASIN/0199236631/geneexpressio-20/) is an awesomely well written and dense book. Like [The Horse, the Wheel, and Language](https://www.amazon.com/exec/obidos/ASIN/0691058873/geneexpressio-20) it is a scholarly work which stays broadly engaging and relevant to a wider audience than specialists. Highly recommended if you have some spare time over Christmas.

This is naturally not a endorsement of every claim made in this book. But whatever the substantive facts, the author managed to implement the plan of argument and the marshaling of evidence in a fashion which is extremely impressive.
