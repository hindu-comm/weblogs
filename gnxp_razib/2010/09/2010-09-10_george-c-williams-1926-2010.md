George C. Williams, (1926-2010)

[Jerry Coyne](http://whyevolutionistrue.wordpress.com/2010/09/10/g-c-williams-rip/) notes that George C. Williams died a few days ago. I’d heard he was ill. Michael Ruse has an [obituary up](http://chronicle.com/blogPost/George-C-Williams-1926-2010/26821/). Williams’ book [Adaptation and Natural Selection](https://www.amazon.com/exec/obidos/ASIN/0691026157/geneexpressio-20) prefigured Richard Dawkins’ [The Selfish Gene](https://www.amazon.com/exec/obidos/ASIN/0192860925/geneexpressio-20).

**Update:** Please see [Carl Zimmer’s reflections and recollections](http://blogs.discovermagazine.com/loom/2010/09/10/george-williams-has-died-reflections-and-an-interview/?utm_source=feedburner&utm_medium=feed&utm_campaign=Feed:+DiscoverMag+(Discover+Magazine)).
