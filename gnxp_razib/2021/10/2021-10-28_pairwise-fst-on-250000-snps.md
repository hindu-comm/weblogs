Population Pairwise Fst on 250,000 SNPs

![](https://i0.wp.com/www.gnxp.com/WordPress/wp-content/uploads/2021/10/Rplot02.jpg?resize=640%2C385&ssl=1)![](https://i0.wp.com/www.gnxp.com/WordPress/wp-content/uploads/2021/10/Rplot02.jpg?resize=640%2C385&ssl=1)

People routinely ask me about a place to find pairwise Fst values. I have a dataset with 250,000 SNPs and 200 populations, and a script using plink that generates pairwise differences crosses populations. Here are two files with the results:

A [file](https://www.dropbox.com/s/fpxy0h132qz7zzy/fst.csv?dl=0) with the Fst values between populations in [rows](https://www.dropbox.com/s/fpxy0h132qz7zzy/fst.csv?dl=0)

A [file](https://www.dropbox.com/s/uctl6az32vshuvy/FstMatrix.csv?dl=0) with the Fst values between populations as a [matrix](https://www.dropbox.com/s/uctl6az32vshuvy/FstMatrix.csv?dl=0)
