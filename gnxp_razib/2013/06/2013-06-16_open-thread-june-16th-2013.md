I stated earlier I can’t remember what it was like to *not* have my daughter in my life, but it is still strange to me that I’m a father, of all people.

For the record, I don’t think we should intervene in Syria **at all.** Most [Americans seem to agree with me](http://www.gallup.com/tag/syria.aspx). But it may not matter. The establishment [may force intervention](http://dish.andrewsullivan.com/2013/06/16/obamas-worst-foreign-policy-decision-as-president/).
