Nature's Oracle finally has a publication date

[![](https://i0.wp.com/blogs.discovermagazine.com/gnxp/files/2013/02/nature-s-oracle.jpg?resize=278%2C420 "nature-s-oracle")![](https://i0.wp.com/blogs.discovermagazine.com/gnxp/files/2013/02/nature-s-oracle.jpg?resize=278%2C420 "nature-s-oracle")](https://www.amazon.com/exec/obidos/ASIN/019860727X/geneexpressio-20)The [W. D. Hamilton](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/W._D._Hamilton) biography, [Nature’s Oracle](https://www.amazon.com/exec/obidos/ASIN/019860727X/geneexpressio-20) finally has a publication date. [Amazon](https://www.amazon.com/exec/obidos/ASIN/019860727X/geneexpressio-20) says March 1st, and other places say April 1st (For the USA). I don’t care much either way, I pre-ordered. Now it would be nice if more academic books were available in Kindl format though. I’m not anticipating the “physical pleasure” of have it shipped to me when I could just get it emailed to me on the day of its release.
