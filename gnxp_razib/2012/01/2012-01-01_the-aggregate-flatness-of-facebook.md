The aggregate flatness of Facebook

Most readers know that I’ve been tracking [Google Trends](https://www.google.com/trends?q=Facebook%2C+Google%2C+Yahoo%2C&ctab=0&geo=all&geor=all&date=all&sort=0) data on Facebook for years. Now on January 1 2012 It seems pretty obviously that in the **international aggregate** this was the year that Facebook finally hit saturation in terms of “mindshare.”

[![](https://i0.wp.com/blogs.discovermagazine.com/gnxp/files/2012/01/fb1.jpg?resize=589%2C295)![](https://i0.wp.com/blogs.discovermagazine.com/gnxp/files/2012/01/fb1.jpg?resize=589%2C295)](https://i0.wp.com/blogs.discovermagazine.com/gnxp/files/2012/01/fb1.jpg)
