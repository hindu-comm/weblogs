Jacques Barzun, 1907-2012

Jacques Barzun [died last week](http://www.nytimes.com/2012/10/26/arts/jacques-barzun-historian-and-scholar-dies-at-104.html?pagewanted=all&_r=0&pagewanted=print). [From Dawn to Decadence](https://www.amazon.com/exec/obidos/ASIN/0060928832/geneexpressio-20) is one of those books which is useful not as much for its descriptive sweep of social history, but for the insights and counter-narratives which it may elicit. I am left in awe of the life of the mind which Barzun experienced in his time.

H/T [Steve Sailer](https://isteve.blogspot.com/2012/10/historian-jacques-barzun-age-104-rip.html).
