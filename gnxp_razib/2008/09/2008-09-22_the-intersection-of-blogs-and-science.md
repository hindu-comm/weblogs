The intersection of blogs and science

[Shelley Batts](http://scienceblogs.com/twominds/?utm_source=bloglist&utm_medium=dropdown), [Nick Anthis](http://scienceblogs.com/scientificactivist/?utm_source=bloglist&utm_medium=dropdown) and [Tara C. Smith](http://scienceblogs.com/aetiology/?utm_source=bloglist&utm_medium=dropdown) have a new paper, [Advancing Science through Conversations: Bridging the Gap between Blogs and the Academy](http://biology.plosjournals.org/perlserv/?request=get-document&doi=10.1371/journal.pbio.0060240), in *PLoS Biology*. Also, [Living the Scientific Life](http://scienceblogs.com/grrlscientist/2008/09/science_blogging_planting_the.php) has posted a similar [article](http://scienceblogs.com/grrlscientist/2008/09/science_blogging_planting_the.php) which will be published in [Research Fortnight](http://www.researchresearch.com/entry/entry.htm).
