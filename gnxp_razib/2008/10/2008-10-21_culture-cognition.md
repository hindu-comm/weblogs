Culture & cognition

There is a new blog some readers might find of interest, [Culture and Cognition](http://www.cognitionandculture.net/index.php?option=com_content&view=section&layout=blog&id=1&Itemid=34&5ac69ac48485e9b1125fe26103cbd580=666d23130b5a55eda6a1fd059815c427). Dan Sperber, who did a [10 questions](https://www.gnxp.com/blog/2005/12/10-questions-for-dan-sperber.php) nearly 3 years ago, is a contributor. Imagine, what if cultural anthropology was dominated by people who didn’t behave like literary critics or aspire to be political revolutionaries?
