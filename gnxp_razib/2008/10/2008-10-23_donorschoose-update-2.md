DonorsChoose Update

As you can see to the left *Gene Expression* [hasn’t raised that much money this year](http://www.donorschoose.org/donors/viewChallenge.html?id=18997&utm_source=BC08&utm_medium=widget&utm_content=GP). That’s not so hot, but, some of the other [ScienceBloggers have raised a bunch](http://www.donorschoose.org/donors/leadershipboard.html?category=17), so that’s heartening. I also wanted to add that *Seed* will be “padding” our contributions this year, but right now the amount that *Seed* is giving is multiples greater than what I’ve raised so far, so I hope readers can help me out a bit….
