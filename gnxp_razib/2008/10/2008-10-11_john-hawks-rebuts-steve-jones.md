John Hawks rebuts Steve Jones

[John Hawks](http://johnhawks.net/weblog/topics/evolution/selection/jones-evolution-stopping-2008.html) succinctly [responds](http://johnhawks.net/weblog/topics/evolution/selection/jones-evolution-stopping-2008.html) to [Steve Jones’](http://www.timesonline.co.uk/tol/news/uk/science/article4894696.ece) argument that evolution is ending. Nothing surprising, but a very tight and accessible exposition.
