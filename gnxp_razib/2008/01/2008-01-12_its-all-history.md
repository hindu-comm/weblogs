It’s all history!

Over the past two days I’ve been arguing that **multiple avenues of insight are critical in historical scholarship.** That’s a general assertion; check out this [bloggingheads.tv exchange](http://bloggingheads.tv/diavlogs/8008?in=00:39:55) between [Carl Zimmer](http://scienceblogs.com/loom/) and paleontologist [Niel Shubin](http://experts.uchicago.edu/experts.php?id=206). Carl points out that though Neil is a paleontologist focused on [Deep Time](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Deep_time), his laboratory is a conventional wet environment where a fair amount of developmental genetic research occurs. [Uniformitarianism](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Uniformitarianism_(science)) strikes again! The present is a window onto the past….
