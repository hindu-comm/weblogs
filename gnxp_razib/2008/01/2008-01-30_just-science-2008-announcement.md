Just Science 2008 announcement

Since [Just Science](http://www.justscience.net/2008/?page_id=1368) starts next week I’m going to have to take a break from [Stephen Jay Gould blogging](https://www.google.com/search?q=stephen+jay+gould&btnG=Search%21&domains=scienceblogs.com%2Fgnxp&sitesearch=scienceblogs.com%2Fgnxp) due to the constraints that I’m going to have to adhere to when it comes to posts (i.e., it has to be science). Expect blogging to focus on chapters from W. D. Hamilton [Narrow Roads of Gene Land, Volume 1: Evolution of Social Behaviour](https://www.amazon.com/exec/obidos/ASIN/0716745305/geneexpressio-20). You know about [kin selection](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Kin_selection), but do you *know* kin selection?
