HGDP browser is out!

Do you have some marginal time today? Well, now you really don’t, play around with the [HGDP browser](http://hgdp.uchicago.edu/cgi-bin/gbrowse/HGDP/). If you click and find yourself a bit bewildered, read [Do It Yourself: searching for evolution’s signature in 53 human populations](http://scienceblogs.com/geneticfuture/2008/11/diy_searching_for_evolutions_signa.php) over at [Genetic Future](http://scienceblogs.com/geneticfuture/2008/11/diy_searching_for_evolutions_signa.php).
