The Secular Right

You probably already know this, but in case you don’t, I’m somewhat involved in a new website, [Secular Right](http://secularright.org/). [Heather Mac Donald](https://www.gnxp.com/blog/2007/01/10-questions-for-heather-mac-donald.php), [Derb](https://www.gnxp.com/blog/2005/11/10-questions-for-derb.php) and [Walter Olson](http://overlawyered.com/) are current contributors. My own postings there will be mostly about philosophy, history and data analysis, as opposed to rapid response to other weblogs or commentary on current politics.
