Yankees, Catholics and Southerners

At [my other weblog](https://www.gnxp.com/blog/2008/11/yankees-irish-catholics-and-mccain-belt.php) I have [a post](https://www.gnxp.com/blog/2008/11/yankees-irish-catholics-and-mccain-belt.php) which presents dozens of charts gleaned from the GSS. The intent was to compare Catholics and Protestants of New England origin to whites from the McCain Belt, and adduce whether the various Catholic immigrant groups such as the Irish and Italians in New England absorbed the values of the Yankee natives.
