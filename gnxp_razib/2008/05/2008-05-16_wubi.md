Wubi

If you don’t have a spare computer, want to try out a Linux distribution, and are a little intimidated by the details of setting up a dual-boot, check out [Wubi](http://wubi-installer.org/). It makes a dual-boot pretty much a “one-click” affair, loading up the [Ubuntu](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Ubuntu_(Linux_distribution)) distribution via a Windows installer.
