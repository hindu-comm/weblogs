Chance in evolution

Carl Zimmer tells [a fascinating story](http://scienceblogs.com/loom/2008/06/02/a_new_step_in_evolution.php) about the role of chance in *E. coli* evolution. The paper isn’t available yet, of course, since it’s PNAS, but it certainly sounds like it’s worth a look.
