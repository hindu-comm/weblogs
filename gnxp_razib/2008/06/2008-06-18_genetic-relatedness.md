Genetic Relatedness

John Hawks on [what it means to be human](http://johnhawks.net/weblog/2008/06/18#what-it-means-to-be-human-2008). RPM [objects](http://scienceblogs.com/evolgen/2008/06/how_many_genes_do_you_share_wi.php) to an assertion John makes about the relationship of distant cousins; worth reading. It’s an interesting exploration of a subtle topic; we’ve covered these issues when discussing [ethnic nepotism](https://www.gnxp.com/MT2/archives/002809.html). In [Narrow Roads of Gene Land](https://www.amazon.com/exec/obidos/ASIN/0716745305/geneexpressio-20) W. D. Hamilton alludes to the fact that molecular and evolution biologists tend to view the central questions and answers differently for instrumental reasons.
