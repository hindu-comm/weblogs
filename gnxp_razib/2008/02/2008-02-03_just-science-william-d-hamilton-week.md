Just Science, William D. Hamilton week

I stated earlier this week that I would post on papers from William D. Hamilton’s [Narrow Roads of Gene Land](https://www.amazon.com/exec/obidos/ASIN/0716745305/geneexpressio-20). I’ve narrowed down which ones I’ll blog:  
Day 1: [The genetical evolution of social behaviour. I](http://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/5875341?dopt=Abstract)  
Day 2: [The genetical evolution of social behaviour. II](http://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/5875340?ordinalpos=2&itool=EntrezSystem2.PEntrez.Pubmed.Pubmed_ResultsPanel.Pubmed_RVDocSum)  
Day 3: [The moulding of senescence by natural selection](http://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/6015424?ordinalpos=2&itool=EntrezSystem2.PEntrez.Pubmed.Pubmed_ResultsPanel.Pubmed_RVDocSum)  
Day 4: [Extraordinary sex ratios](http://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pubmed/6021675?ordinalpos=4&itool=EntrezSystem2.PEntrez.Pubmed.Pubmed_ResultsPanel.Pubmed_RVDocSum)  
Day 5: [Innate social aptitudes of man](https://scholar.google.com/scholar?q=innate+social+aptitudes+of+man+hamilton&hl=en&lr=&btnG=Search)
