ScienceDebate 2008 announcement

Got an email from [Sheril](http://scienceblogs.com/intersection/2008/02/its_official_candidates_invite.php) today:

> Well it’s finally happened! It’s official. Hillary Clinton, Mike > Huckabee, John McCain, and Barack Obama have been invited to > ScienceDebate2008.
> > The location? Philadelphia’s Franklin Institute, named after one of > our nation’s greatest scientists (and greatest patriots). The date? > April 18, days before the Pennsylvania > Primary.[](http://scienceblogs.com/intersection/2008/02/its_official_candidates_invite.php) >
> [](https://actionforspace.blogspot.com/2008/01/interview-with-chief-counsel-for-spacex.html)We’re > so close to seeing this through and now more than ever, we need you’re > help! This is our biggest news yet and the first tangible Call to > Action for the blogger coalition beyond the announcement. The time > has come to mobilize your readers. >
> We are asking that you not only announce the invitation, but also to > engage readers to take action. How people can get involved is listed > within our post:
> <http://scienceblogs.com/intersection/2008/02/its_official_candidates_invite.php> >
> 1\. Contact the campaigns, and tell them to attend ScienceDebate2008! > A  
> list of contact information for the campaigns can be found > [here](https://actionforspace.blogspot.com/2008/01/interview-with-chief-counsel-for-spacex.html) >
> . >
> 2\. Write letters to the editor of your local newspapers, raising  
> further awareness about this initiative. Some handy letter writing > tips  
> can be found > [here](http://www.ucsusa.org/ucs/about/writing-a-letter-to-the-editor.html). >
> 3\. Tell a friend about ScienceDebate2008 (handy > [link](http://www.thedatabank.com/dpg/335/tf.asp?formid=SD2008TAF)  
> here). We need to spread the word as much as possible at this > critical  
> time. We’re at 13,000 supporters right now; we want to get to 15,000
