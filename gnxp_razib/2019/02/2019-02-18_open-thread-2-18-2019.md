Speaking of Peter, check out his recent blog post, [An Anarchist View of Human Social Evolution](http://peterturchin.com/cliodynamica/an-anarchist-view-of-human-social-evolution/), which is basically a critique of the two of scholars and an essay, [Are we city dwellers or hunter-gatherers?](https://newhumanist.org.uk/articles/5409/are-we-city-dwellers-or-hunter-gatherers) The interesting sociological aspect is that one of the scholars is a pretty unpleasant disputant with critics on social media…and that seems to redound to his fame and influence. Unfortunate incentives.

[An Honest Living: What is it like to go from a tenured professorship to an hourly wage driving buses? This piece tries to make sense of an unusual transition](https://stevesalaita.com/an-honest-living/). The author is, to be frank, [kind of a dick](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Steven_Salaita). But there are lots of people with unpleasant and intolerable personalities in academia.
