Siete Habanero Sauce, 4.5 stars out of 5.0

[![](https://i0.wp.com/www.gnxp.com/WordPress/wp-content/uploads/2019/03/siestahab.jpg?resize=200%2C352&ssl=1)![](https://i0.wp.com/www.gnxp.com/WordPress/wp-content/uploads/2019/03/siestahab.jpg?resize=200%2C352&ssl=1)](https://www.amazon.com/exec/obidos/ASIN/B07FYJRT3W/geneexpressio-20/ref=as_at?imprToken=4eCdp7CyEAhhsE8qB0QKPg&slotNum=0&imprToken=4LS-khviLiYDnUTj5TRwNQ&slotNum=0&imprToken=dYL3EOReywQfjoKJGOwa7w&slotNum=0&imprToken=5EHNBoZ4GhCS8VYrjK..kA&slotNum=0&imprToken=67TxncJrNOO11LKRkLbXog&slotNum=23&creativeASIN=0878933085&linkCode=w61&imprToken=QiG2bf7fc5-czG6VLZ9cSg&slotNum=164)Tried out [Siete Habanero Hot Sauce](https://www.amazon.com/exec/obidos/ASIN/B07FYJRT3W/geneexpressio-20/ref=as_at?imprToken=QiG2bf7fc5-czG6VLZ9cSg&slotNum=164&creativeASIN=0878933085&linkCode=w61&th=1) today. I really like it!

That being said, I have my biases. I like them hot. It’s moderately hot…it won’t burn a hole through your alimentary system, but it will kick you gently in the mouth. Second, it’s not a very sweet sauce, but a savory one. That makes sense in light of the avocado oil.

But the most exceptional and pleasant aspect of [Siete Habanero Hot Sauce](https://www.amazon.com/exec/obidos/ASIN/B07FYJRT3W/geneexpressio-20/ref=as_at?imprToken=QiG2bf7fc5-czG6VLZ9cSg&slotNum=164&creativeASIN=0878933085&linkCode=w61&th=1) is the fact that somehow the spice kicks in later on. Instead of barging in the front door you can taste the creamy avocado before there is a “finish” of habanero spiciness. It’s something I always look for in a hot sauce since it allows for full flavor appreciation.
