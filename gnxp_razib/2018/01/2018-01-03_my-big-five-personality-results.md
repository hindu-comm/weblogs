My Big Five Personality Results

I assume most readers know that the “Big Five Personality Test” is really the only one psychologists feel to be scientifically valid. [FiveThirtyEight](https://fivethirtyeight.com/features/most-personality-quizzes-are-junk-science-i-found-one-that-isnt/) has a post on the topic up. I’ve taken [a quiz](http://www.personalitylab.org/tests/bfi2_self_pol.htm) on this test before. I figured I would share mine below (no great surprises).
