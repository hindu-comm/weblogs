Scientists of note now on ScienceBlogs

[Pamela Ronald](http://scienceblogs.com/tomorrowstable/?utm_source=ScienceBlogs+Weekly+Recap&utm_campaign=f4b9dbb97f-Recap_10_08_to_10_21_2009&utm_medium=email) of [Tommorow’s Table](http://scienceblogs.com/tomorrowstable/?utm_source=ScienceBlogs+Weekly+Recap&utm_campaign=f4b9dbb97f-Recap_10_08_to_10_21_2009&utm_medium=email), author of [Tomorrow’s Table: Organic Farming, Genetics, and the Future of Food](https://www.amazon.com/exec/obidos/ASIN/0195301757/geneexpressio-20/). Additionally, [David Sloan Wilson](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/David_Sloan_Wilson), has moved his blog to [ScienceBlogs](http://scienceblogs.com/evolution/?utm_source=ScienceBlogs+Weekly+Recap&utm_campaign=f4b9dbb97f-Recap_10_08_to_10_21_2009&utm_medium=email). It’s called [Evolution for Everyone](http://scienceblogs.com/evolution/?utm_source=ScienceBlogs+Weekly+Recap&utm_campaign=f4b9dbb97f-Recap_10_08_to_10_21_2009&utm_medium=email), after the book [of the same name](https://www.amazon.com/exec/obidos/ASIN/0385340214/geneexpressio-20).
