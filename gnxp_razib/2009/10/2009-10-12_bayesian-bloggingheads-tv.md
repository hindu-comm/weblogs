Bayesian bloggingheads.tv

[Eliezer Yudkowsky](http://yudkowsky.net/) is on bloggingheads.tv with one of my favorite producers of brain-candy, the statistician [Andrew Gelman](http://www.stat.columbia.edu/~gelman/blog/). Here is Eliezer’s [An Intuitive Explanation of Bayes’ Theorem](http://yudkowsky.net/rational/bayes), and David Spiegelhalter’s article on [Bayesian statistics](http://www.scholarpedia.org/article/Bayesian_statistics) in [Scholar pedia](http://www.scholarpedia.org) (which Gelman refers to favorably).
