Race & IQ

Dan MacArthur’s post [Should scientists study race and IQ?](http://scienceblogs.com/geneticfuture/2009/02/should_scientists_study_race_a.php) points to a [debate in *Nature*](http://www.nature.com/nature/journal/v457/n7231/full/457788a.html). Well, scientists are studying genetic variation. And others are engaged in the project of [psychometrics](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Robert_Plomin). This seems to fall into the category of the economist predicting the past perfectly. Lagging indicators & all. I mean, if we’re talking about [whole genome sequencing of newborns in 2019](http://scienceblogs.com/geneticfuture/2009/02/value_of_newborn_genome.php)….
