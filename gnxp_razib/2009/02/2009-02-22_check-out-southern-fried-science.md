Check out Southern Fried Science

I recently got bored and played a joke on [Southern Fried Science](http://southernfriedscientist.wordpress.com/) a few days ago…my usual PC-tard schtick,\* see [the comments](http://southernfriedscientist.wordpress.com/2009/02/18/should-we-study-how-intelligence-is-related-to-gender-and-race/#comments). In any case, the author of the weblog suggests that I was being unkind at his expense. I won’t stop playing these sorts of jokes (see this [Greg Laden comment thread](http://scienceblogs.com/gregladen/2009/02/race_gender_iq_and_nature.php)), but I figured it would be a *mitzvah* to [send some traffic his way](http://southernfriedscientist.wordpress.com/). It takes some courage for a privileged white male to attempt to abolish cant when it comes to discussion about race in this nation of cowards.  
\* My usually method is to not think about what I’m typing, but just stick together random things I’ve heard or seen said and attempt to keep some sort of syntactic coherency. Usually people make some sense out of it.
