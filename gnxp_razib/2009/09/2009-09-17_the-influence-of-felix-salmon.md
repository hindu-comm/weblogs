The influence of Felix Salmon

[The Big Money](http://www.thebigmoney.com/articles/impressions/2009/09/16/spawning-salmon?page=full) has a [profile](http://www.thebigmoney.com/articles/impressions/2009/09/16/spawning-salmon?page=full) of [Felix Salmon](http://blogs.reuters.com/felix-salmon/) up. Here is why I reckon that Salmon has some rank, every few weeks he shows up as a guest on [Marketplace](http://marketplace.publicradio.org/). And he consistently pronounces Kai Ryssdal’s first name as if it is really the female name “Kay.”
