Avatar & the death of "Star Trek aliens"

Since readers of this weblog tend toward nerdishness I’m assuming they’re following the buzz around *Avatar: The Movie*. I only got interested in it last night trying to figure out the references in yesterday’s *South Park* episode, [Dances with Smurfs](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Dances_with_Smurfs). Check out the tailer below. Obviously actors in regular films aren’t going to be replaced by CGI in the next few years, *but*, looks like we’re on the cusp of a the shift when it comes to a human being necessary to portray humanoid aliens. The [“uncanny valley”](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Uncanny_Valley) is to some extent an upside in sci-fi, excluding the problems that will generate when it comes to the sticky issue of hybridization.
