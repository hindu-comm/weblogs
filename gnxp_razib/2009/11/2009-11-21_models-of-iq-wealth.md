Models of IQ & wealth

[Steve Hsu](https://infoproc.blogspot.com/) has been interesting of late (interesting like [Steve](https://isteve.blogspot.com/), not [Malcolm](http://www.gladwell.com/)). So, [IQ, compression and simple models](https://infoproc.blogspot.com/2009/11/iq-compression-and-simple-models.html) and [If you’re so smart, why aren’t you rich?](https://infoproc.blogspot.com/2009/11/if-youre-so-smart-why-arent-you-rich.html).
