Woman attacked by polar bear (live footage)

[![polarbearattackwoman.png](https://i0.wp.com/blogs.discovermagazine.com/gnxp/files/polarbearattackwoman.png?resize=250%2C372)![polarbearattackwoman.png](https://i0.wp.com/blogs.discovermagazine.com/gnxp/files/polarbearattackwoman.png?resize=250%2C372)](http://www.cnn.com/2009/WORLD/europe/04/11/polar.bear.attack/index.html)There are nearly 7 billion human beings in the world, so I guess it shouldn’t surprise us that there are adult women who decide to [jump into a polar bear exhibit during feeding time.](http://www.cnn.com/2009/WORLD/europe/04/11/polar.bear.attack/index.html) Right? When I saw this on CNN’s front page one word that described the photo would be “compelling.” If polar bears could talk…. (you can see [some video here](http://www.newsoftheworld.co.uk/news/260180/See-video-of-terrifying-moment-when-a-woman-is-MAULED-by-a-polar-bear-She-JUMPED-into-its-zoo-enclosure-at-feeding-time.html)) This really needs to make it into a *South Park* episode in the future.
