A How-To Guide To Faking Fingerprints

Doesn’t it just ruin your day when you’re skulking around some high security area and you need to get into a secure room that is access controlled via a fingerprint scanner? I know it’s ruined many a day for me.

Never fear, for here is step by step guide on how to defeat those dastardly devices, but be sure to have a supply of Jell-O at your disposal. Here is [one](http://www.ccc.de/biometrie/fingerabdruck_kopieren?language=en) guide, and here is [another.](http://www.puttyworld.com/thinputdeffi.html) Here is more [background](http://www.dansdata.com/uareu.htm) and here is the [technical paper](http://cryptome.org/gummy.htm) that started it all. Here is a page that discusses the [legal](http://research.yale.edu/lawmeme/modules.php?name=News&file=article&sid=214) implications.
