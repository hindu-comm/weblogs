Captain Scarlet

Remember the 1960’s marionette shows Thunderbirds and Captain Scarlet? I guess the general consensus is that the Hollywood Thunderbirds remake sucked if you were older than 12, but I’ve seen the first two CGI episodes of Captain Scarlet, and man, they rock.

If you’ve got Real Player you can watch the [Captain Scarlet Trailer](http://www.bbc.co.uk/mediaselector/check/collective/captainscarlet?size=16x9&bgc=8A9EC3&nbram=1&bbram=1). Here is Gerry Anderson’s [webpage](http://www.bbc.co.uk/dna/collective/A2860715) with more details on the project. Here is a Captain Scarlet [nostalgia](http://www.bbc.co.uk/cult/anderson/scarlet/index.shtml) page.
