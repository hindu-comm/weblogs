Dutch Flag Prohibited For Dutch Students

[Joanne Jacobs](http://www.joannejacobs.com/mtarchives/014819.html) points me to this [story](https://dutchreport.blogspot.com/2005/02/dutch-flag-prohibited.html) about Dutch students being prohibited from displaying the Dutch flag on their bookbags. One of the young students was asked whether he was a racist.
