Hobbit hhhmm….

Carl Zimmer passes on a [report](http://www.corante.com/loom/archives/2005/04/29/hobbits_alive.php) of a community of Pygmy Negritos a mile from the site where the Flores Hobbit was excavated. 13,000 years is a long time to maintain local continuity, but who knows?

A few weeks ago John Hawks declared that the Flores specimen is [pathological](http://johnhawks.net/weblog/fossils/flores/australopithecine_retraction.html).

I feel like I’m starting to smell Cold Fusion….
