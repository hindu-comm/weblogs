Endless forms truncated

[Evolution at Two Levels: Gene and Form](http://biology.plosjournals.org/perlserv/?request=get-document&doi=10.1371/journal.pbio.0030245), is an article (based on a lecture) given by Sean Carroll, author of [Endless Forms Most Beautiful](https://www.amazon.com/exec/obidos/redirect?link_code=ur2&camp=1789&tag=geneexpressio-20&creative=9325&path=ASIN/0393060160/qid=1117155246/sr=2-1/ref=pd_bbs_b_2_1). Evolgen [has more](https://evolgen.blogspot.com/2005/07/genotype-and-phenotype-and-how-to.html). If you haven’t read the book, but read this article, save your money (I don’t think the first half of the book is worth it for the savvy reader, he spends way too much time on operons)….
