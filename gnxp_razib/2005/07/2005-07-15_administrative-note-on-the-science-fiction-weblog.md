Administrative note on the Science Fiction weblog

The Movable Type issues are gone, but I am going to stay with Blogger for now because I am skeptical about the future support for the old free version of MT that ran this site (this blog is too big for the licensing conditions for the current gratis service, and the number of authors is way too high to be economical). But, I have left everything intact and switched the link back to the old [Science Fiction Weblog](http://sciencefiction.gnxp.com/) so that people with privs over there can keep posting with MT.
