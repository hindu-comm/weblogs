Diamond days on TV….

John Hawks [notes](http://johnhawks.net/weblog/reviews/tv/balter_guns_germs_review_2005.html) that [Guns, Germs and Steel](https://www.amazon.com/exec/obidos/ASIN/0393317552/geneexpressio-20/102-7846807-1152140) is now [turning into a PBS special](http://www.pbs.org/gunsgermssteel/). Looks like Diamond is making for a Sagan, trying to jump from being well know amongst the \>1 standard deviation set to transitioning into a cultural icon. In any case, I echo many of John’s concerns about *GSS*‘s thesis, the more we know about the arc of the development of past societies, the more we know we didn’t know and don’t know. Wish I could say otherwise….
