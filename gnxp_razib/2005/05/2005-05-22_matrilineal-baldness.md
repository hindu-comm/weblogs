Matrilineal baldness

A new [study](http://www.journals.uchicago.edu/AJHG/journal/issues/v77n1/42360/brief/42360.abstract.html) pinpoints a polymorphism on the androgen receptor gene (AR), located on the X chromosome, as the primary factor in early male pattern baldness among German men. The authors also postulate recent selection for this gene.
