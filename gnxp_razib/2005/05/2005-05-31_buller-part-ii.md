Buller, part II

Chris of [Mixing Memory](https://mixingmemory.blogspot.com) has an extremely in depth [critique](https://mixingmemory.blogspot.com/2005/05/has-evolutionary-psychology-been_30.html) of David J. Buller’s [Adapting Minds](https://www.gnxp.com/MT2/archives/003949.html). He isn’t impressed with Buller’s command of the literature in cognitive science. Also, here is a [paper](http://www.niu.edu/phil/%7Ebuller/tics.pdf) by Buller that summarizes his main points.
