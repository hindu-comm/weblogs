Nordic beauty wins again!

[](https://www.gnxp.com/blog/uploaded_images/228125-711180.jpg)[Miss Iceland](http://www.missworld.tv/bio/bio.sps?iBiographyID=51629) [wins](http://www.deccanherald.com/deccanherald/dec112005/update6552920051211.asp) [Miss World](http://www.missworld.tv/)! Now, of course, the title is kind of a joke, because she looks a bit different than [Miss Sweden](http://www.missworld.tv/bio/bio.sps?iBiographyID=51825) or [Miss Denmark](http://www.missworld.tv/bio/bio.sps?iBiographyID=51752), though not as exotic as [Miss Norway](http://www.missworld.tv/bio/bio.sps?iBiographyID=51729). In fact, though it is surely seeing what you want to see, she seems to resemble [Shannen](https://www.imdb.com/gallery/granitz/3313/Events/3313/ShannenDoh_Grant_5415649_400.jpg?path=pgallery&path_key=Doherty,%20Shannen) [Doherty](http://genealogy.about.com/library/surnames/d/bl_name-DOCHERTY.htm) or one of the [Corrs](http://givemethecorrs.free.fr/index.jpg) sisters. This is post facto observation, as there is evidence of [Irish](http://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/entrez/query.fcgi?cmd=Retrieve&db=pubmed&dopt=Abstract&list_uids=10712214&query_hl=5) [admixture](http://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/entrez/query.fcgi?cmd=Retrieve&db=pubmed&dopt=Abstract&list_uids=10931763&query_hl=5) [in this](http://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/entrez/query.fcgi?cmd=Retrieve&db=pubmed&dopt=Abstract&list_uids=11179019&query_hl=5) one Nordic nation.

Update: While doing a little research I found out that [Miss Thailand](http://www.missworld.tv/bio/bio.sps?iBiographyID=51823), Sindee Jensen, has a Danish father! What’s up with the Siam-Scandinavian combination?

Update II: Dienekes has a post on [Norwegian Y chromosomes](https://dienekes.blogspot.com/2005/12/y-chromosomes-of-norway.html).
