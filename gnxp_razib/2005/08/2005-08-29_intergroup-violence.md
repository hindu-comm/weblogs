Intergroup violence

PNAS finally posted the paper [The evolution of lethal intergroup violence](http://www.pnas.org/cgi/content/abstract/0505955102v1) (previously [discussed](https://www.gnxp.com/blog/2005/08/to-maintain-peace-one-must-be-prepared.php)).1 I’ve uploaded the PDF in the [files section](http://groups.yahoo.com/group/gnxpforum/files/) of the [gnxp forum](http://groups.yahoo.com/group/gnxpforum/) as “intergroupviolence” for readers who are interested (you might have to register to view files).
