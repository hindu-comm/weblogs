The evo-psych debate

Well, I wasn’t going to comment on the Amanda Schaeffer [slam](http://www.slate.com/id/2124503/?nav=tap3) of Evolutionary PsychologyTM in Slate drawn heavily from David Buller’s [Adapting Minds](https://www.amazon.com/exec/obidos/ASIN/0262025795/geneexpressio-2/104-1458141-1243947), but [Steve just has](https://isteve.blogspot.com/2005/08/slatecom-makes-human-biodiversity-case.html), and GC pointed out that someone on [Brad Plummer’s blog](https://plumer.blogspot.com/2005_08_01_plumer_archive.html#112430697536082728) also [noted](http://www.haloscan.com/comments/sedationbaby/112430697536082728/#140731) the [implicit opening for human variation](https://www.gnxp.com/MT2/archives/003949.html) that she produced by acknowledging the reality of microevolution over the past 10,000 years. I’ll leave you with the links….
