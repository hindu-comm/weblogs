My new podcast with Spencer Wells

[![](https://i0.wp.com/www.gnxp.com/WordPress/wp-content/uploads/2017/12/podcast-1.jpg?resize=600%2C281)![](https://i0.wp.com/www.gnxp.com/WordPress/wp-content/uploads/2017/12/podcast-1.jpg?resize=600%2C281)](https://itunes.apple.com/us/podcast/the-insight/id1324744423)

Spencer Wells and I have a new [podcast](https://itunes.apple.com/us/podcast/the-insight/id1324744423), [The Insight](https://itunes.apple.com/us/podcast/the-insight/id1324744423). On the first episode, we’ll be talking about the Neolithic revolution.

We’ve already got several more in the pipeline that will come out in the next few weeks (being edited), including one with John Hawks. This will be a regular thing, so please [subscribe](https://itunes.apple.com/us/podcast/the-insight/id1324744423)!
