Totality, 2017

[![](https://i0.wp.com/www.gnxp.com/WordPress/wp-content/uploads/2017/08/totality.jpg?resize=200%2C310)![](https://i0.wp.com/www.gnxp.com/WordPress/wp-content/uploads/2017/08/totality.jpg?resize=200%2C310)](https://i0.wp.com/www.gnxp.com/WordPress/wp-content/uploads/2017/08/totality.jpg)We saw totality in Independence, Missouri. It was a pretty long car trip with the kids, all of whom are under school age (though just barely in the case of the eldest). We had a big fright because there was a massive thunderstorm in the morning…but it cleared out for a few hours. And that was when the total eclipse occurred.

Really words don’t do the experience justice.

Chase a total eclipse if you get a chance.
