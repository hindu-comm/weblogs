Everyone knows about Facebook now

[![Screenshot from 2015-12-28 03:14:11](https://i0.wp.com/www.unz.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/12/Screenshot-from-2015-12-28-031411.png?resize=640%2C389)![Screenshot from 2015-12-28 03:14:11](https://i0.wp.com/www.unz.com/wp-content/uploads/2015/12/Screenshot-from-2015-12-28-031411.png?resize=640%2C389)](https://www.google.com/trends/explore#q=Facebook%2C%20Sex%2C%20Porn%2C%20Google%2C%20Yahoo&cmpt=q&tz=Etc%2FGMT%2B8)

It looks like people are searching for Facebook less often on Google over the past 3 years. Probably because everyone knows about Facebook now. People have been looking for signs of [decline](http://www.forbes.com/sites/karstenstrauss/2012/07/18/facebook-users-decline-along-with-share-price-competition-from-samsung/) for many years. I was in that game too. It seemed inevitable. But perhaps Facebook is going to be the boring and persistent “climax ecosystem” of the social web for the next generation or so?
