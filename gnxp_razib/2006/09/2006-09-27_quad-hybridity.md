Quad-hybridity

[](https://www.gnxp.com/blog/uploaded_images/daniella-744843.jpg)I was doing a “literature survey” and I stumbled upon [Daniella Alonso](http://www.maximonline.com/girls_of_maxim/pictures_and_bio/1212/DaniellaAlonso.girl), the product of a marriage between a Japanese Peruvian father as a Puerto Rican mother (European + African + Amerindian). I am haunted by dreams of synergistic epistasis and *Übermensch*….
