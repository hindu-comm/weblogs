10 Questions gets you interviewed elsewhere!

Just a note, a few weeks ago [Australian Radio](http://www.abc.net.au/rn/saturdayextra/) emailed me about [Matthew Stewart’s](https://www.gnxp.com/blog/2006/08/10-questions-for-matthew-stewart.php) contact information since they couldn’t find him anywhere else. I passed it along, and now Matthew will be doing an interview for them Saturday morning ([the archive](http://www.abc.net.au/rn/saturdayextra/) should show up in a few days).
