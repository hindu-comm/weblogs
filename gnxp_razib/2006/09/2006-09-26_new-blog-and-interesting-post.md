New blog and interesting post

Along with [Dave](http://scienceblogs.com/cognitivedaily/2006/09/two_new_blogs.php) I would like to bring to your attention [AlphaPsy](http://www.cognition.ens.fr/~alphapsy/blog/), a blog devoted to the naturalistical paradigm in cultural anthropology, drawing deeply from the well of cognitive science. [Posts](http://www.cognition.ens.fr/~alphapsy/blog/?2006/09/16/28-naive-biology) like [this](http://www.cognition.ens.fr/~alphapsy/blog/?2006/09/16/28-naive-biology), exploring the cognitive grounding of our understanding of biology, are typical. Also, I want to point to you this [post](http://www.altmuslim.com/perm.php?id=1766_0_25_0_C) over at [alt.muslim](http://www.altmuslim.com/perm.php?id=1766_0_25_0_C) titled [The Evolution of Monotheism](http://www.altmuslim.com/perm.php?id=1766_0_25_0_C). I am generally skeptical of the existence of a “religious Left” in Islam, but only because I believe they are thin on the ground, not because they are non-existent. This post is witness to the existence of such a thing within the House of Islam.
