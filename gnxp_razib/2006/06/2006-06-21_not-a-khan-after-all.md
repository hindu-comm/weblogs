Not a Khan after all!

[Steve](http://isteve.com) points me to this [story](http://www.nytimes.com/2006/06/21/science/21genghis.html?hp&ex=1150948800&en=dfbe7c8270409f37&ei=5094&partner=homepage) which reports that the man reported to be the first direct line descent of Genghis Khan among Europeans is *not* a Khan. Nevertheless, the more important point holds: **the success of the Khan patrilineage seems distinctly an Asian phenomenon**, showing how fickle social status for males can be across space & time….
