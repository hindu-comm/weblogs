Fun with the GSS: Ghosts and IQ

Bloggers [Half Sigma](http://www.halfsigma.com/) and [Inductivist](https://inductivist.blogspot.com/) are having ongoing fun with the [General Social Survey](http://webapp.icpsr.umich.edu/GSS/) data set: ethnic, gender, and religious comparisons galore! Half Sigma [looks at the relationship](http://www.halfsigma.com/2006/06/religious_peopl.html) between the GSS mini-IQ test and religious belief, and finds what we [already](https://www.gnxp.com/MT2/archives/001523.html) [knew](https://www.gnxp.com/MT2/archives/001527.html).
