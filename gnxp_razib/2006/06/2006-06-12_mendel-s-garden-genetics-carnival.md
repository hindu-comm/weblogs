Mendel's Garden – Genetics "Carnival"

I’ve never been big into carnivals, but [RPM](http://scienceblogs.com/evolgen/2006/06/mendels_garden_1.php) pointed me to a new genetics focused one, [Mendel’s Garden](http://blogcarnival.com/bc/cprof_349.html). I’ve actually always wished something like this was around, so hopefully I’ll be submitting (one of the submission categories is “gene expression”)…pass the word. Looks like you just got [“scooped”](http://www.geneticsandhealth.com/2006/06/04/gene-talk-2-gene-expression/#comment-8756) Lei (though this won’t go biweekly probably). The current host is [Paul DeCelles](https://theforcethat.blogspot.com/).
