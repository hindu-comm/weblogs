You germy fat pig….

Interesting [article](http://www.boston.com/news/globe/health_science/articles/2006/05/22/gut_bugs_studied_as_a_cause_of_obesity/?page=full) in *The Boston Globe* which profiles researchers who suggest that variation in gut flora (the mix of bacteria) might be the cause of differences in body weight. Interesting fact: there are an order of magnitude more bacteria in your gut than cells in your body. Also, to my knowledge (hearing this from a microbiologist last year) this gut ecosystem hasn’t really been replicated in the lab, so that kind sucks for a scientific understanding beyond description.

Update: Aetiology [has much more](http://scienceblogs.com/aetiology/2006/05/obesity_and_your_microbes.php).
