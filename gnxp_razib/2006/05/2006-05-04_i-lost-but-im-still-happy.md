I lost, but I’m still happy

Some reader emailed me to explain that my secret identity has been blown. Well, you can [read about it here](http://www.thedailystar.net/2005/12/24/d512241403100.htm). But note that [I’m staying positive](http://www.thedailystar.net/2005/12/24/d512241403100p.htm). Remember, Clay sold more than Reuben. Yes, I love science, but singing is my soul!
