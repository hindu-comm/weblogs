Brown, itz not a bug, itz a feature

My friend Manish Vij just started a new [brown themed weblog](http://www.ultrabrown.com), aptly titled [UltraBrown](http://ultrabrown.com). Anyway, [this entry is hilarious](http://www.ultrabrown.com/posts/perspective). Talk about seeing the world only through your own lens! When I was in San Francisco once someone came up to me in a shop at Ghirardelli Square and asked “where I was from.” You see, I had bleached my hair blonde at that point, so she was wondering where the land of brown-skined blondes was! (there are [dark-skinned blondes](https://www.gnxp.com/blog/2005/08/blonde-australian-aboriginals.php) among the Aboriginals of Australia)
