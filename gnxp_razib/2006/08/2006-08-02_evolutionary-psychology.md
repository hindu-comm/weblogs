Evolutionary Psychology

The journal [*Evolutionary Psychology*](http://human-nature.com/ep/) is completely open access. It is probably worthwhile to check in there periodically. A just-published original research article, [“Do some taxa have better domain-general cognition than others? A meta-analysis of nonhuman primate studies,”](http://human-nature.com/ep/downloads/ep04149196.pdf) is getting some [press coverage](http://www.eurekalert.org/pub_releases/2006-08/dumc-anm072806.php).

It seems to me this journal contains a fair deal of book reviews and editorials. [Geoffrey Miller](https://www.gnxp.com/MT2/archives/002103.html) has a piece called [“The Asian future of evolutionary psychology”](http://human-nature.com/ep/downloads/ep04107119.pdf) that I find extremely dubious, even for a forum where speculations only loosely moored to any empirical findings are clearly invited. My take on Miller as a sensationalist who cares more about leaving vivid impressions than seeking the truth is confirmed to my satisfaction.
