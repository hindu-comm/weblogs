Godless Heather Mac Donald

The American Conservative is asking for opinions on what makes liberals and conservatives. I was surprised by Heather Mac Donald’s entry, as it seemed that she [took an opportunity](http://www.amconmag.com/2006/2006_08_28/article14.html) here to stand up for atheist conservatives. I knew Mac Donald was an unbeliever from her Luke Ford [interview](http://www.lukeford.net/profiles/profiles/heather_macdonald.htm), but I’d always assumed she would be the typical secular conservative, keeping a low profile and giving religion its due in her political movement. Since this weblog is frequented by many who are both unbelievers and Right-of-Center (as both founders of the weblog are), I thought you’d find it interesting (via [Steve](http://www.isteve.com)).

Addendum: Also, if you haven’t seen it, I highly recommend [her most recent piece](http://www.city-journal.org/html/16_3_immigration_reform.html) about immigration in City Journal.
