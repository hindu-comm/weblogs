Might be on E! by mistake

Lisa Loeb has a new show, [\# 1 single](http://www.eonline.com/On/Single/), on the E! network. Anyway, she was at the [Angelika Film Center](http://www.angelikafilmcenter.com/newyork/) on a date with some dude having coffee, and I wandered into the shot while checking out some cupcakes at the counter. Later, she walked toward the screens and cameras were pointed straight at me, and I looked up from reading Simon Blackburn’s [Think](https://www.amazon.com/exec/obidos/ASIN/0192100246/geneexpressio-20/) and glared. I don’t know if it will making to the show, but Loeb’s series starts on the 22nd of this month. The dude she was on a date with had lank brown hair and was kind of not too hot (sorry if you somehow read this googling dude). I’m wearing a red jacket.
