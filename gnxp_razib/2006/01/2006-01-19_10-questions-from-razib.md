10 questions from Razib

Over at [my other weblog](https://www.gnxp.com/) I have been doing a “10 questions” series. Here are the ones I’ve done so far:  
[John Derbyshire](https://www.gnxp.com/blog/2005/11/10-questions-for-derb.php) of *National Review* and author of [Prime Obsession](https://www.amazon.com/exec/obidos/ASIN/0452285259/geneexpressio-20/)  
[Armand Leroi](https://www.gnxp.com/blog/2005/12/10-questions-for-armand-m-leroi.php) of [Imperial College](http://www.bio.ic.ac.uk/research/amleroi/) and author of [Mutants](https://www.amazon.com/exec/obidos/ASIN/0670031100/geneexpressio-20/)  
[Warren Treadgold](https://www.gnxp.com/blog/2005/12/10-questions-for-warren-treadgold.php) of [Saint Louis University](http://www.slu.edu/departments/history/treadgold.htm) and author of [A History of Byzantine State and Society](https://www.amazon.com/exec/obidos/ASIN/0804726302/geneexpressio-20/104-4703056-1415937)  
[Dan Sperber](https://www.gnxp.com/blog/2005/12/10-questions-for-dan-sperber.php) of [Institut Jen Nicod](http://www.dan.sperber.com/) and author of [Explaining Culture](https://www.amazon.com/exec/obidos/ASIN/0631200452/geneexpressio-20/104-4703056-1415937)  
[Ken Miller](https://www.gnxp.com/blog/2006/01/10-questions-for-ken-miller.php) of [Brown University](http://research.brown.edu/myresearch/Kenneth_Miller) and author of [Finding Darwin’s God](https://www.amazon.com/exec/obidos/ASIN/0060930497/geneexpressio-20/104-4703056-1415937)
