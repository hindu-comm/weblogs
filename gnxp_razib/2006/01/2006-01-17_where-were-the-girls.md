Where were the Girls?

[](http://us.news3.yimg.com/us.i2.yimg.com/p/ap/20060114/capt.cael10301142251.rubiks_competition_cael103.jpg?x=380&y=249&sig=cC7oj6fMZ0rX82mrjCoVUg--)  
A new world record was set last Saturday, when [Leyan Lo](http://news.yahoo.com/s/ap/20060115/ap_on_fe_st/rubik_s_world_record) unscrambled a Rubik’s Cube in 11.13 seconds. Yahoo!News brings us the details.

Here are the [scoring details](http://www.speedcubing.com/) on this competition, and if you’d like to see videos of Cubes being solved you can find them on this [site](http://www.henage.net/dan/cube/caltechspring.htm). Most startling, mainly to the anti-Summers lynch mob, is that girls are involved in this activity as well, as these [pictures](http://www.henage.net/dan/cube/caltechspring.htm) show.

My question for the anti-Summers mob is why there is such a huge male-female disparity in top scores? Is it discrimination?
