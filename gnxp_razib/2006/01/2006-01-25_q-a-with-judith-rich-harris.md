Q & A with Judith Rich Harris

[Judith Rich Harris](http://home.att.net/~xchar/tna/) is author of [The Nurture Assumption](https://www.amazon.com/exec/obidos/ASIN/0684857073/geneexpressio-20/104-4703056-1415937) and the forthcoming [No Two Alike: Human Nature and Human Individuality](https://www.amazon.com/exec/obidos/ASIN/0393059480/geneexpressio-20/104-4703056-1415937). Her controversial thesis is that parents don’t matter, genes and peers do, in making you who you are as a person. **You can read my [Q & A with her](https://www.gnxp.com/blog/2006/01/10-questions-for-judith-rich-harris.php)** over at [my other website](https://www.gnxp.com/blog/2006/01/10-questions-for-judith-rich-harris.php). Here is the [paper](http://www.apa.org/journals/features/rev1023458.pdf) (PDF) that started it all….
