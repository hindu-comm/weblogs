Transhumanist delusions about the first derivative?

In response to a skeptical response to my post below from [RPM](http://scienceblogs.com/evolgen/) I have [posted an entry](https://www.gnxp.com/blog/2006/01/perception-of-change-reality-or.php) on my [other weblog](https://www.gnxp.com) asking whether I am deluding myself in thinking that our generation is at an [axial](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Axial_Age) pivot in the progression of ages. If [previous](https://www.gnxp.com/MT2/archives/003598.html) [posts](https://www.gnxp.com/MT2/archives/003627.html) on this topic are any clue, the [discussion](http://www.haloscan.com/comments/raldanash/113872844360759877/) should be spirited.
