Peter Frost, dark men & fair women

Since very few of you have likely read [Fair Women, Dark Men: the Forgotten Roots of Racial Prejudice](https://www.amazon.com/exec/obidos/ASIN/1877275727/geneexpressio-20/) by Peter Frost, I’d like to you point you [to his website](http://pages.globetrotter.net/peter_frost61z/), where he introduces many of his ideas in a series of essays. Steve also has an essay on based [on Frost’s ideas](http://www.vdare.com/sailer/050612_blondes.htm), and you might find this paper by Frost, [European hair and eye color A case of frequency-dependent sexual selection?](http://www.sciencedirect.com/science?_ob=ArticleURL&_udi=B6T6H-4H68T8M-1&_coverDate=09%2F26%2F2005&_alid=359678340&_rdoc=1&_fmt=&_orig=search&_qd=1&_cdi=5031&_sort=d&view=c&_acct=C000050221&_version=1&_urlVersion=0&_userid=10&md5=8debf40f1654867eb564a7a5815acace), of interest.
