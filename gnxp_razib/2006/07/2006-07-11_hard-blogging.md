Hard blogging

[John Hawks](http://johnhawks.net/weblog) is the [Chris](http://scienceblogs.com/mixingmemory/) of [Mixing Memory](http://scienceblogs.com/mixingmemory/) for paleoanthropology, and [Chris](http://scienceblogs.com/mixingmemory/) of [Mixing Memory](http://scienceblogs.com/mixingmemory/) is the [John Hawks](http://johnhawks.net/weblog) of cognitive psychology. And [Robert Skipper](http://drrob.typepad.com/hpb_etc/) is the philosopher & historian of science equivalent of the pair. Can you name other weblogs that stand head & shoulders above the pack in terms of their scholarly orientations?
