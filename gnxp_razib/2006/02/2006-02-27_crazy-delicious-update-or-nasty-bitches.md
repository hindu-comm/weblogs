Crazy delicious! (update: or nasty bitches?)

This [new](http://scienceblogs.com/frinktank/) [Science Blog](http://scienceblogs.com/frinktank/) is hilarious. I *love* their tagline!  
**Update:** OK, I take it back. What kind of dreamworld do these *bitches* think they’re living in? I post something EDT giving props to their blog, and *they’re still on top* of the [Science Blogs](http://www.scienceblogs.com) front page because they posted at “5 P.M.” It’s 11 AM while I write, what the hell? Are these fuckers on another continent or something???  
Yo cousins, new boys don’t jump to the front of the line!
