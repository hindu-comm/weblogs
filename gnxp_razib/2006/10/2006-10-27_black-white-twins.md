Black & white twins

[](https://www.gnxp.com/blog/uploaded_images/alltwins-751586.jpg)  
We’ve received a lot of search engine traffic over the past few days because of the phenomenon of “black” and “white” fraternal twins (two cases within the last week). Aside from the [original post](https://www.gnxp.com/blog/2006/02/black-and-white-twins.php) on this blog, I’ve commented on it a few times at my other blog, [here](http://scienceblogs.com/gnxp/2006/10/ivory_ebony_twins_againand_aga.php), [here](http://scienceblogs.com/gnxp/2006/10/black_white_twins_again.php) and [here](http://scienceblogs.com/gnxp/2006/09/can_you_tell_if_youre_black_or.php). Also, some of you might find this 20/20 [segment](http://abcnews.go.com/GMA/story?id=1813509) on the original British twins interesting, [click on the video](http://abcnews.go.com/Video/playerIndex?id=1817335). I can’t but help wonder if Brazilian readers don’t find the commotion a bit amusing…
