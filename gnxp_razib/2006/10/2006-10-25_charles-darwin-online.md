Charles Darwin Online

Well, you know that [The Complete Work of Charles Darwin Online](http://darwin-online.org.uk/) is now live, right? But remember to give some love to the [R.A. Fisher Digital Archive](http://digital.library.adelaide.edu.au/coll/special/fisher/), where you can read his [genetical](http://digital.library.adelaide.edu.au/coll/special/fisher/genetics.html) and [non-genetical](http://digital.library.adelaide.edu.au/coll/special/fisher/stat_math.html) papers. Also, keep an eye over at [Abebooks](http://www.abebooks.com/servlet/SearchResults?sts=t&y=0&tn=genetical+theory+of+natural+selection&x=0) for cheap copies of [The Genetical Theory of Natural Selection](http://www.abebooks.com/servlet/SearchResults?sts=t&y=0&tn=genetical+theory+of+natural+selection&x=0). They’re all north of \$65 US dollars right now (that’s like \$2,000 Canadian right?), but I got my copy for \$33, so just keep checking.
