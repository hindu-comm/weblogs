The Wizard of Woz

Steve Wozniak (inventor of the personal computer) was [interviewed today](http://www.onpointradio.org/shows/2006/10/20061005_b_main.asp) on the radio show On Point. Nerds should check it out, he’s a really interesting & entertaining fellow, though it seems he has some form of Asperger’s (e.g., he keeps talking how he’s just such a great engineer! All true of course, but a more socially aware person would cultivate a bit more false modesty). Also, In Our Time returned this week with a [show](http://www.bbc.co.uk/radio4/history/inourtime/inourtime.shtml) about the Andalusian philosopher Averroes, who was such an influence on the Aristotelian Renaissance.
