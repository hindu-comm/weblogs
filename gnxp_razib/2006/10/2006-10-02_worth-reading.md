Worth reading….

Chris at Mixing Memory has [two](http://scienceblogs.com/mixingmemory/2006/10/auditory_mirror_neurons.php) [posts](http://scienceblogs.com/mixingmemory/2006/09/is_that_my_hand_or_yours_mirro.php) on [Mirror Neurons](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Mirror_neurons). Why are Mirror Neurons of interest? Go [read](http://www.edge.org/3rd_culture/ramachandran06/ramachandran06_index.html) what the brown dude has to say. Noah Millman has a definite [read the whole thing](https://www.gideonsblog.blogspot.com/2006_09_01_gideonsblog_archive.html#115956440816036739) post on Iran. The [first comment](http://comments.enetation.co.uk/comments.php?user=gideonsblogger&commentid=115956440816036739&usersite=http://www.gideonsblog.blogspot.com/2006_09_01_gideonsblog_archive.html) is pretty ironic! Also, I’ve [revisited](http://scienceblogs.com/gnxp/2006/09/can_you_tell_if_youre_black_or.php) the example of [black and white twins](https://www.gnxp.com/blog/2006/02/black-and-white-twins.php) on the other blog. This post got a lot of weird comments…some of which I had to delete. And of course, Reihan Salam’s [review](http://www.slate.com/id/2150627/) of [Idiocracy](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Idiocracy), the movie which synthesizes Beavis & Butthead and [The Genetical Theory of Natural Selection](https://www.amazon.com/exec/obidos/ASIN/0198504403/geneexpressio-20/).
