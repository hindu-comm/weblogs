Why do I oppose cultural sensitivity?

Well, because sometimes it results in [babies born without eyes](http://www.nytimes.com/2006/03/21/science/21bedo.html?_r=1&pagewanted=2&ei=5094&amp;amp;amp;amp;amp;amp;amp;amp;amp;amp;en=6f4281d2146ce9a3&hp&ex=1142917200&partner=homepage&oref=slogin)! Detailed [commentary here](https://www.gnxp.com/blog/2006/03/bedouin-pedigree-collapse.php). Think the title is asinine? Well, the National Society of Genetic Counselors [suggests that cultural respect](http://www.nsgc.org/newsroom/release_040302.asp) trumps individual and social prudence. In Britain cultural sensitivity has [resulted in serious consequences](http://news.bbc.co.uk/1/hi/programmes/newsnight/4442010.stm) for their Health Service. Man is not an island, he swims in the sea of his fellows (only a quasi-libertarian would state that as if that was news! I plead guilty)….
