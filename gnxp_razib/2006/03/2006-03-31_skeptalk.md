Skeptalk

I just found the useful [Freethought Multimedia](http://www.reitstoen.com/multimedia.php) website, which collects and archives the Internet audio/visual leavings of Richard Dawkins, Steven Pinker, Daniel Dennett, Michael Shermer, and a few related intellectuals. Courtesy of their Pinker section we get [the video](http://www.reitstoen.com/pinker.php) of Pinker’s January [talk](https://www.gnxp.com/blog/2005/12/jason-on-seed-on-pinker-on-cochran-on.php) at the Institute for Jewish Research on the [Cochran-Hardy-Harpending paper](https://www.gnxp.com/MT2/archives/004045.html).
