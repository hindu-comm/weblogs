Steven Pinker on scientific genealogy

Steven Pinker [explores](http://www.tnr.com/docprint.mhtml?i=20070806&s=pinker080607) [The Genealogy Craze in America](http://www.tnr.com/docprint.mhtml?i=20070806&s=pinker080607) in *TNR*. He covers most of the angles, and expands a bit out from a laser-like focus on scientific genealogy toward the relevance of relatedness in the evolution of social behavior.
