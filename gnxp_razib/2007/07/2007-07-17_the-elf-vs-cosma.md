The Elf vs. Cosma

I just got an email that [The Elf](http://www.janegalt.net/archives/009901.html) has decided to take aim at [Cosma Shalizi’s](http://cscs.umich.edu/%7Ecrshalizi/weblog/494.html) posts dismissing *g* & the heritability of IQ. Now, Cosma is an acquaintance of mine and we share some common interests, but here I hope the Elf and her kindred give him hell! Any GNXP readers with knowledge and spare time cycles should left the Elvenkind a helping hand.

Related: [Tyler Cowen](http://www.marginalrevolution.com/marginalrevolution/2007/07/iq-and-the-weal.html) & [Arnold Kling](http://econlog.econlib.org/archives/2007/07/ill_defend_iq.html) on IQ. And Michael Stansty [too](http://mahalanobis.twoday.net/stories/4085280/).
