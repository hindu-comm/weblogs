David Warsh on Farewell to Alms

David Warsh [does not like](http://www.economicprincipals.com/issues/07.08.12.html) [A Farewell to Alms](https://www.amazon.com/exec/obidos/ASIN/0691121354/geneexpressio-20/). Warsh is the author of [Knowledge and Wealth of Nations](https://www.amazon.com/exec/obidos/ASIN/0393329887/geneexpressio-20/) (I have the book, haven’t gotten to it, but will probably read it before Greg Clark’s book). Here is a copy of Clark’s [Genetically Capitalist](http://econ.binghamton.edu/papers/clark.pdf). Via [Tyler Cowen](http://www.marginalrevolution.com/marginalrevolution/2007/08/farewell-to-alm.html), who is also hosting a [Book Forum](http://www.marginalrevolution.com/marginalrevolution/2007/08/farewell-to-alm.html) on the topic.

Update: The [Ambrosini Critique](http://www.ambrosini.us/wordpress/) keeps posting on Greg Clark’s work. Also, Michael Stastny [smacks up](http://mahalanobis.twoday.net/stories/4165306/) Warsh a bit.
