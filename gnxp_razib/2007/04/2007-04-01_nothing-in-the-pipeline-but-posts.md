Nothing in the pipeline but posts?

If you haven’t, you might be interested in checking out [In the Pipeline](http://www.corante.com/pipeline/). Derek Lowe has been “in transition” between jobs for a month or so and has been taking the down time to crank out good quality posts everyday. It’s a joy. No doubt he’ll have a job soon, so check it out while it’s still happening….
