UK "Social Trends" 2007

Readers with an interest in UK affairs may like to know that the annual statistical survey **Social Trends** has recently been published. Go [here](http://www.statistics.gov.uk/StatBase/Product.asp?vlnk=13675) for a free download (click on the link “Full Report” for a 3Mb pdf file).

GNXP readers may find the chapter on population (migration, birth rates, etc) the most interesting.
