Changes @ ScienceBlogs

Today we debuted the [Denialism Blog](http://scienceblogs.com/denialism/), while David Dobbs of [Smooth Pebbles](http://scienceblogs.com/smoothpebbles/2007/04/farewell_to_seed_sciblog_and_w.php) bids farewell to *ScienceBlogs*. David offers cogent rationales for why he decided to leave *ScienceBlogs* (the proximate reason is that he just isn’t posting much as far as bloggers go). One thing to note that is I don’t think a blog is really worthwhile for most people without an intelligent commentariat. I’ve learned **a lot** from critiques, suggestions and recommendations from comments on my blogs over the past 5 years. Of course, the key is **intelligent**. Most humans aren’t very smart, so they’re basically just expending the minutes in your life.
