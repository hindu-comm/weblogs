French demographics

I found some interesting [fertility data](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Demographics_of_France#Fertility) for France in Wikipedia. I’ve converted them into Excel charts for easier viewing. A note for those who are interested in this topic, a [January poll](https://translate.google.com/translate?u=http%3A%2F%2Fwww.20minutes.fr%2Farticle%2F142664%2F20070228-actualite-france-Croyants-et-athees-ou-habitent-ils-en-France.php&langpair=fr%7Cen&hl=en&safe=off&amp;amp;amp;amp;amp;amp;amp;amp;ie=UTF-8&oe=UTF-8&prev=%2Flanguage_tools) suggests that 3% of French identify as Muslims. This is probably due in part to the fact that a majority of individuals whose ancestry traces to historically Muslim regions are secularized (an individual in the GNXP chat suggested that they suspect that 25% of French are Muslim because the government doesn’t take surveys. Remember, **google is your friend!**).
