PKM Zeta on This American Life

The new Showtime version of This American Life has a [segment featuring Todd Sacktor and Andre Fenton](http://www.sho.com/site/thisamericanlife/episodes.do?episodeid=127173). They published a [remarkable paper](http://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/entrez/utils/fref.fcgi?itool=AbstractPlus-def&PrId=3051&uid=16931766&db=pubmed&url=http://www.sciencemag.org/cgi/pmidlookup?view=long&pmid=16931766) ([discussed on gnxp](https://www.gnxp.com/blog/2006/08/ltp-memory.php)) last year showing that specific inhibition of PKM Zeta (a consitutively active version of protein kinase C) can erase memories.
