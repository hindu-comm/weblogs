Refugia & demographic history

John Hawks has a [long interesting post](http://johnhawks.net/weblog/2007/04/21#arctic_fox_ancient_dna_refugia_2007) on the possible phylogeographic impact of Ice Ages and Interglacials upon Neandertal demographics. I believe John had earlier [posited](http://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/entrez/query.fcgi?db=pubmed&cmd=Retrieve&dopt=AbstractPlus&list_uids=15757241&amp;query_hl=1&itool=pubmed_docsum) that worldwide variations in long effective population size might generate the differences in genetic diversity across modern human populations.
