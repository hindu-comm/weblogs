Youth is wasted on the young

The New Yorker has an [excellent article](http://www.newyorker.com/reporting/2007/04/30/070430fa_fact_gawande?currentPage=all) on geriatrics and the physiology of aging, including a mention of the classic studies in *C. elegans*. Now I know we have some older readers, and I hope they take no offense, but I have to say, shit, getting old must really suck. And for the younger readers, we are reminded that time is short– for the love of God, eat, drink, and screw while you still can.
