Count down to triple digits!

Remember back in the late 90s when there were all those “countdown to legality” websites? Well, take note, [Jacques Barzun](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Jacques_Barzun), author of [From Dawn to Decadence: 500 Years of Western Cultural Life 1500 to the Present](https://www.amazon.com/exec/obidos/ASIN/0060928832/geneexpressio-20), will be **turning 100 on November 30th of this year!** In homage to Barzun’s centenary you will note to the right a ticker which will countdown until his 100th.
