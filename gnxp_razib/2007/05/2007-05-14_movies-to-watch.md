Movies to watch

I don’t watch many films, but I thought I would pass this along…  
[Live and Become](https://www.imdb.com/title/tt0388505/), never checked the time. Great film. It was long, but the time flew by.  
[Hot Fuzz](https://www.imdb.com/title/tt0425112/), checked the time only once, 3/4 of the way through the film. Pretty good.  
[The Namesake](https://www.imdb.com/title/tt0433416/), checked the time a fair amount, especially the second half of the film. Tried to do too much.  
[Spiderman 3](https://www.imdb.com/title/tt0413300/), checked the time a lot. Am I old? The special effects didn’t do it for me. Give me some 3-D CGI and smells or something new.
