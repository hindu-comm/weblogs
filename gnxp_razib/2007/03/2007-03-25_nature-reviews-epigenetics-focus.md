Nature Reviews Epigenetics Focus

I’m working on a follow-up to my knee-jerk negative reaction to [Miller and Sweatt](http://www.neuron.org/content/article/abstract?uid=PIIS0896627307001420) last week. Surveying the field, I found that Nature Reviews Genetics just dropped an issue focusing in epigenetics with a [complementary website](http://www.nature.com/nrg/focus/epigenetics/index.html).

At least a couple of the reviews spotlight our recently acquired ability to assay DNA methylation states on a genome-wide scale. I’m reading the [Jirtle and Skinner](http://www.nature.com/nrg/journal/v8/n4/full/nrg2045.html) piece now. Go check it out and report back.
