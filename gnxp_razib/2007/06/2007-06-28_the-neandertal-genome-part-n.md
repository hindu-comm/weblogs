![caveman_1.jpg](https://i0.wp.com/blogs.discovermagazine.com/gnxp/files/2007/06/caveman_1.jpg?resize=309%2C236)![caveman_1.jpg](https://i0.wp.com/blogs.discovermagazine.com/gnxp/files/2007/06/caveman_1.jpg?resize=309%2C236)John Hawks has [the details](http://johnhawks.net/weblog/reviews/genetics/ancient/neandertal_sequencing_damage_2007.html) on a [new paper](https://dx.doi.org/10.1073/pnas.0704665104) (DOI might not work yet) coming out in *PNAS*. The researchers trying to reconstruct the Neandertal genome are reporting biases in degradation which is aiding their task. *Scientific American* has a [summary](http://www.sciam.com/article.cfm?articleID=64DFDA17-E7F2-99DF-32A2ED5FF323905D).
