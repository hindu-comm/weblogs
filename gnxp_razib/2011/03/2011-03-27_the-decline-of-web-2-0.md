The decline of web 2.0

In 2006-2007 I worked at a firm which had its own web application, and “web 2.0” was a big term in the marketing materials. This article in [DealB%k](http://dealbook.nytimes.com/2011/03/27/is-it-a-new-tech-bubble-lets-see-if-it-pops/?partner=rss&emc=rss), [Is It a New Tech Bubble? Let’s See if It Pops](http://dealbook.nytimes.com/2011/03/27/is-it-a-new-tech-bubble-lets-see-if-it-pops/?partner=rss&emc=rss), made me wonder what happened to that term.
