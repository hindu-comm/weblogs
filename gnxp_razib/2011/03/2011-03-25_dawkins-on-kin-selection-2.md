Dawkins on Kin Selection

Back in 2004 I wrote a [summary](https://www.gnxp.com/MT2/archives/002882.html) of Richard Dawkins’s oft-cited article ‘Twelve Misunderstandings of Kin Selection’. At that time the article was not, as far as I could see, available on the internet or in any easily accessible reprint. However, I have found that a free online pdf is now available, and anyone interested in the subject can read it [here](http://c2377742.cdn.cloudfiles.rackspacecloud.com/Twelve%20Misunderstandings%20of%20Kin%20Selection.pdf).
