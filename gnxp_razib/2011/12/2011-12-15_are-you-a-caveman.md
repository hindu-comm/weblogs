Are you a caveman?

[23andMe](https://www.23andme.com/you/labs/neanderthal/) finally unveiled a [Neanderthal Ancestry](https://www.23andme.com/you/labs/neanderthal/) estimation feature. I’m at \~2.4 percent. What I’m curious about is the fact that out of the 45 “friends and family” who are surveyed, only two are at 3 percent. **One of my them is my sibling who I found seems to have the Neandertal copy of a [dystrophin variant](http://blogs.discovermagazine.com/gnxp/2011/01/neandertal-admixture-revisiting-results-after-shaken-priors/)!** I have hypothesized that this may be the reason for his relatively robust build (he looks a lot like me, except kind of Neandertaloid).
Also of curiosity, I’m the least Neandertal in my family, including my parents!
