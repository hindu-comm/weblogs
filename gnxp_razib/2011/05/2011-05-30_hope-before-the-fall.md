Hope before the fall

I think it is pretty irrational to bet on the Mavericks against the Heat in the [NBA Finals](http://www.reuters.com/article/2011/05/31/us-nba-finals-wade-idUSTRE74U0GQ20110531). And since my Celtics lost I haven’t been following what’s going on closely, but I hope Jason Kidd gets his ring.
