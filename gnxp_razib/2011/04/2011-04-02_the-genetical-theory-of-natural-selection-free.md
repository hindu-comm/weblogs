The Genetical Theory of Natural Selection, free!

Long time readers are aware that one of my favorite books is R. A. Fisher’s [The Genetical Theory of Natural Selection](https://www.amazon.com/exec/obidos/ASIN/0198504403/geneexpressio-20). It’s a touch on the spendy side for a slim, though dense, book. But looking for stuff that’s public domain for my [Kindle](https://www.amazon.com/exec/obidos/ASIN/B002FQJT3Q/geneexpressio-20) I noticed that you can get the [1930 version free](http://www.archive.org/details/geneticaltheoryo031631mbp). And it’s not just in Kindle formats, you can get it in PDF as well and read it on your computer. I think of [TGTNG](https://www.amazon.com/exec/obidos/ASIN/0198504403/geneexpressio-20) as somewhat like the [Critique of Pure Reason](https://www.amazon.com/exec/obidos/ASIN/0521657296/geneexpressio-20) of evolutionary genetics; even if your inclination is to rubbish it, it’s an importance place to begin.
