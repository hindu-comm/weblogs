Beware of one tree!

Sometimes in the comments of this weblog people get into heated disagreements about **one** figure and its proper interpretation. I don’t get much involved most of the time because **different visualization techniques often differ on the margin, so getting obsessed with minor details is a fool’s errand.** For example, in the [paper I reviewed below](http://blogs.discovermagazine.com/gnxp/2011/04/the-evolutionary-effect-of-the-sky-gods/) there was a [neighbor-joining phylogenetic tree](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Neighbor-joining). Take a look. The length of the branches are proportional to genetic distance.
