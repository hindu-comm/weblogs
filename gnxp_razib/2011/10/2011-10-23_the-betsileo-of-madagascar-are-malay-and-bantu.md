The Betsileo of Madagascar are Malay and Bantu

A month ago I [posted the genetic results of a Malagasy individual](http://blogs.discovermagazine.com/gnxp/2011/09/the-merina-of-madagascar-are-malay-and-bantu/) of Merina identity. Today I post those for someone of Betsileo heritage. All the technical details are the same. You can find all the ADMIXTURE and PCA files [here](https://docs.google.com/leaf?id=0B8Tdg8RZEWOONTM3Y2FjNDctMTdmOC00NjJhLThlZGItYjdjNzFjZjdjZWJj&hl=en_US).

This genotyping was paid for by readers. I’ll update the post with the names of those who contributed below the fold later. If you contributed but don’t want to be named, email me at contactgnxp -at- gmail -dot- com, and I’ll leave you off the list.
