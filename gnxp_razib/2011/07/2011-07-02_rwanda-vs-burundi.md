Rwanda vs. Burundi

Reading [Dancing in the Glory of Monsters: The Collapse of the Congo and the Great War of Africa](https://www.amazon.com/exec/obidos/ASIN/1586489291/geneexpressio-20). [Rwanda](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Rwanda) looms large in the narrative. Out of curiosity I wanted to use Google Data Explorer to compare Rwanda to [Burundi](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Burundi). The two nations are almost mirror images in various demographic statistics (e.g., ratio of Tutsi to Hutu, etc.), with the main difference between that Rwanda experienced a period of populist Hutu domination between the 1960s and the genocide.
