**What do you say of a man, whom even Stalin feared to leave his daughter alone with?**

Beria was among the powerful of the Soviet leaders, no actually read the most powerful. As chief of the dreaded NKVD, predecessor to KGB, he literally called the shots. He presided over the expansion of the Gulags, set up the ***Sharashka,*** the secret detention facilities for scientists and engineers. And after the war, oversaw the communist takeover of all state institutions in Central and Eastern Europe.

Now he was also a notorious sexual predator, or womanizer whichever way you choose to call him. He used his power well, to force himself on women, who could not afford to complain against him. He would prowl the streets of Moscow, point to a young woman to whom he had taken a fancy to. She in turn, would be carted away to his mansion, treated to fine wine and food, before being taken into his private chamber and being raped.

And after the incident, the victim was handed a flower boquet, to imply it was consensual sex. Refusal would usually mean arrest, so she had no other choice but to comply. Quite a lot of women also submitted to him, for the freedom of their imprisoned relatives. As it happened to **Tatiana Okunevskaya,** a well known actress,who was taken to his dacha, and asked to submit, if she wanted her father to be freed from prison. In truth her father along with other relatives had already been executed. And she was later arrested on trumped up charges,placed in solitary confinement.

Such was his notorious reputation, that even Stalin never trusted him. There was this incident of Stalin’s daughter Svetlana, being alone with Beria at his house, when he called her and asked her to get out from there as soon as she could. Yes the guy spared none in his lust.

When he complimented the daughter of **Alexander Poskrebyshev,** on her beauty, he pulled her aside, and warned her never to accept a lift from Beria. He once shadowed the daughter in law of **Kliment Voroshilov,** one of the top 5 Marshals**,** all the way in his car.

He asked his bodyguard Colonel Rafael Semyonovich Sarkisov to keep a list of the names and phones of all his sexual encounters,which would also prove to be his nemesis. When his fall from power began , Sarkisov simply passed the list to **Viktor Abakumov,** former head of SMERSH, a grouping of 3 counter intelligence agencies of the Red Army. Stalin himself was seeking to get rid of Beria, who by now had become an embarassment, and the list was quite handy.

Skeletal remains of many young girls, were found in the garden of his Moscow villa, during the mid-90s. It was the testimony of his former bodyguards Sarkisov and Colonel Sardion Nikolaevich Nadaraia, that bought to light the guy’s sexual predations.

And like most tyrants, he met a bloody end after the death of Stalin,when he sought to form a Govt, along with close ally Malenkov, and did call the shots for quite some time. However Kruschev, along with Molotov, Malenkov and Nikolai Bulganin, seized power in a coup. Arrested on June 26, 1953, he was found guilty of treason, terrorism( Purge of the Red Army) and Counter Revolutionary activity. He was shot dead on December 23, 1953, as he collapsed to his knees and pleading for his life. Quite an ending to a guy who once upon a time, lorded it over the Soviet Empire.
