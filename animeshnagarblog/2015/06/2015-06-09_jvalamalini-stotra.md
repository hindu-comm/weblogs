jwAlAmAlinI stotra

The following stotras belongs to some lost shAktAgam ( shAkta tantra ).
Jain nAstikas have adopted this stotras from hindU tantras for worship
of shAsan devtI ‘ jvAlAmalinI ‘.The stotra belongas to adhaAmanya (
adhasrotas ) .
