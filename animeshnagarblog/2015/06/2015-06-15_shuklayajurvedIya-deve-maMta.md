शुक्लयजुर्वेदीय देवे मंत्रा:

Only mahArAshTra based shuklayajurvedIns uses this mantras in
pushpAnjalI ( mantra pushpaM ) .In present even among them rarely full
mantras are recited. Out of complete 23 mantras only 7 mantras are
recited presently .These mantras are called **deve** because they are
addressed to *vishvedevtas* ( all gods ) and all mantras of ‘*deve
collection*‘ have word ‘devA’ in them .Modern vAjasaneyA employs only
following mantras –  
1) गणानांत्वा०  
2) अम्बेअम्बिके ०  
3) यज्ञेनयज्ञ०  
4)राजाधिराजाय०  
5) स्वस्तिसम्राज्यम् ०  
6)विश्वत ०  
7) इष्ट गायत्री
