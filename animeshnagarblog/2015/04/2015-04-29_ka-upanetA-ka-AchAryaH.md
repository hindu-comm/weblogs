Once devas and rRishIs asked bramhA , Who among us is old ( jAnavrIddha
) ? Who is initiator ? Who is Acharya ? Who is qualified for post of
bramhA ? Hence told bramhA  
”aTharvan is old, aTharvan is  
initiator , aTharvan is Acharya, aTharvan is qualified for post of
bramhA as told in richA-**bramhajyeshThA**” .
