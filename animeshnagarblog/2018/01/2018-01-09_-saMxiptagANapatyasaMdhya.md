॥ संक्षिप्तगाणपत्यसंध्याप्रयोगः ॥

SandhyA vandanam for gANapatyAs ( upasaka of gaNapatI ). Those who are
initiated in any mantra of srI gaNesha are eligible ( adhikArI ) for
this sandhyA.
