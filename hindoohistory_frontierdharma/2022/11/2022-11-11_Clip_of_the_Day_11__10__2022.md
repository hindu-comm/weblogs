From the Silver State, 1909:

![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fbucketeer-e05bbc84-baa3-437e-9518-adb32be77984.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F9f9653bf-2fb6-4b53-bd5f-23ef76bc4bdd_187x510.jpeg "Image")

"Sunday evening at 7:30 there will be a most interesting service. A converted Hindoo will appear in the costume of his native land and tell of Hindooism and why he forsook the worship of Krishna for the living God and His Son, Jesus Christ. This young man has been in America three years, and is preparing himself for missionary work among his people in India"
