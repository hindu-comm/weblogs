An ad for a doctor, published in the Record-Union in 1891:

![](https://substackcdn.com/image/fetch/w_1456,c_limit,f_auto,q_auto:good,fl_progressive:steep/https%3A%2F%2Fbucketeer-e05bbc84-baa3-437e-9518-adb32be77984.s3.amazonaws.com%2Fpublic%2Fimages%2F59c75e61-1ba2-4e54-a23d-d64a77a3b517_251x602.jpeg "Image")

"We have thrown away pity upon the Hindoo fanatics who sacrifice their lives by casting themselves beneath the chariot of the God Juggernaut, and allow them to be ground and mangled by the terrible wheels. Are we not in this Christian country doing the same thing with our health?"
