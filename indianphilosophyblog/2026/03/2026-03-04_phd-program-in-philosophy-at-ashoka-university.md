Ashoka University is now accepting applications for our PhD program in Philosophy (details here:[https://www.ashoka.edu.in/programme/phd-programme-in-philosophy/](https://www.ashoka.edu.in/programme/phd-programme-in-philosophy/)). The program is five years long andis fully funded. Students do coursework for two years then write a dissertation. International applicants are welcome. Our faculty with expertise in Indian philosophy include Arindam Chakrabarti, Alex Watsonand Justin Holder. Ashoka also has a Sanskrit program teaching both introductory and advanced Sanskrit language courses.
