Dear friends,

first an apology, for my lack of posting - personal circumstances have necessitated my departure from X/Twitter and have kept me from writing with any kind of regularity. I hope you can forgive me for that. I can’t explain the details.

Second, I will begin writing again, but only to you my friends, through a private newsletter. New requests for a subscription have to be approved by me. I will soon produce a more thorough analysis, but I believe the time has come for a greater deepening of the *private internet*. Group chats, direct messages, non-public content and discussion are now necessary to prevent the total dissolution of thought into a morass of grifting, circular churn.

A huge thank-you to those who have kept their subscription running, I am immensely grateful and humbled actually, that so many have chosen to continue their support. I hope to repay this.

Keep an eye on your inbox, I will write again soon!

Yours,

SAH
