My latest book is now out and [available on Amazon](https://www.amazon.co.uk/dp/B0CQ2HDRRX), in both paperback and Kindle format. With any luck you should get it before Christmas, should you wish to surprise a loved one!

The book is a compilation of essays broken down by theme, most of them published on my Substack over the last year, some of them behind a paywall. It builds on what has become my trademark, a combination of prehistoric and historic topics that explore lesser-known events or phenomena, combined with modern anthropology of the strange and unusual, which you won’t easily find in mainstream publications.

A huge thanks to you all, my readers and subscribers. Without your support I wouldn’t have considered writing even one, let alone two books!

Stay tuned, I have many exciting ideas and projects for 2024.
