Dr. [Marie Favereau](https://www.parisnanterre.fr/mme-marie-favereau-doumenjou-favereau--929647.kjsp?RH=1569504797814) is an associate professor at [Paris Nanterre University](https://www.parisnanterre.fr/mme-marie-favereau-doumenjou-favereau--929647.kjsp?RH=1569504797814). Her [academic work](https://scholar.google.com/scholar?hl=en&as_sdt=0%2C38&q=Marie+Favereau&btnG=) has been on the Mamlukes of Egypt and the Mongol Golden Horde. Most recently, she is the author of [The Horde: How the Mongols Changed the World](https://www.amazon.com/exec/obidos/ASIN/B08VF4PJ1M/geneexpressio-20).
