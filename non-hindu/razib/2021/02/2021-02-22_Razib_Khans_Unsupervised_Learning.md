Young is the author of [Growing Up in Moscow: Memories of a Soviet Girlhood](https://www.amazon.com/exec/obidos/ASIN/0899195113/geneexpressio-20) and [Ceasefire!: Why Women and Men Must Join Forces to Achieve True Equality](https://www.amazon.com/exec/obidos/ASIN/0684834421/geneexpressio-20).
