Back then he was leading a team at Microsoft, and moonlighting as a writer. Over the last twenty years, he’s changed careers, and become a full-time writer and speaker. He’s the author of three science fiction books, [Crux](https://www.amazon.com/exec/obidos/ASIN/B00TOZI7J8//geneexpressio-20), [Apex](https://www.amazon.com/exec/obidos/ASIN/B00K4BA758//geneexpressio-20), and [Nexus](https://www.amazon.com/exec/obidos/ASIN/B00TOZI7FM//geneexpressio-20).
