Recently, I’ve been trying out the platform Clubhouse (you can follow me there, I’m “Razib Khan”).

Tonight, Friday, February 12th, I’m going to hold [a discussion](https://www.joinclubhouse.com/event/myoRj90W) on my two Substack Indian Genetics deep dives from a couple weeks ago’ plus the genetic history of India more broadly with my friends [David Mittelman](https://twitter.com/evolvability?lang=en) and [Carlos Bustamante](https://twitter.com/cdbustamante). If you have the Clubhouse app (iPhone only so far) and would like to join us, here is the link: [https://www.joinclubhouse.com/event/myoRj90W](https://www.joinclubhouse.com/event/myoRj90W)

The original pieces are available for paying Substack subscribers here:

- [Stark Truth About Aryans: a story of
  India](https://razib.substack.com/p/stark-truth-about-aryans-a-story)

- [Stark Truth About Humans: a story of
  India](https://razib.substack.com/p/stark-truth-about-humans-a-story)

(I’ll be at Clubhouse starting a couple hours before that as well, to join an [event](https://www.joinclubhouse.com/event/xj6qv4eN) David is hosting with [Nick Thompson](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Nicholas_Thompson_(editor)), now CEO of *The Atlantic.*)
