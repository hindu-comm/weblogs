Allen Ancient DNA Resource in PLINK format

The [Reich lab released a bunch of data in January 2021](https://reich.hms.harvard.edu/allen-ancient-dna-resource-aadr-downloadable-genotypes-present-day-and-ancient-dna-data). Someone emailed me about the format. I converted their earlier release to PLINK (PEDIGREE) format, and they wondered if I could do the same again for this relase. I did so. Remember that the “FAMILY ID” is the population as identified from their annotation files.

Here are the files:
