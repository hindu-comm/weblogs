But, I can say I’ve read [Byzantine State and Society](https://www.amazon.com/exec/obidos/ASIN/B01M7SP1L7/geneexpressio-20) three times front to back.

[Ten Months After George Floyd’s Death, Minneapolis Residents Are at War Over Policing](https://www.nytimes.com/2021/03/28/us/minneapolis-george-floyd.html). I’m feeling more people will make sure to stay in the suburbs or small towns. The re-urbanization wave was overplayed but it was real. But it’s a clown show now in these big cities.

[Welcome to the Decade of Concern](https://scholars-stage.blogspot.com/2021/04/welcome-to-decade-of-concern.html).
