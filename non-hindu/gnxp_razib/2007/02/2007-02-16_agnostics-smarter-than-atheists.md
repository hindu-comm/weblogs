Agnostics smarter than Atheists?

The always fascinating [Ron Gunhame](https://inductivist.blogspot.com/2007/02/agnostics-are-most-intelligent-if-you.html) parses the [GSS](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/General_Social_Survey) and religion & intelligence data. He finds:  
**Mean vocabulary score – Whites**  
6.52 Doesn’t believe  
7.24 No way to find out  
6.96 Some higher power  
6.02 Believes sometimes  
6.42 Believes but doubts  
6.05 Knows God exists  
Ron concludes that atheists are less intelligent than agnostics from this, but Jason Malloy in the [comments](https://inductivist.blogspot.com/2007/02/agnostics-are-most-intelligent-if-you.html#c2475761975610632358) has several follow ups which clear up the issue a bit and suggest that Ron spoke too soon.
