Bacchus is very good!

I went to [Bacchus](http://brooklyn.citysearch.com/profile/37011711/brooklyn_ny/bacchus.html) on Atlantic Avenue in Brooklyn tonight. Great food, great wine. But here is why I am giving them props: ***habanero* tabasco sauce!**. Yes, instead of offering me cayenne tabasco, they actually offered me a nice spicy condiment. And I shall remain eternally grateful. So:  
Bacchus  
409 Atlantic Ave  
Brooklyn, NY 11217-1702  
Phone: (718) 852-1572  
Check it out. And ask for ***hot*** sauce!
