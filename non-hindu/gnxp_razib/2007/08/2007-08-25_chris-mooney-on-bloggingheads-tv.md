Chris Mooney on bloggingheads.tv

Check it, Chris Mooney is on [bloggingheads.tv](http://bloggingheads.tv/video.php?id=367). He’s promoting his book [Storm World](https://www.amazon.com/exec/obidos/ASIN/0151012873/geneexpressio-20), which is a really good read. I can’t speak in detail to the area of science which Chris covers, but the bigger picture issue of the “intersection” between public policy and the culture of science and the ensuing controversies have a universal resonance.
