Evolution & genetics media

Thought I’d pass on two links. Greg Laden has [posted](http://scienceblogs.com/gregladen/2007/12/john_maynard_smith_interview.php) a
[video](http://scienceblogs.com/gregladen/2007/12/john_maynard_smith_interview.php) of Robert Wright’s interview with the late [John Maynard Smith](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/John_Maynard_Smith). I’ve watched it before, it’s a great interview (or, at least Smith’s responses are really interesting & meaty quite often). Also, this week’s *In Our Time* covers [Genetic Mutation](http://www.bbc.co.uk/radio4/history/inourtime/inourtime.shtml). If names like [Hugo de Vries](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Hugo_de_Vries) are unfamiliar to you, probably worth listening to.
