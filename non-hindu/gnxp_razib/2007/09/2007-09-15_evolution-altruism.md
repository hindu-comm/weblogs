Evolution & altruism

Olivia Judson’s piece [The Selfless Gene](http://www.theatlantic.com/doc/prem/200710/generosity-and-evolution) in *The Atlantic* is a pretty good survey of the various theories of the evolution of altruism. If you read [Narrow Roads of Gene Land](https://www.amazon.com/exec/obidos/ASIN/0716745305/geneexpressio-20) or [Natural Selection and Social Theory](https://www.amazon.com/exec/obidos/ASIN/0195130626/geneexpressio-20/), nothing new, but if you aren’t familiar with the lay of the land it’s worth checking out. But you can’t find it free online, so you’ll have to get a subscription to *The Atlantic* or run out to the book store and sneak a peak.
