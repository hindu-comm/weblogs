Males as a gamble, etc., at the APA

In this invited address to the American Psychological Association, Roy Baumeister has quite a lot of fun with his topic: [“Is there anything good about men?”](http://www.psy.fsu.edu/~baumeistertice/goodaboutmen.htm) His major themes will be familiar to GNXP regulars–Larry Summers, the high male variance of IQ, genetic and cultural explanations, a rejection of the culture war and a call for science–but it’s a tale well told. A typical quote:

> \[M\]en really are better AND worse than women.

Great to see the APA having this kind of discussion.

(Hat tip: Bryan Caplan of [Econlog](http://econlog.econlib.org/archives/2007/09/understanding_g.html).)
