Disease & genetics

*Nature* today [published a paper](http://www.nature.com/nature/journal/v447/n7145/abs/nature05911.html) which reports on a massive [genome association](http://www.answers.com/topic/genetic-association) study of the British white population with the intent of smoking out loci implicated in disease. **The paper is open access, so you can read it yourself!** There are many [popular press articles](https://news.google.com/news?hl=en&ned=us&ie=UTF-8&ncl=1117024301), but as usual [p-ter has a summary](https://www.gnxp.com/blog/2007/06/genome-wide-association-studies-in-uk.php) of the important points.
