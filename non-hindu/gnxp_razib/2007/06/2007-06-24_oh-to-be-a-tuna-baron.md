Oh to be a tuna baron

I enjoyed [this review](http://www2.washingtonmonthly.com/features/2007/0706.koerner.html) of [The Sushi Economy](https://www.amazon.com/Sushi-Economy-Globalization-Making-Delicacy/dp/1592402941/ref=pd_bbs_sr_1/002-4543496-2443260?ie=UTF8&s=books&qid=1182712019&sr=8-1), mainly because I was fully unaware of the fact that there are people referred to as “tuna barons”. Apparently they’re Australian aquaculturists that have made a killing on the rising popularity of sushi. It doesn’t have quite the ring of “oil baron”, but hey, you take what you can get.
