Bound to repeat

[Xun Zi](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Xun_Zi), the least idealistic of the great Confucian sages stated, “[Mozi](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Mo_tzu) was blinded by utility and did not understand culture.” Blinded by the utility, it seems a problem which intellectuals are still plagued by, until they realize they are bounded by the culture….
