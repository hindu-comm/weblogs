The American Scene – part II

[The American Scene](http://www.theamericanscene.com/) has just premiered as a new group blog with a fresh look & feel. [Daniel Larison](http://larison.org/) is a contributor. Now, keeping in mind that many of you use spiffy RSS readers with AJAX functionality which entails a non-trivial client side computational overhead be careful if you’re on an older machine. Larison has a tendency to go “machine gun” in regards to post frequency and he might blow up your computer’s CPU.
