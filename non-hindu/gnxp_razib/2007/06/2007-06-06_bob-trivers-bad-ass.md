Bob Trivers – bad ass?

About six months ago the great evolutionary biologist [Robert Trivers](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Robert_L._Trivers) won the [Crafoord Prize](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Crafoord_Prize). Well, he is now in a [public dispute](http://www.insidehighered.com/news/2007/05/29/lecture) with Alan Derschowitz, resulting in the cancellation of a talk at Harvard. *Frontpage Magazine* has an [amusing article](http://frontpagemagazine.com/Articles/ReadArticle.asp?ID=28494) up.
