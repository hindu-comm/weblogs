The Pig Men Cometh

[](https://www.gnxp.com/blog/uploaded_images/s-george-785454.jpg)“I wish there were pig-men. You get a few of those pig-men walking around, suddenly I’m looking a lot better.”

-George Constanza of Seinfeld
