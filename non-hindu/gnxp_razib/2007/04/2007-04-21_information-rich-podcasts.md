Information-rich podcasts

Doing dissections is boring. Learning about international politics while doing dissections is less boring. Hence, I recommend checking about the [Carnegie Council for Ethics in International Affairs podcast](http://www.cceia.org/resources/audio/rss/index.html). Here’s a [list of more educational podcasts](https://helpychalk.blogspot.com/2006/04/downloadable-lectures.html) from Big Monkey, Helpy Chalk, including a [conference](http://www.philosophie.uni-mainz.de/metzinger/assc/2/mp3_archive.html) on Neural Correlates of Consciousness.

On a less nerdy tip, these [mixes](http://siik.org/the-mixes) by siik are dope. And the [Mixtape Show](feed://feeds.feedburner.com/mixtapeshow)… Wack Shit – Not Allowed. Ever.
