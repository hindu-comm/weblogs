Spencer Wells in Vanity Fair

Spencer Wells, author of [The Journey of Man](https://www.amazon.com/exec/obidos/ASIN/0812971469/geneexpressio-20), has a [write up](http://www.vanityfair.com/culture/features/2007/07/genographic200707) of [The Genographic Project](https://www3.nationalgeographic.com/genographic/) and “Out of Africa” in *Vanity Fair*. Nothing new or groundbreaking, **but you know historical population genetics has come a long way if it’s in *Vanity Fair***. On a related note *The Genographic Project* has finally [yielded a major paper](http://genetics.plosjournals.org/perlserv/?request=get-document&doi=10.1371/journal.pgen.0030104) in PLOS Genetics. More method than meat though.  
Via [Eye on DNA](http://www.eyeondna.com/).
