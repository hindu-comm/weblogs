Giant Lion-Eating Chimps

Not an April Fool hoax, but apparently an entirely serious [story](http://environment.guardian.co.uk/conservation/story/0,,2126328,00.html) in the **Guardian**. Actually, one sighting of a chimp nibbling the carcass of a leopard doesn’t quite add up to ‘lion-eating’, but the facts as reported are impressive enough. Read it and make up your own mind.
