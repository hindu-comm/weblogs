Jacques Barzun on video

Mary Murphy of [The Jacques Barzun Centennial](http://barzuncentennial.murphywong.net/) sent me this [link](http://www.geminiink.org/JacquesBarzun.mp4) to a [video](http://www.geminiink.org/JacquesBarzun.mp4) of Jacques Barzun accepting an award recently (large file). He talks about his great-grandmother who would talk about events in her life when he was young. She was born in 1830.
