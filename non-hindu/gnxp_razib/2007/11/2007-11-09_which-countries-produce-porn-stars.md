Which countries produce porn stars?

Just curious. I have a rough intuition. So I went to [Porn stars by nationality](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Category:Porn_stars_by_nationality) on Wikipedia. I clicked the entries and tallied up the number of porn stars. I excluded English speaking countries since I figure that the listing would be biased that way because this is an English language encyclopedia.
