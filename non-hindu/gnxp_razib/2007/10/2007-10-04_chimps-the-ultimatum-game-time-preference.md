Chimps, the ultimatum game & time preference

In *Science* there’s a new report, [Chimpanzees Are Rational Maximizers in an Ultimatum Game](http://www.sciencemag.org/cgi/content/abstract/318/5847/107?rss=1); the title says it all. On the other hand, [The Evolutionary Origins of Human Patience: Temporal Preferences in Chimpanzees, Bonobos, and Human Adults](http://www.current-biology.com/content/article/abstract?uid=PIIS0960982207018507) doesn’t clue you in in the title that **chimpanzees have *lower* [time preference](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Time_preference)** when it come food (they’re more patient and will take a bigger later payoff than humans). The most recent issue of *The Economist* [covers these results](http://economist.com/science/displaystory.cfm?story_id=9898270) as well as the finding of [heritability in response to the ultimatum game](https://www.gnxp.com/blog/2007/10/heritability-of-ultimatum-game.php).
