Over at [GNXP Classic](https://www.gnxp.com/blog/2007/03/plos-one-genetics-and-genomics.php) Ikwa points to some papers from the [PLOS One](http://www.plosone.org/home.action) project, which facilitates public feedback in the peer review process.   Phencyclidine
