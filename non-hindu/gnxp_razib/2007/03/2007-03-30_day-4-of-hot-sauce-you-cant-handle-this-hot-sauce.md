Day 4 of hot sauce – You Can’t Handle this Hot Sauce

![canthandle.jpg](https://i0.wp.com/blogs.discovermagazine.com/gnxp/files/2007/03/canthandle.jpg?resize=350%2C355)![canthandle.jpg](https://i0.wp.com/blogs.discovermagazine.com/gnxp/files/2007/03/canthandle.jpg?resize=350%2C355)[You Can’t Handle This Hot Sauce](http://www.hotsauceworld.com/youcanhanthi.html) is subject to the same critique as Dave’s Insanity.
