Day 5 of hot sauce – Mad Dog Liquid Fire

For [Mad Dog Liquid Fire](http://www.cosmicchile.com/site/mad-dog-liquid-fire.html) the same critique that I applied to Ass in Space applies. The only difference is that there was a nice tangy smoked flavor to it, so I will actually give it a 4 out of 10 (I was too disgusted with Ass in Space‘s lack of spice to rate it).
