The unbelieving conservatives

Daniel Larison points me to [this piece](http://www.newenglishreview.org/custpage.cfm?frm=5778&sec_id=5778) in The New English review which discusses intersection of unbelief and conservatism, sparked in part by Heather Mac Donald’s “coming out” last year. The article references [my 10 questions](https://www.gnxp.com/blog/2007/01/10-questions-for-heather-mac-donald.php) with Heather. Over at Real Clear Politics there is [a response](http://www.realclearpolitics.com/articles/2007/03/atheists_conservatives_and_chr.html) to the piece in TNE. Finally, Larison offers [his critique](http://larison.org/2007/03/11/backlash/).

(you can view the technorati responses to the interview [here](http://technorati.com/search/www.gnxp.com%2Fblog%2F2007%2F01%2F10-questions-for-heather-mac-donald.php))
