Why is New York coffee so bad?

A local coffee shop that I frequent carries [Barista Magazine](http://www.baristamagazine.com/). Though I’m not a “coffee nerd,” I pick it up to pass the time, and I noted an article which mentioned that the coffee is New York City is **shockingly bad**. Not that it is as bad, for example, as the coffee in Billings, Montana (no offense Billiings!). Rather, for a city famous for its food & culture it seems surprising that one would have to rely upon Starbucks as the font of all bean goodness. So what is it? Do people here disagree?^(\*)  
\* Please don’t offer an opinion if you haven’t had Pacific Northwest coffee!
