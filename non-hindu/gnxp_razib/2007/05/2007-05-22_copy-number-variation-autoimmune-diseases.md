Copy number variation & autoimmune diseases

[*FCGR3B* copy number variation is associated with susceptibility to systemic, but not organ-specific, autoimmunity](http://www.nature.com/ng/journal/vaop/ncurrent/full/ng2046.html). The “Brief Communication” in *Nature* is open access. Here’s a [popular press](http://www3.imperial.ac.uk/newsandeventspggrp/imperialcollege/newssummary/news_22-5-2007-14-49-46?newsid=11754) summation.
