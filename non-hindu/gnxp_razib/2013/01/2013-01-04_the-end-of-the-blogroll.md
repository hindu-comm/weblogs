The end of the blogroll

One of the major annoyances with the redesign of this weblog was that its precipitous nature was such that many of the sidebar links, etc., were removed. But, it did make me admit a major point: **blogrolls are pretty much dead**. In the early years of the blogsophere they served as a way to share traffic and endorse sites of interest. But with the rise of RSS, and later Twitter and its confederates they went into decline. By the end I barely recalled which sites I had on my blogroll; most of them I followed in via RSS. So I’m not going to recreate one at this point. Rather, if you want to get a sampling of what I read and such, please see my [Pinboard page](http://pinboard.in/u:gnxp) (to which you can [subscribe via RSS](http://feeds.pinboard.in/rss/secret:5f68175b50bbb2200605/u:gnxp/) if it suits you). And of course you can follow me on [Twitter](https://twitter.com/razibkhan), though that will include my banter with other people and such. A more likely avenue is to note which websites I link to in my posts…though I’m not a copious linker to other blogs at this point….
