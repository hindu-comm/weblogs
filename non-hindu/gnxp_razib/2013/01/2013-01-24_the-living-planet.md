The Living Planet

I realized that my [post on David Attenborough](http://blogs.discovermagazine.com/gnxp/2013/01/the-abating-of-the-human-plague/#.UQD0n1QR-So) simply presumed you know who the man is. The reality is who he is is less critical than what he’s done. If you haven’t seen it, you need to see [The Living Planet](https://www.youtube.com/results?search_query=the+living+planet&oq=the+living+plane&gs_l=youtube.3.0.0l10.797.2900.0.3890.16.11.0.1.1.1.315.1979.0j1j6j1.8.0...0.0...1ac.1.0KOU1-H4gco).
