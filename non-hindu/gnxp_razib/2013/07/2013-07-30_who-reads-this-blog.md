Who reads this blog

Every now and then [Ed Yong](http://phenomena.nationalgeographic.com/2013/07/29/who-are-you-the-2013-edition/) has a “de-lurking” post up. That reminds me that it is often useful for long time readers who rarely comment, as they see they are not alone. I won’t put any stipulations on what you have to say (aside from that it has to be about who you are, etc.). So in imitation of Ed I figured that this is as good a time as any to open up the floor (and, I know there’s a large intersection of readership, so you may be practiced).

**Addendum:** I put the [2011 reader survey raw results](https://docs.google.com/spreadsheet/ccc?key=0AsTdg8RZEWOOdERtR2RGdlBrTHkxa2drOVB1SXpQSkE&hl=en_US#gid=0) online, if anyone wants to process them. I should do something like that again soon….
