Bay Area Population Genomics (BAPG) IX Conference

[Registration](https://docs.google.com/spreadsheet/ccc?key=0AkVPHwMq5BVZdG5pQ2U3OEEycTJaWFptTnB0U0ZpMEE#gid=0) is **free**. It will be hosted by the [Nielsen Group](http://cteg.berkeley.edu/~nielsen/resources/bapg-ix/) in Berkeley on October 5th. As I am not going to be at the methods-orgy (OK, my own peculiar perspective) that is going to be ASHG 2013 I am definitely going to BAPG to get a preview of anything that might be unveiled in Cambridge a few months later (and to be frank I [registered last June](https://groups.google.com/forum/#!forum/bayareapopulationgenomics) when it was announced).
