On Discourse

The word “discourse” is used a lot now. I’ve seen it even bleeding out of academic *discourse*, to general interest news. So I thought I’d look at the comparison between it an a few other terms in [Google’s Ngram Viewer](https://books.google.com/ngrams/graph?content=discourse%2Cdiscussion%2Cdialogue&year_start=1800&year_end=2000&corpus=15&smoothing=3&share=), which surveys mentions in books. The results were mildly interesting.
