Mezzeta California Habañero Hot Sauce

![hawt.jpg](https://i0.wp.com/blogs.discovermagazine.com/gnxp/files/hawt.jpg?resize=302%2C235)![hawt.jpg](https://i0.wp.com/blogs.discovermagazine.com/gnxp/files/hawt.jpg?resize=302%2C235)I ***really*** love shrimp & hot sauce. A lot. Over Christmas I purchased some [Mezzeta California Habañero Hot Sauce](http://www.mezzetta.com/mm5/merchant.mvc?Screen=PROD&Store_Code=mezzetta&Product_Code=10100609&Category_Code=gourmetspec). I checked out the label, no carrot juice to ruin the flavor of the pepper, but likely no pure [capsaicin](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Capsaicin) to render the aftertaste chemical. I finished it up pretty quickly, definitely a keeper, I give it a 7 out of 10. The non-hot flavors are closer to 8 or 9, but it could have been somewhat spicier….
