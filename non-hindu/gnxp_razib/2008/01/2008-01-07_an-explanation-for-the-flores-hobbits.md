An explanation for the Flores “Hobbits”?

[p-ter](https://www.gnxp.com/blog/2008/01/dwarfism-and-cell-division.php) has the [details](https://www.gnxp.com/blog/2008/01/dwarfism-and-cell-division.php). For me one of the most confusing aspects of the Hobbits were the concomitant phenomena of extremely small cranial capacities (inference: low intelligence) with relatively advanced toolkits. If it the Hobbits exhibited the pathology reported in the paper which p-ter discusses that might be the solution.
