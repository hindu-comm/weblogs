Science bloggers & million comment party

![millioncommentpary.jpg](https://i0.wp.com/blogs.discovermagazine.com/gnxp/files/millioncommentpary.jpg?resize=100%2C236)![millioncommentpary.jpg](https://i0.wp.com/blogs.discovermagazine.com/gnxp/files/millioncommentpary.jpg?resize=100%2C236)I’ll be in San Francisco on the 26^(th) at a [Million Comment Party](http://scienceblogs.com/seed/2008/09/celebrate_one_million_comments.php) for ScienceBlogs. Specifically, I’ll be [hanging with the Bleiman Brothers](http://scienceblogs.com/zooillogix/2008/08/come_one_come_all_zooillogix_p.php), [Craig](http://scienceblogs.com/deepseanews/about.php), [Janet](http://scienceblogs.com/ethicsandscience) and [Josh](http://scienceblogs.com/tfk/).  
**So again, 9 PM on Friday the 26th @ [Tonic](http://www.tonic-bar.com/)**
