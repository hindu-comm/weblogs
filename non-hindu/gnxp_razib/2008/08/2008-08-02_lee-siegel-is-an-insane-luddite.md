Lee Siegel is an insane Luddite

Listen to Lee Siegel, [Internet Reading: Speeding Us Up by Dumbing Us Down?](http://www.kcrw.com/news/programs/tp/tp080801internet_reading_spe). He’s positively unhinged, totally out of control. I don’t really mind Siegel, he obviously going to spend as much of his professional capital as possible attacking the internet after his own negative [self-inflicted experiences](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Lee_Siegel#The_New_Republic_controversy). Why is the media putting this guy on the phone? A 7 year old farting on air would be more informative and edifying. Siegel’s been all over the place basically claiming that the internet heralds the arrival of Gog and Magog. I really wouldn’t be too shocked if during the next interview Siegal pulls a young girl next to his phone and as she’s screaming he cuts her throat and murmurs to the interviewer that ***the internet did it, that the internet was responsible for it and that the internet has blood on its hands***.
