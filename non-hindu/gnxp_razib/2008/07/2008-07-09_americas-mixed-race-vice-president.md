America’s mixed-race Vice President

![charlescurtis.jpg](https://i0.wp.com/blogs.discovermagazine.com/gnxp/files/charlescurtis.jpg?resize=179%2C225)![charlescurtis.jpg](https://i0.wp.com/blogs.discovermagazine.com/gnxp/files/charlescurtis.jpg?resize=179%2C225)Most Americans are not aware that Herbert Hoover’s Vice President, [Charles Curtis](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Charles_Curtis), was 3/8 Native American and spent time on the Kaw reservation as a youth. He was also a Kansan. *NPR* has a [piece up](http://www.npr.org/templates/story/story.php?storyId=92301413&ft=1&f=1012) which looks back at this historical footnote.
