Why genome wide scans aren’t all that

Dan MacArthur at [Genetic Future](http://www.genetic-future.com/2008/03/why-do-genome-wide-scans-fail.html) [has the details](http://www.genetic-future.com/2008/03/why-do-genome-wide-scans-fail.html). Some of the stuff coming out of genomics reminds me a lot of what you see with social science; **lots** of sexy studies which turn out not to be as significant upon later analysis. Perhaps [hypotheses are overrated](https://www.gnxp.com/blog/2007/12/hypotheses-are-overrated.php)?
