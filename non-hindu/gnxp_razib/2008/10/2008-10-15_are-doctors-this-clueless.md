Are doctors this clueless?

I was browsing info on [Drugs.com](https://www.drugs.com/) for cholesterol meds after reading [Matt’s comment](http://js-kit.com/api/static/pop_comments?ref=http://gnxp.com&path=/6172575236033639278?url=http://www.gnxp.com/blog/2008/10/another-nobel-for-new-germ-theory-of.php&thetime=10/07/2008%2009:41:00%20PM#2614816) about them.. This sentence was somewhat amusing: “People of Asian descent may absorb Crestor at a higher rate than other people. **Make sure your doctor knows if you are Asian.** You may need a lower than normal starting dose.”

(before a humorless M.D. takes me to task, the title was just a joke)
