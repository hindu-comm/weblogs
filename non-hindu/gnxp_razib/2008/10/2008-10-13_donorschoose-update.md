DonorsChoose update

Just an update on the DonorsChoose drive for this year. I’ve removed some funded challenges from [my drive](http://www.donorschoose.org/donors/viewChallenge.html?id=18997&utm_source=BC08&utm_medium=widget&utm_content=GP), and [added a whole lot more](http://www.donorschoose.org/donors/viewChallenge.html?id=18997&utm_source=BC08&utm_medium=widget&utm_content=GP). In general they’re either bioscience related, or, they’re projects from really poor schools. This year I haven’t raised much money through my drive, though some of the [other ScienceBlogs](http://www.donorschoose.org/donors/leadershipboard.html?category=17) are doing really well on the [Leaderboard](http://www.donorschoose.org/donors/leadershipboard.html?category=17). Obviously “winning” isn’t that big of a deal here, but both years this weblog’s challenges have finished out really strong, so I’m not too worried….  
(shout out to those who gave early!)
