Islam in China

It’s a big blogosphere. [Aziz](http://blog.beliefnet.com/cityofbrass/) points me [Islam in China](http://islaminchina.wordpress.com/). They have an interesting post up which reiterates much of [what I said](https://www.gnxp.com/blog/2008/10/uyghurs-chinese-muslims-etc.php) about the [diversity of Chinese Muslims](http://islaminchina.wordpress.com/2008/09/11/ramadan-crackdown-the-use-of-terminology-chinese-muslim-vs-uiygar-vs-hui-vs-han-muslim/), along with obscure facts such as that the second ranking [Vice Premier](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Executive_Vice-Premier_of_the_State_Council) of China is [Hui](http://islaminchina.wordpress.com/2008/09/03/chinese-muslim-vice-premier-hui-liangyu/). Not that there’s anything wrong it….
