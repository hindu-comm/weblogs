Synteny – who is right?

PZ Myers outlines [synteny](http://scienceblogs.com/pharyngula/2008/06/basics_synteny.php). RPM says [he’s kind of wrong](http://scienceblogs.com/evolgen/2008/06/synteny_a_semantic_debate.php). Check out [the definition](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Synteny) in Wikipedia. Since RPM came down on me for confusion on this term I knew he would bring this up. I don’t really care much about which definition is “correct,” but I thought I’d point interested readers to the debate…..
