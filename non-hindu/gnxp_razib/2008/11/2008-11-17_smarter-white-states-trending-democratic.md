Smarter white states trending Democratic?

That’s what the [The Audacious Epigone](https://anepigone.blogspot.com/2008/11/white-iq-and-change-in-white-vote-by.html) concludes (based in part on a [post of mine](http://scienceblogs.com/gnxp/2008/11/the_great_white_sort.php)). This is a hobby-horse of [Half Sigma’s](http://www.halfsigma.com/). He’s not the only one who has observed the alliance between [Whiter People](http://www.openleft.com/showDiary.do?diaryId=9729) and minorities; roughly the highest and lowest socioeconomic strata. Historically and internationally this is not too surprising a coalition, though from what I recall during the French revolution the nobility and religious peasantry of the Vendee were allied on reactionary grounds against the radical bourgeoisie in the middle. Perhaps a better analog might be the relationship between the anti-clerical intelligentsia and proletariat against the traditionalist middle class in some European countries. Also, pooling the whole white population without regard to geography, the intelligence difference is [not large](http://scienceblogs.com/gnxp/2008/09/conservatives_are_as_smart_as.php), if it exists, when it comes to political ideology.
