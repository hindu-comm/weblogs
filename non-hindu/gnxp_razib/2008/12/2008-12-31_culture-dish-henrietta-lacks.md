Culture Dish & Henrietta Lacks

ScienceBlogs has a new contributor, [Rebecca Skloot](http://scienceblogs.com/culturedish/?utm_source=bloglist&utm_medium=dropdown) of [Culture Dish](http://scienceblogs.com/culturedish/?utm_source=bloglist&utm_medium=dropdown). The title is in part a reference to her new book, [The Immortal Life of Henrietta Lacks](http://home.earthlink.net/~rskloot/index_HeLa.htm). Looks interesting. If [Henrietta Lacks](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Henrietta_Lacks) doesn’t ring a bell, look her up, it’s rather freaky….
