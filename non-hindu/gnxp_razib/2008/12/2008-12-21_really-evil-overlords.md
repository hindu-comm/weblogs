Really Evil Overlords?

Check this out, [Matt Yglesias](http://yglesias.thinkprogress.org/archives/2008/12/the_new_moderate.php) was dissing on an obscure D.C. outfit, [Third Way](http://yglesias.thinkprogress.org/archives/2008/12/the_new_moderate.php), a few days ago. Today, the CEO of The Center For American Progress put up a [Very Special Post](http://yglesias.thinkprogress.org/archives/2008/12/a_special_note_re_third_way.php) on *his* weblog clarifying that CAP thinks much of Third Way. Creepy. I guess we always knew there was a puppet master back there, but it seems a bit unseemly to pull the strings so publicly.
