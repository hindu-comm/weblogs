Winds of Winter not likely in 2018

Martin Throws Even More Cold Water on Winds of Winter Dreams](https://www.vanityfair.com/hollywood/2018/02/george-r-r-martin-winds-of-winter-release-date-song-of-ice-and-fire-game-of-thrones).

Basically, it looks like he will come out with a different book first. It’s hard to imagine him squeezing out the next book in *A Song of Ice and Fire* before that in 2018.

There were two years between [A Game of Thrones](https://www.amazon.com/exec/obidos/ASIN/B00R6S0W84/geneexpressio-20/ref=as_at?creativeASIN=0306814803&linkCode=w61&imprToken=2V8mucmrP5k2pb2psPBQjg&slotNum=24) and [A Clash of Kings](https://www.amazon.com/exec/obidos/ASIN/0553579908/geneexpressio-20/ref=as_at?creativeASIN=0306814803&linkCode=w61&imprToken=2V8mucmrP5k2pb2psPBQjg&slotNum=24). Two years between [A Clash of Kings](https://www.amazon.com/exec/obidos/ASIN/0553579908/geneexpressio-20/ref=as_at?creativeASIN=0306814803&linkCode=w61&imprToken=2V8mucmrP5k2pb2psPBQjg&slotNum=24) and [A Storm of Swords](https://www.amazon.com/exec/obidos/ASIN/055357342X/geneexpressio-20/ref=as_at?creativeASIN=0306814803&linkCode=w61&imprToken=2V8mucmrP5k2pb2psPBQjg&slotNum=24). Five years between [A Storm of Swords](https://www.amazon.com/exec/obidos/ASIN/055357342X/geneexpressio-20/ref=as_at?creativeASIN=0306814803&linkCode=w61&imprToken=2V8mucmrP5k2pb2psPBQjg&slotNum=24) and [A Feast For Crows](https://www.amazon.com/exec/obidos/ASIN/055358202X/geneexpressio-20/ref=as_at?creativeASIN=0306814803&linkCode=w61&imprToken=2V8mucmrP5k2pb2psPBQjg&slotNum=24). Finally, six years between [A Feast For Crows](https://www.amazon.com/exec/obidos/ASIN/055358202X/geneexpressio-20/ref=as_at?creativeASIN=0306814803&linkCode=w61&imprToken=2V8mucmrP5k2pb2psPBQjg&slotNum=24) and [A Dance of Dragons](https://www.amazon.com/exec/obidos/ASIN/0553582011/geneexpressio-20/ref=as_at?creativeASIN=0306814803&linkCode=w61&imprToken=2V8mucmrP5k2pb2psPBQjg&slotNum=24).

If the next book was released now, it would be more than six years. It looks like we’ll go beyond seven years.

The trend is not promising. I’m pretty sure I’m not the only person who is, was (?), a big fan of the series to go through the [five stages of grief](https://grief.com/the-five-stages-of-grief/). It is what it is.
