Evolutionary imperialism

So there’s a slick new webzine coming out, [Evolution: this view of life](http://www.thisviewoflife.com/home-33133.php). It’s another one of [David Sloan Wilson’s](http://scienceblogs.com/evolution/) projects. I don’t agree a lot with the specifics of David’s theories, but I admire his ambition. [James Winters](http://www.replicatedtypo.com/) pointed me to the fact that [they’re trying to raise money](http://www.kickstarter.com/projects/1506603022/evolution-this-view-of-life-online-magazine) for this webzine via [KickStarter](http://www.kickstarter.com/projects/1506603022/evolution-this-view-of-life-online-magazine). Their goal is \$5,000. Having run much more bare bones [websites](https://www.gnxp.com/) for years this seems like a really modest amount in relation to their aims. I admire David’s attempts in this area enough that I gave some money. He tries a lot of things, many of which don’t succeed, but that’s science….
