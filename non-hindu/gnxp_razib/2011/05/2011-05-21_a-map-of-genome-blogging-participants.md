A map of genome blogging participants

Both [Eurogenes](https://bga101.blogspot.com/2011/05/eurogenes-bga-participant-map-plz-join.html) and [Harappa](http://www.harappadna.org/2011/05/harappa-participants-map/) now have map interfaces where you can drop in the origin of your location if you’re a participant. If you have submitted your data you should add your information in. We’re at a point where data is relatively plentiful, at least before the tsunami of whole genomes, so visualization and representation is of the essence.
