The Genetical Theory of Natural Selection

I flog R. A. Fisher’s [The Genetical Theory of Natural Selection](https://www.amazon.com/exec/obidos/ASIN/0198504403/geneexpressio-20) a fair amount on this site. You don’t need to understand everything in the book, nor do you have to agree with everything in it, but it is a great point of departure toward understanding evolutionary genetics. I’ve noted that you can get it free in [PDF format](http://blogs.discovermagazine.com/gnxp/2011/04/the-genetical-theory-of-natural-selection-free/).
