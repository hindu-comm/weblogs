Top 25 referral sites in 2011 to GNXP

A few qualifications. First, I removed all Google referral sites except for G+. Second, I removed [Discover Magazine](http://discovermagazine.com/) urls. Some of these sites should perhaps have been omitted from the list as well because of my past or current association with them ([gnxp.com](http://gnxp.com),[Secular Right](http://secularright.org),[Sepia Mutiny](http://sepiamutiny.com)and[Brown Pundits](http://brownpundits.com)).[ScienceBlogs](http://scienceblogs.com)is mostly, though not exclusively, from my old website there. I’m a little amused that[razib.com](http://razib.com)is rather high on the list, but that site is the first hit usually for querying my name on [Google](https://www.google.com/#hl=en&sugexp=pfwe&cp=6&gs_id=o&xhr=t&q=Razib+Khan&pf=p&sclient=psy-ab&site=&source=hp&pbx=1&oq=Razib+&aq=0&aqi=g4&aql=f&gs_sm=&gs_upl=&bav=on.2,or.r_gc.r_pw.,cf.osb&fp=4ff67c7544f25416&biw=1334&bih=643)(and therefore [Bing](https://www.bing.com/search?q=Razib+Khan&go=&qs=n&sk=&form=QBLH), which seems to just copy Google’s results).
