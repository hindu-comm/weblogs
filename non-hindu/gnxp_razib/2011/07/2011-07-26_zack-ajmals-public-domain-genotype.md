Zack Ajmal's public domain genotype

See his announcement: [Genome in the Wild](http://www.zackvision.com/weblog/2011/07/genome-in-the-wild/?utm_campaign=twitter&utm_medium=twitter&utm_source=twitter). If you don’t know, Zack is the driving force behind the [Harappa Ancestry Project](http://www.harappadna.org/). Seeing how the [Indian scientific-bureaucratic complex](http://www.harappadna.org/2011/06/changes-to-1000-genomes-south-asians/) still seems to be retarding rapid progress in human genomics (how many people have heard of the [Indian Genome Variation Consortium](http://www.igvdb.res.in/)? Their blog, which is hosted on blogspot, was last updated [1 year ago](https://igvbrowser.blogspot.com/)!), I think it can be argued that Zack has done more for the understanding of the population relationships of the Indian populations in the last 6 months than the Indian government has in 60 years! I’m hoping I’ll be proven wrong with a list of awesome and result rich publications in the comments.
