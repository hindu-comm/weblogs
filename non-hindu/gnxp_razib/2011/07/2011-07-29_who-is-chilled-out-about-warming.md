Who is chilled out about warming?

[Chris Mooney](http://blogs.discovermagazine.com/intersection/2011/07/29/cool-dudes-climate-denial-among-conservative-white-males/) pointed me to a [report on a study](http://bigthink.com/ideas/39500) which finds that white males are the most sanguine in relation to climate change. Unfortunately there wasn’t a link to the full report that I could see. But no worries, the [GSS](http://sda.berkeley.edu/cgi-bin/hsda?harcsda+gss10) added a variable, TEMPGEN1, which asks: “In general, do you think that a rise in the world’s temperature caused by climate change is….”

1 – Extremely dangerous for the environment  
2 – Very dangerous  
3 – Somewhat dangerous  
4 – Not very dangerous, or  
5 – Not dangerous at all for the environment?
