Goal met on the reader survey, now the data….

We’re [well north of 500](http://www.questionpro.com/akira/ShowResults?id=2462467) for the [reader survey](http://surveys.questionpro.com/akira/TakeSurvey?id=2462467). Thanks to everyone who participated. I’ll let it run until Saturday morning and then close it. I figure if you aren’t reading the blog at least once a week right now you’re not a core reader anyway (yes, I know that people get busy!). But I did promise to release the raw results at some point. I’m doing so now. It’s in [Google Docs](https://spreadsheets.google.com/spreadsheet/ccc?key=0AsTdg8RZEWOOdERtR2RGdlBrTHkxa2drOVB1SXpQSkE&hl=en_US).
