Subscribe to Nature for 1 year for $36?

I saw this on twitter and though I would pass it on. [A 1 year subscription to *Nature* print + online for \$36](https://secure.nature.com/store/svc/campaign?prod=NATURE&text=NA1107BA000&WT.mc_id=TWT_NatureNews). I have a lot of ambivalence about the way that some scientific publishers comport themselves, but this seems like a good deal, and I know many readers of this weblog don’t have academic access…

This is a screenshot of what I saw….
