My Big Five personality profile

The [Big Five personality](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Big_Five_personality_traits) traits are apparently much more scientifically accepted than the [Myers-Briggs](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Myers-Briggs_Type_Indicator) categories (to be more precise, the latter have no scientific support, while the former have some). So I just took a [Big Five](http://www.celebritytypes.com/big-five/test.php) test which matches you up with a particular president. I have no idea how it does this, **but I guessed ahead of time correctly which president would be my match**. I think long time readers of this blog will not be surprised by the fact that it suggests I have high extroversion and low agreeableness.
