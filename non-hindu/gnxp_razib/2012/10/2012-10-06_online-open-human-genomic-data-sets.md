Online open human genomic data sets

I had a friend recently email me about human genetic data sets. Some, like [POPRES](http://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pmc/articles/PMC2556436/), are restricted to researchers. But there are a lot of data available for the public. [Zack Ajmal](http://www.harappadna.org/category/dataset/) has posted on [most of them](http://www.harappadna.org/category/dataset/) at some point. Feel free to post links to others in the comments.
