Back to the 70s?

Peter Turchin has basically implied that it’s [1970 again](http://www.nature.com/news/human-cycles-history-as-science-1.11078), and we’re in for a new age of disturbance. I’m rather skeptical…but, today a co-worker pointed out that I have “70s hair.” My sideburns, yes, but that’s just Gen-X irony or whatever. But she argued that there was a notable pre-1980 shagginess to my hair. Off to the barbershop! And yet…look at the [mops that *One Direction* is sporting](http://sinchronicitymagazine.com/wp-content/uploads/2011/09/1-Direction-Judges-Houses-e1285963314678.jpg). When I last spoke about this group I wasn’t even aware that [the group is already big in the United States!](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/One_Direction#Up_All_Night_and_international_breakthrough_.282011.E2.80.9312.29)Am I old enough now that the styles of my pre-conscious age are now creeping back into fashion? Though I would still bet against polyester and plaid, perhaps David Frum’s unread [book about the 1970s](https://www.amazon.com/exec/obidos/ASIN/0465041965/geneexpressio-20) will start selling a few copies soon.
