Quantify 'Honey Boo Boo'?

As someone without a television I tend to be aware of only ***“cultural phenomenon”*** level shows. For example, [Jersey Shore](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Jersey_Shore_(TV_series)), and of course [South Park](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Sout_Park). The past week something has impinged upon my consciousness, [Honey Boo Boo](http://www.macon.com/2012/08/15/2136684/honey-boo-boo-tours-warner-robins.html). I am quite willing to cede ground to the [humanists](http://blogs.discovermagazine.com/gnxp/2012/08/the-art-of-science/) in analyzing this new cultural phenomenon. Of course there is the issue that the young [Sayyid Qutb-](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Sayyid_Qutb)‘s-to-be are watching this….
