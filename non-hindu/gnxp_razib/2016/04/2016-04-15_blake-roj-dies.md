Blake Roj dies

Long time readers will be aware that I’m a fan of [Blake’s 7](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Blake%27s_7). Or, more precisely, as a child of the 1980s this BBC science fiction show set in a dark dystopian future loomed large in my childhood because it presented a different vision of the future than I was used to. Sadly, the actor who played Blake Roj has died, [Gareth Thomas, Blake’s 7 actor, dies aged 71](http://www.bbc.com/news/entertainment-arts-36041534).
