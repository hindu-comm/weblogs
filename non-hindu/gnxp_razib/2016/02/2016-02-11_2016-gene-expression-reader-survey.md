2016 Gene Expression Reader Survey

I’ve been doing [reader surveys](http://www.unz.com/gnxp/?s=%22Reader+Survey%22&searchsubmit=Search&authors=razib-khan&ptype=gnxp) for a while, so I figured now was about time. For the demographic questions I tried to mimic the GSS more than usual. It’s two pages and 40 questions, but it should be pretty quick (it’s not a quiz, you shouldn’t have to think).

**[Here is the link](https://www.surveymonkey.com/r/VLTLZ2X)to the survey.**

There will be instant result updates, but after a few days I will post a link to the raw results on this blog post (I’ve turned off IP collecting by the way). Usually I get 300-400 responses, so I assume that’s the “core” readership. It’s been stable for a long while now.

You can use this as an “unlurk” thread if you want.

**Update:** You can [check the results](https://www.surveymonkey.com/results/SM-QWB3RYHQ/) as they get updated. I will put the raw results link up when there are more people who submit….
