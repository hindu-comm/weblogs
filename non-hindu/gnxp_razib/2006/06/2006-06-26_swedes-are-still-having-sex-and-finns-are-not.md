Swedes are still having sex, and Finns are not

Well, pretty soon [Craig Newmark’s](https://images.google.com/images?q=craig+newmark&hl=en&btnG=Search+Images) cherubic visage is going to be on Time. [Craig’s List](http://craigslist.org/) is expanding and taking over the world.

So, the numbers of “personals” posted in [Sweden’s Craig’s List](http://stockholm.craigslist.org/): 131. [Finland?](http://helsinki.craigslist.org/) 34. As for the debate about which is the “gay capital of Europe” between partisans of Stockholm & Helsinki:

Men seeking men in Stockholm: [13](http://stockholm.craigslist.org/m4m/)  
Men seeking men in Helsinki: [1](http://helsinki.craigslist.org/m4m/175196641.html) (and I think this is a bisexual individual?)

Are Finns even terrified of the internet? It seems that they are proto-[Solarians](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Solaria).

(note, Finland’s total fertility rate is a bit higher than Sweden’s).
