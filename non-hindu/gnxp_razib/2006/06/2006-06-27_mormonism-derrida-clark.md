Mormonism + Derrida = Clark

While I’m recommending weblogs, I thought I’d point readers to Clark Gobles’ [Mormon Philosophy & Theology](http://www.libertypages.com/clark/). Now, I know that most readers here aren’t religious, and there is probably a mildly anti-philosophical bias as well, but, if you want to sample an “[Intermontane](http://dictionary.reference.com/search?r=2&q=Intermontane)” flavor of Derrida discourse, Clark is your man. Think of him as the Napoleon Dynamite of philosophy and cognitive psychology.
