Derbyshire stirring some serious shit

The title says it all. If you haven’t, you should check out [A Frigid and Pitiless Dogma](http://www.newenglishreview.org/custpage.cfm?frm=3190&sec_id=3190), John Derbyshire’s review of [A Party of Death](https://www.amazon.com/exec/obidos/ASIN/1596980044/geneexpressio-20/102-9856414-3062510) in [The New English Review](http://www.newenglishreview.org/). Excepts do not suffice, just be aware that Derb has the rusty knife out and dogmas and cults are out of luck (there is a reason that National Review is not going to publicize this work of commentary). Pity the brown man, for Derb has taken up the white man’s skeptical burden.
