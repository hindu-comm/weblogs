Alzheimer's a balanced polymorphism?

Alzheimer’s = Sickle Cell. Severe Childhood Diarrhea = Malaria. Here’s an [older paper](http://www.pedresearch.org/cgi/content/full/57/2/310) describing the association. The [new report](http://www.eurekalert.org/pub_releases/2006-11/uovh-uf111006.php) with mice isn’t out yet.
