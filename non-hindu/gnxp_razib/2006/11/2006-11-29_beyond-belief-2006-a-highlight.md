Beyond Belief 2006 – a highlight

Been watching [Beyond Belief 2006](http://beyondbelief2006.org/Watch/). Funniest moment so far, V.S. Ramachandran recounts the % of people in a survey who considered themselves “above average” in intelligence. Take a guess. Answer below the fold.

98% of people surveyed (representative survey mind you!) considered themselves above average in intelligence.
