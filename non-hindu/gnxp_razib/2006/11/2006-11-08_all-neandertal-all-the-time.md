All Neandertal all the time…..

I’m [posting](http://scienceblogs.com/gnxp/) a [crap load](http://scienceblogs.com/gnxp/) of Neandertal related junk over at my other weblog (more scheduled). I wonder if [John](http://johnhawks.net/weblog/) is going to keep it up. As I note on my other blog two popular articles on the Paabo group’s work just came out, which makes me suspicious re: timing. I would keep track of google news, [query Neandertal](https://news.google.com/news?hl=en&ned=us&q=neandertal&btnG=Search+News) and [MCPH1](https://news.google.com/news?hl=en&ned=us&ie=UTF-8&q=MCPH1). Even [Slashdot](http://science.slashdot.org/article.pl?sid=06/11/08/2316217) got in on the action. I’d also track technorati for [Neandertal](http://technorati.com/search/neandertal?language=en&authority=a1) and [MCPH1](http://technorati.com/search/mcph1). And for what it’s worth, I’m guessing that the “living Neandertals” are on the Celtic Fringe.
