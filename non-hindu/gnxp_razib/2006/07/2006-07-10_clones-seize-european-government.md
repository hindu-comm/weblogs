Clones seize European government

This is somewhat *Onion* worthy, but it happens to be true: [identical twins now run Poland](http://www.washingtonpost.com/wp-dyn/content/article/2006/07/08/AR2006070800992.html). Specifically, *both* the legislative (Prime Minister) and executive branches (President) are under the control of a of monozygotic twin (via [ParaPundit](http://parapundit.com/)).
