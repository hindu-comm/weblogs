Islam & Creationism & Evolution

*The Guardian* has a [long piece up](http://commentisfree.guardian.co.uk/inayat_bunglawala/2006/07/darwinism_muslim_scientists_ha.html) by a Muslim who has wrestled with the issue of reconciling his faith with evolutionary theory. The author is a secretary for [The Muslim Council of Britain](http://www.mcb.org.uk/), an organization criticized for its illiberal tendencies (e.g., rejection of the participation of homosexual Muslim groups under their umbrella group). The interesting point, for me, is that the author alludes to the evolution of Muslims themselves, as they encounter evolutionary biology with their own pre-scientific models of the universe.
