Critique of DNA testing makes it big time

[John Hawks](http://www.slate.com/id/2138059) has a [piece](http://www.slate.com/id/2138059) in *Slate* critiquing the recent scientific genealogy trend. Congrats for John for going “mainstream,” and kudos for *Slate* to contributing something substantive to the discourse. And I’ve noted before, most of the people taking the tests won’t find out anything they don’t know….
