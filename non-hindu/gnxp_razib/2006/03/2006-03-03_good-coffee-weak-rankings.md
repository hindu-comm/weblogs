Good coffee, weak rankings?

*USA Today* has [coffee shop rankings](http://www.usatoday.com/travel/destinations/10great/2006-03-02-coffeeshops_x.htm) up. I’ve been to two on the list, [Stumptown](http://stumptowncoffee.com/cafes/division.html) in Portland, and [Caffe Dante](http://newyork.citysearch.com/profile/7109271?cslink=search_name_noncust&ulink=search__searchslot2_520__0_profile_2_1) in Greenwich Village. All I’ve got to say is that Caffe Dante is ***weak*** compared to Stumptown’s muddy blend. They should have kept [World Cup](http://www.worldcupcoffee.com/) on the list. Frankly, Starbucks was the best coffee I found in New York. Pacific Northwest representing!
