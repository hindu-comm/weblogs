Jan-Michael Vincent, lost Finnish orphan?

[](https://www.gnxp.com/blog/uploaded_images/salonen1_b-736135.jpg)  
Is washed up 80s TV star [Jan-Michael Vincent](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Jan-Michael_Vincent) a Finnish adoptee? Note the similarity in appearance to “famous” Finn, [Esa-Pekka Salonen](http://virtual.finland.fi/netcomm/news/showarticle.asp?intNWSAID=26174). Suspiciously they both have hyphenated first names, and likely have had “grandfathers” who were made into cuckolds by Chinese sailors. Additionally, Vincent is a known drunk, a vice that Finns are all too familiar with. Raised by non-Suomi parents Vincent’s genotype was subject to a different [norm of reaction](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Norm_of_reaction) than might be have been the case in Finland. With its suffocatingly introverted and maladjusted culture Vincent might have flourished as a withdrawn curiosity. As it is, in the glamor seeking non-Suomi cultural milieu of the United States he withered under a harsh glare which evolution in the cool northern climes did not prepare him for….
