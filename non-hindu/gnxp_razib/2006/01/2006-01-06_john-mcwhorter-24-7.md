John McWhorter 24-7

[John McWhorter](http://www.manhattan-institute.org/html/mcwhorter.htm) has a new book out, [Winning the Race](https://www.amazon.com/exec/obidos/ASIN/1592401880/geneexpressio-20), so he’s making the media rounds. He was just on [Talk of the Nation](http://www.npr.org/templates/story/story.php?storyId=5079499), [News & Notes with Ed Gordon](http://www.npr.org/templates/story/story.php?storyId=5080678) and [On Point](http://www.onpointradio.org/shows/2006/01/20060102_b_main.asp). Keep an eye (and ear) out for him.
