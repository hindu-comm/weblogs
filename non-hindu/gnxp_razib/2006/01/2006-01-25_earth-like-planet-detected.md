Earth like planet detected?

Since [Chad Orzel](http://scienceblogs.com/principles/) hasn’t posted on it, I figured I’d link to this [story](http://www.nature.com/news/2006/060123/full/060123-5.html) about a *Nature* [letter](http://www.nature.com/nature/journal/v439/n7075/abs/nature04441.html) which announces the discovery of a planet 5.5 times as massive as our own! This is pretty cool, I was a big fan of astronomy when I was a kid, and it certainly is the science with tickles my “shock & awe”-o-meter. But combined with the possibility of sub-thousand dollar genomes in the next 5 years, it really does make me feel like we are at the End of Times and soon we shall be as gods. OK, that was a hyperbole, but I can dimly grok the difficulty of a extrasolar detection feat like this….  
**Update:** [Chad comments copiously now](http://scienceblogs.com/principles/2006/01/a_new_life_awaits_you_in_the_o.php).
