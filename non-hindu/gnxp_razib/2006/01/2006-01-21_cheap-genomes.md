Cheap genomes

*The Harvard Crimson* [reports](http://www.thecrimson.com/article.aspx?ref=510899) on [George Church’s](http://arep.med.harvard.edu/gmc/) attempt to develop super-cheap genomic sequencing, though this time he’s giving a \$10-20,000 price point quote instead of [\$1,000](http://www.boston.com/news/globe/magazine/articles/2006/01/08/1000_for_your_genome/). *Scientific American* has a [subscription only piece](http://www.sciam.com/article.cfm?chanID=sa006&colID=1&articleID=00046D59-19C0-13A7-99C083414B7F0124).
