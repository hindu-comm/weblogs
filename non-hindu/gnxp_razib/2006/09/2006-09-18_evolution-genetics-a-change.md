Evolution + genetics … a change

Not much on evolution and genetics of late…well, sometimes life goes in other directions. But, I though I’d point you to JP over at [my other blog](https://www.gnxp.com/blog/2006/09/humans-nothin-special.php) [commenting](https://www.gnxp.com/blog/2006/09/humans-nothin-special.php) on a paper where the author dismisses the rate of human genomic evolution next to his vaunted *Drosophila* (the full paper can be found in the links over there). RPM [responds](http://scienceblogs.com/evolgen/2006/09/adaptive_evolution_in_genomes.php) over at [his blog](http://scienceblogs.com/evolgen/2006/09/adaptive_evolution_in_genomes.php). The [comment boards](http://www.haloscan.com/comments/raldanash/115854136034965640?url=http://www.gnxp.com/blog/2006/09/humans-nothin-special.php) on my other blog have been hoppin’, with RPM being the Drosophile, and JP making the case that *H. sapiens* is the next big model organism. Scroll down and you’ll see the [Allen Iverson](http://www.kidzworld.com/site/p1419.htm).’  
I just got [Evolutionary Genetics: Concepts & Case Studies](https://www.amazon.com/exec/obidos/ASIN/0195168186/geneexpressio-20) in the mail, so except some evo-gen fun in the next few weeks….
