Adapting Minds, David J. Buller

[Chris of Mixing Memory](http://scienceblogs.com/mixingmemory/2006/09/a_review_of_buller_on_ep.php) points me to this [very interesting review](http://www.pitt.edu/%7Emachery/papers/debunking%20Adapting%20Minds%20Machery%20Barrett.pdf) \[PDF\] (demolition to be honest) of David J. Buller’s [Adapting Minds](https://www.amazon.com/exec/obidos/ASIN/0262025795/geneexpressio-2), a critique of Evolutionary Psychology which made [some waves](https://isteve.blogspot.com/2005/05/is-sharon-begley-as-dim-as-she-sounds.html) last year. Even if you haven’t read Buller’s book, the review will be of interest as it covers a lot of ground.
