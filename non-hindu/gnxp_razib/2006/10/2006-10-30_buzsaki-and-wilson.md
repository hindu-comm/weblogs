Buzsaki and Wilson

For the die-hards. I taped the Buzsaki and Wilson lectures at the Visualizing and Recording Large-Scale Ensembles short course at SFN. Quality isn’t really great and a lot of it doesn’t make sense without the slides, but, hey, it’s there if you want it:  
[  
Buzsaki Intro and Lecture](http://www.mccoffeemug.com/lectures/Buzsaki%20Intro%20and%20Lecture.mp3) (mostly about multi-unit recording and unit isolation)  
[Wilson Lecture](http://www.mccoffeemug.com/lectures/Wilson%20Lecture.mp3) (more about the same, but focus on tetrodes and some data)  
[Buzsaki vs. Wilson](http://www.mccoffeemug.com/lectures/Wilson%20and%20Buzsaki%20discussion.mp3) (Breakout group with some questions, interesting to hear them converse and joke. Best sound quality I think.)
