Impending Tripoli Six trial

It’s been a few weeks since I posted on the Tripoli Six, but while I’ve been busy with other things there are still six lives in the balance. [Revere](http://scienceblogs.com/effectmeasure/2006/10/tripoli_six_campaigns_new_and.php) has [more details](http://scienceblogs.com/effectmeasure/2006/10/tripoli_six_campaigns_new_and.php), and Janet has a [long post](http://scienceblogs.com/ethicsandscience/) on the [importance of writing letters](http://scienceblogs.com/ethicsandscience/). The trial starts October 31st, so let’s raise some consciousness as a lead up!
