I’m gettin’ a little air time

About 1/4 of the way into the *Radio Open Source* [The Al-Jazeera Effect](http://www.radioopensource.org/the-al-jazeera-effect/) episode Brendan mentions [my comments](http://www.radioopensource.org/the-genetics-of-genealogy/) regarding the science of genealogy (he notes that I’m “producing” their show for them). I just talked to [David](http://www.radioopensource.org/author/david/), the staffer that started that thread, this afternoon, and we bounced some ideas off each other. So, the moral is that blogging gets results!  
In related news, Dienekes has a [review](https://dienekes.blogspot.com/2006/02/more-african-american-lives.html) of some of the findings in [African American Lives](http://www.pbs.org/wnet/aalives/).
