Foreskin: hot or not?

John Hawks [casts a skeptical eye](http://johnhawks.net/weblog/reviews/health/pathogens/hiv_circumcision_cyberchase_2006.html) on the “circumcision reduces HIV transmission” [story](http://www.nytimes.com/2006/12/14/health/14hiv.html?em&ex=1166245200&en=0cdfbc3e4f64cfa1&ei=5087%0A). Think how embarrassing it would be if, under pressure from Western governments, African nations start chopping off foreskins willy-nilly only later to find, well, hm, maybe it’s not that effective after all. The outrage that would result from that is in itself reason enough to be careful about rushing into things.
