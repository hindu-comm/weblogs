Off with her head!

I stand with the other science bloggers in encouraging everyone to do what they can to oust the Creationist on the Ohio School Board. More from [Ed Brayton](http://scienceblogs.com/dispatches/2006/08/help_defeat_a_creationist_in_o.php), [Chad](http://scienceblogs.com/principles/2006/08/get_out_the_vote_in_ohio.php), [John](http://scienceblogs.com/strangerfruit/2006/08/what_you_can_do_ohio_edition.php), [Bora](http://scienceblogs.com/clock/2006/08/call_for_action.php), [Kevin](http://scienceblogs.com/bushwells/2006/08/we_fink_not.php) and [Tara](http://scienceblogs.com/aetiology/2006/08/ohio_is_not_kansas.php). If you lose enough small battles the big war is lost.
