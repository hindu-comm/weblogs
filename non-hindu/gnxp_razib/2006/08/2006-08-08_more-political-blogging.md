More political blogging….

More [political blogging](https://dean2004.blogspot.com/2006/08/wow-im-political-blogger.html) from me over at Neo-neo-centrist weblog [Nation Building](https://dean2004.blogspot.com/2006/08/wow-im-political-blogger.html) on [political](https://dean2004.blogspot.com/2006/08/wow-im-political-blogger.html) & [foreign](https://dean2004.blogspot.com/2006/08/wrong-reason.html) policy issues. The short form is this: I am fast becoming convinced that many “hawks” are coming to resemble the child who beats up the nearest warm human body after being hurt from falling off a bike. From the Left every citizen born at home is equally capable of being a college graduate, while from the Right every enemy abroad is the potential seed for [Götterdämmerung](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Ragnarok). I am haunted by the specter of looming potentialities never realized….
