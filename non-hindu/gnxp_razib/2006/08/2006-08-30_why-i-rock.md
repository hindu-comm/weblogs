Why I rock

![Habanero.jpg](https://i0.wp.com/blogs.discovermagazine.com/gnxp/files/2006/08/Habanero.jpg?resize=350%2C234)![Habanero.jpg](https://i0.wp.com/blogs.discovermagazine.com/gnxp/files/2006/08/Habanero.jpg?resize=350%2C234)To the right you see a [habanero pepper](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Habanero), [100-350 K Scoville units](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Scoville_scale#List_of_Scoville_ratings) (Jalapeno is 2.5-8 K). I can eat **2 habeneros in one sitting and *enjoy* it**. And what does not kill you can [cure cancer](http://www.hotsauceblog.com/hotsaucearchives/hot-pepper-kills-prostate-cancer-cells-in-study/). Hot dog! On the other hand, if I want something which is a little less spicey and has a more tangy, aromatic flavor, I really enjoy *green* [Thai peppers](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Thai_pepper). I’m the *spicey* *ScienceBlogger*.
