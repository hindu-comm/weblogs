The New Yorker on Poincare Conjecture

[The New Yorker](http://www.newyorker.com/fact/content/articles/060828fa_fact2) has an interesting piece up about the characters involved in the [Poincare Conjecture](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Poincar%C3%A9_conjecture). I have to say, [Grigori Perelman](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Grigori_Perelman) seems out of central casting for [Simon Baron-Cohen](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Simon_Baron_Cohen).
