Save a brown baby

[](https://www.gnxp.com/blog/uploaded_images/header-773346.jpg)The cute little girl to the left has *Acute Lymphoblastic Leukemia*. She needs a bone marrow donor. She’s brown (obviously). I know I have many brown readers, some of you live near [donor drive locations](http://savenirali.com/nirali/Main/UpcomingDrives). If you read this blog, you also know that for those of us from small racial minorities our genetic distinctiveness can be a death warrant. Change occurs one step at a time. (via [Sepia Mutinty](http://www.sepiamutiny.com/sepia/archives/003718.html), [Nirali’s website](http://savenirali.com/nirali/))
