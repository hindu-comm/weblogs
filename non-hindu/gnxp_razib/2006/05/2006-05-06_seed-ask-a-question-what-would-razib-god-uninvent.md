SEED “Ask a question,” what would Razib-God uninvent

*Seed* is asking which invention I’d uninvent. My *reflexive* response is atomic **weapons**. I want to emphasize **weapons** because I think nuclear energy is going to be important, just as [James Lovelock](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/James_Lovelock#Nuclear_Power) does. But if I was God who could change the world by fiat, well, atomic weapons would probably be it. Now, I say this with the convinction that I suspect more lives would be lost between 1945-2000 than in the “atomic age.” I think convential wars would be more brutal and more frequent than under the specter of the nuclear apocolypse. But, I suspect in my lifetime some idiotic small banana republic will blow up another worthless banana republic, and that’s going to cause problems.  
But since [Evolgen](http://scienceblogs.com/evolgen/2006/05/seed_asks_i_answer.php) already said nuclear weapons, I will say that television is my runner up.
