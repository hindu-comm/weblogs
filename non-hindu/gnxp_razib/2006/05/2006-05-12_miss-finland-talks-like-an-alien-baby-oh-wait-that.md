Miss Finland talks like an alien baby!!! (oh wait, that's just suomen kieli)

Watch Miss Finland, Ninni Laaksonen, talk like a [freaky alienoid infant](http://www.seiska.fi/huhhuh/seiskatv/_a25789/miss+suomi+ninni+laaksonen/). Here are some pics of her [non-earthling self](http://www.mtv3.fi/ohjelmat/misssuomi2005/finalistit.shtml?345233). Oh, and the new [Miss Sweden](http://www.teaterhogskolan.se/CV/CVutskrift.asp?Namn=Josephine%20Alhanko) looks like a depigmented [Jessica Alba](https://www.imdb.com/gallery/granitz/4691/Events/4691/JessicaAlb_Devan_8464671_400.jpg?path=pgallery&path_key=Alba,%20Jessica) (who is 1/4 Danish) to me.

Update: It has been brought to my attention that 1) Miss Finland might be of Swedish ethnic origin (apparently the interviewer asks Miss Finland if it is an advantage to be a Swedish Finn in the competition), and Miss Sweden is probably a 1/4 each: Finnish & Russian.
