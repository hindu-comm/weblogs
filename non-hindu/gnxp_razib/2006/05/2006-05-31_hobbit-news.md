One point that is really confusing about the Hobbits is their small brain size. Consider the graph to the left.[](https://www.gnxp.com/blog/uploaded_images/brainsize-754597.gif) Human (hominids) brains keep getting larger, come rain or shine. The Hobbits, if they truly are what is claimed, radically reversed this trend. Convential explanations of island ecology seem a bit weak when set against this almost inevitable encephalization that seems to have characterized our lineage.
