Sunday Times

A couple of items of interest from today’s London Sunday Times.

First is this front page [story](http://www.timesonline.co.uk/article/0,,2087-1572533,00.html) on the level of illegal immigrants in the UK, allegedly estimated by Home Office officials at around 500,000.

Second, an interesting feature [article](http://www.timesonline.co.uk/article/0,,2092-1572031,00.html) (book extract) by Tarquin Hall on the immigrant ‘community’ in East London. (Read it and you’ll see why I put quotes round ‘community’.) The article reads not unlike a spoof by Sacha Baron-Cohen, but it is, apparently, genuine.

(Added) …and [here’s](http://www.timesonline.co.uk/article/0,,2092-1572239,00.html) another extract from Tarquin Hall’s book, that I thought was part of the same article, but has a separate URL.
