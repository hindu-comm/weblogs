Kapu: Charles Murray on Race, Sex, and Intelligence

I’m off the computer until this evening, but *Commentary* has Charles Murray’s September issue article [*The Inequality Taboo*](http://www.commentarymagazine.com/production/files/murray0905.html) up on their website now, available for reading.

**Update from Dobeln:** The most interesting thing about this essay is not the text itself – it’s a wrap-up, with no new revelations. Rather, it’s the fact that it got linked by blogging powerhouse [Instapundit](http://instapundit.com/archives/week_2005_08_21.php). This in turn indicates that the Instapundit is a card-carrying member of [“the stealth consensus”](https://www.gnxp.com/MT2/archives/002424.html). Something tells me unorthodox ideas will be harder to squelch in the Internet era…
