Neets News

The Neets phenomenon (see my post below) seems to be catching on.

Today’s London Daily Mirror has a follow-up to the Sunday Times article [here](http://www.mirror.co.uk/news/allnews/tm_objectid=15342528&method=full&siteid=50143&headline=meet-the-neets--that-s-not-in-education--employment-or-training-name_page.html).

More surprisingly, the same phenomenon is causing concern in Japan, of all places – see [this](http://www.asianews.it/view.php?l=en&art=2849) article.

But I guess that both the nature and the scale of the problem are different from in Britain.

Addendum

This Guardian [article](http://education.guardian.co.uk/egweekly/story/0,5500,1340813,00.html) from last year is mainly about Japanese Neets. Not much in common with the British phenomenon.
