Arthur Jensen (in his own words): A Matter of Genes

![](https://i0.wp.com/www.isironline.org/photos/2004/conference/images/ISIR42.jpg?w=640)![](https://i0.wp.com/www.isironline.org/photos/2004/conference/images/ISIR42.jpg?w=640)  
\[pictured with [Linda Gottfredson](http://www.udel.edu/educ/gottfredson/) in 2004; photo courtesy of [ISIR](http://www.isironline.org/) via [Jonathan Wai](http://www.vanderbilt.edu/Peabody/SMPY/jonathan_wai.htm)\]

For those who have not had the opportunity to hear [Dr. Arthur Jensen](http://www.individualdifferences.info/LondonJensen.htm) in person, I have uploaded a brief interview he did circa 1971 with the [Center for the Study of Democratic Institutions](http://www.csdi.org/) 1. The interview is entitled A Matter of Genes and he gives his own (rather non-controversial, by today’s standards) positions w.r.t his 1969 [HER](http://www.gse.harvard.edu/~hepg/her.html) and related articles.

Here is the [link to the file](http://65.254.63.127/alex/JensenGenes.mov) (a \~ 30 minute, 80 MB .mov, which means you’ll either have to download [Quicktime](http://www.apple.com/quicktime/download/win.html) or do a conversion to a different format).

\[1\] If that link doesn’t work, you can get a feel for them by looking at their [NPQ publication](http://digitalnpq.org/).
