Hotties gotz hormones….

This paper, [Facial appearance is a cue to oestrogen levels in women](http://www.journals.royalsoc.ac.uk/link.asp?id=l1v7h163455t8732), is getting a lot of [press right now](http://news.independent.co.uk/world/science_technology/article324101.ece). This tells us nothing new, there have been many, many, studies which show that there is a positive correlation between elevated estrogen levels and feminine (i.e., attractive) features. But check out the photos, isn’t it striking how shaving a millimeter here and there can change things?
