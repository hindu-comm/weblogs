Nerd alert!

Oracle and PHP in the [same sentence](http://news.com.com/2102-1007_3-5705448.html?tag=st.util.print)? PHP has seen some growth over the past few years (see [here](http://www.tiobe.com/tpci.htm) or [here](http://www.cs.berkeley.edu/~flab/languages.html)), but security is always a headache.
