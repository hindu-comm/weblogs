Unhiding the embryo

[Reacting to this](http://corner.nationalreview.com/05_12_11_corner-archive.asp#085016), I felt the need to point out that this is what a **blastocyst** looks like:

[](https://upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/en/9/93/Blastocyst.jpg)

Those round things are individual cells. The zona pellucida is the human equivalent of an egg shell. The inner cell mass is the important part.
