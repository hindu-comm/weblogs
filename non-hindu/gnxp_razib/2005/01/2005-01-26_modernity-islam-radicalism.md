Modernity + Islam ~ Radicalism???

[Fundamentalism begins at home](http://www.spiked-online.com/Printable/0000000CA816.htm) is an article which reviews the theories of a French sociologist. I don’t buy the whole thesis, primarily because there is so much complexity in decomposing a construct like “Islamic fundamentalism.” Nevertheless I think it is a step forward to acknowledge that “religions” or “ways of life” do not simply flow transparently from injunctions that exist in a holy book, rather, they are surface manifestations of a dynamic process of interpretation and reformulation shaped by many interacting variables in a constant state of flux. That is, Islamic fundamentalism is simply a [metastable](http://dictionary.reference.com/search?q=metastable) condition.
