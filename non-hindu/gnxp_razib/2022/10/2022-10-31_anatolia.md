Anatolia!

In case you haven’t seen, in October I posted three essays and one podcast on Anatolia’s history and genetics:

1\. [Ararat’s long shadow: Asia Minor’s major impact on humanity](https://razib.substack.com/p/ararats-long-shadow-asia-minors-major)  
2. [Hittite Words, Byzantine Walls: what the West as we know it owes Anatolia’s empires](https://razib.substack.com/p/hittite-words-byzantine-walls-what)  
3. [The Turkification of Anatolia: tales of Rome’s last conquerors](https://razib.substack.com/p/hittite-words-byzantine-walls-what)

And the podcast, [Anatolia over 10,000 years – From first farmers to the Turks](https://razib.substack.com/p/anatolia-over-10000-years#details).
