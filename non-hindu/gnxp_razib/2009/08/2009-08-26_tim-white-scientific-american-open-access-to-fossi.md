Tim White, Scientific American, open access to fossils

[Kambiz Kamrani](http://anthropology.net/2009/08/25/science-suffers-from-the-idiots-at-scientific-american/) of [Anthropology](http://anthropology.net/2009/08/25/science-suffers-from-the-idiots-at-scientific-american), normally a rather staid blogger, has posted something titled [Science Suffers From The Idiots At Scientific American](http://anthropology.net/2009/08/25/science-suffers-from-the-idiots-at-scientific-american/). It’s in reference to this widely circulated editorial, [Fossils for All: Science Suffers by Hoarding](http://www.scientificamerican.com/article.cfm?id=fossils-for-all). I can’t really summarize it, and I think the title certainly does invite you to [read the whole post at Anthropology.net](http://anthropology.net/2009/08/25/science-suffers-from-the-idiots-at-scientific-american)
