Inequality & wealth

[A review over at ScienceBlogs](http://scienceblogs.com/gnxp/2009/11/to_crush_your_enemies_and_stea.php) of a new paper, [Intergenerational Wealth Transmission and the Dynamics of Inequality in Small-Scale Societies](http://www.sciencemag.org/cgi/content/abstract/326/5953/682). Iâ€™m going to comment more in the near future, as I think this an give us insight into [historical dynamics](https://www.google.com/search?ie=UTF-8&oe=UTF-8&q=historical+dynamics&btnG=With+Google&domains=gnxp.com&sitesearch=gnxp.com). An interesting find is that pastoralists and settled agriculturalists exhibit the same levels of heritability of material wealth (as well as the same values on material wealth). Hunter-gatherers and slash & burn agriculturalists seem to be at the other end of the spectrum.
