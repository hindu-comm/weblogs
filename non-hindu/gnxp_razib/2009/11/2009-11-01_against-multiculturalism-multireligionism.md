Against multiculturalism (multireligionism)

I rarely post much political commentary here, because it would add little value as I have nothing distinctive to say in that domain. At [Secular Right](http://secularright.org/) I am wont to do data analysis because I think data is something that needs to be injected into political discussions and commentary. But in any case, today I [put up an essay](http://secularright.org/wordpress/?p=3123), [Religious diversity & its discontents](http://secularright.org/wordpress/?p=3123). In it I make clear my distaste for multiculturalism, so if you are a reader who would find such opinions to *your* distaste, I invite you not to click! My own opinion is that multiculturalism as it is presented is not a noble lie, but a silly one.
