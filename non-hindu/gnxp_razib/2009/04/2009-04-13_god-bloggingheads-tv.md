God & Bloggingheads.tv

Two highly recommended Bloggingheads.tv below the fold on religion. First, the cognitive science of religion in [Why Are We Religious?](http://bloggingheads.tv/diavlogs/18974), and second religion & conservatism in [God and Man on the Right](http://bloggingheads.tv/diavlogs/18973). My co-blogger at [Secular Right](http://secularright.org/), Heather Mac Donald, is getting into it with a future columnist for *The New York Times*.
