Swine flu…2009 as annus horribilis?

See [FuturePundit](http://www.futurepundit.com/archives/006155.html) & [Effect Measure](http://scienceblogs.com/effectmeasure/). Also see [H5N1](http://crofsblogs.typepad.com/h5n1/). CDC recommends (especially for residents of California & Texas):

  
\* Cover your nose and mouth with a tissue when you cough or sneeze. Throw the tissue in the trash after you use it.  
\* Wash your hands often with soap and water, especially after you cough or sneeze. Alcohol-based hands cleaners are also effective.  
\* Try to avoid close contact with sick people.  
\* If you get sick, CDC recommends that you stay home from work or school and limit contact with others to keep from infecting them.  
\* Avoid touching your eyes, nose or mouth. Germs spread that way.
