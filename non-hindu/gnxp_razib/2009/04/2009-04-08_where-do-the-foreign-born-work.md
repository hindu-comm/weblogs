Where do the foreign born work?

*The New York Times* has an awesome [interactive map](http://www.nytimes.com/interactive/2009/04/07/us/20090407-immigration-occupation.html#view=all) which shows which sectors the foreign born from various countries are concentrated. Nothing too surprising, but nice to see it quantitatively displayed. Below the fold screenshots from “Computer software developers” and “Skilled construction workers.”
