Parents don't matter as much as you think….

[Jonah Lehrer](http://scienceblogs.com/cortex/) [interviews](http://www.sciam.com/article.cfm?id=parents-peers-children) [Judith Rich Harris](https://www.gnxp.com/blog/2006/01/10-questions-for-judith-rich-harris.php) on the topic of [The Nurture Assumption](https://www.amazon.com/exec/obidos/ASIN/0684857073/geneexpressio-20). Related but coincidently I [pointed to new research](http://scienceblogs.com/gnxp/2009/04/adoptive_parents_invest_more_g.php) on the investment and returns which adoptive parents get, and how the results are not surprising in a behavior genetic context.
