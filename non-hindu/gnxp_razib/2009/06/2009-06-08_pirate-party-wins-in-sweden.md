Pirate Party wins in Sweden!

It looks like the [PirateParty](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Pirate_Party#2009_European_Parliament_election) will gain 1 seat in the EU parliament. This means [Christian Engström](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Christian_Engström) will have a job and income. He’s been spending the last 3 years as an activist living off his savings.
