10,000 Year Explosion & bloggingheads.tv

You can watch me talk to Greg Cochran about his book [The 10,000 Year Explosion](https://www.amazon.com/exec/obidos/ASIN/0465002218/geneexpressio-20/) on bloggingheads.tv this weekend….

There’s something interesting about the front page of bloggingheads.tv right now.

  
![browndueonright.jpg](https://i0.wp.com/blogs.discovermagazine.com/gnxp/files/browndueonright.jpg?resize=442%2C520)![browndueonright.jpg](https://i0.wp.com/blogs.discovermagazine.com/gnxp/files/browndueonright.jpg?resize=442%2C520)

Check it. Not only are the two colored people on the front not talking to each other (yeah, I’m talking about the Glenn & John duo), but both happen to be secular Bangladeshi American Republican males. Who have met each other to boot. Conspiracy? Or coincidence? You decide.
