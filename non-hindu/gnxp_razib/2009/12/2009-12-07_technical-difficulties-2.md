Technical difficulties

There were some difficulties with the site overnight. Probably best place to check for updates is my [twitter feed](https://twitter.com/razibkhan), [http://twitter.com/razibkhan](https://twitter.com/razibkhan). 99% of the stuff there are just re-posts of my blog content. If you don’t have my email address, you can also contact me [http://razib.com](http://razib.com/). Additionally, I set up an [aggregator weblog](http://www.razib.com/wordpress/) a few weeks ago, which is basically all the stuff from GNXP.COM, ScienceBlogs GNXP and my posts from Secular Right (just a feed of the “David Hume” author archives).
