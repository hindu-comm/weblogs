WolframAlpha

When I first used Google in 1998 I was amazed. The search experience was qualitatively different. All of my friends who I encouraged to use Google in 1998-1999 had the same experience. [WolframAlpha](http://www.wolframalpha.com) doesn’t seem to be in the same league. So the [NationMaster](http://www.nationmaster.com/index.php) bookmark stays. Google:WolframAlpha :: Michael Jordan:Harold “Baby Jordan” Miner? I hope not.
