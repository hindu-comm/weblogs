The paradox of relationship thrift?

*The Onion* presents an interesting spin on the financial retrenchment that’s in vogue right now. I do wonder it’s really the best for the real estate sector, as it seems like it would reduce aggregate demand for rental units. Also, note the shout out to [Robert Ingersoll](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Robert_G._Ingersoll).
