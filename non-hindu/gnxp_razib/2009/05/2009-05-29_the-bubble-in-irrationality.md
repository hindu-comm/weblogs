The bubble in irrationality

[Felix Salmon](http://blogs.reuters.com/felix-salmon/2009/05/28/what-use-economic-history/) pointed me to [The Myth of the Rational Market: A History of Risk, Reward, and Delusion on Wall Street](https://www.amazon.com/exec/obidos/ASIN/0060598999/geneexpressio-20/) today. There really is a boom in these sorts of books recently! Are we overdoing the “irrationality” bit? Probably. [Mike](http://scienceblogs.com/mikethemadbiologist/2009/05/behavioral_economics_not_every.php) offers up some skepticism about the creeping of [irrationality as an explanation for everything](http://scienceblogs.com/mikethemadbiologist/2009/05/behavioral_economics_not_every.php).
