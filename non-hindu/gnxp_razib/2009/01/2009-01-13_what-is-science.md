What is science?

Our local [White Male Patriarch](http://scienceblogs.com/builtonfacts/2009/01/what_is_science.php) has a post up, [What is science?](http://scienceblogs.com/builtonfacts/2009/01/what_is_science.php), where he offers a very succinct definition. I have no great disagreements with his definition, but I will add my own overly simple one just to offer another dimension from the perspective of a non-Patriarchal Person of Color:  
*Science is a culture*
