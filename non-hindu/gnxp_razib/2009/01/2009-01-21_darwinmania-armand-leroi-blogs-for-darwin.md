Darwinmania & Armand Leroi & Blogs for Darwin

Just noticed that [The BBC](http://www.bbc.co.uk/darwin/) has a [new site](http://www.bbc.co.uk/darwin/) all about Darwin! God bless the British television owners! Also, our old friend [Armand Leroi](https://www.gnxp.com/blog/2005/12/10-questions-for-armand-m-leroi.php) has a [program](http://www.bbc.co.uk/darwin/?tab=6&start_at=3) this Monday, [What Darwin Didn’t Know](http://www.bbc.co.uk/programmes/b00h6sbt). And Armand’s been busy, apparently he’s going to host a special for [National Geographic](http://test.pronetis.com/Ng.Uk2008/programmes/darwins-lost-voyage/schedule), [Darwin’s Lost Voyage](http://test.pronetis.com/Ng.Uk2008/programmes/darwins-lost-voyage/schedule). Also, remember about [Blogging For Darwin](http://citizenship.typepad.com/blogfordarwin/). I suspect I’ll participate over at *ScienceBlogs*…but I’m not going to unless I reread *Origin*, which I haven’t since I was about 12 years old when I didn’t know anything about evolution.
