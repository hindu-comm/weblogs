ResearchBlogCast on iTunes

[![resblog](https://i0.wp.com/blogs.discovermagazine.com/gnxp/files/2010/04/resblog.png?resize=125%2C123)![resblog](https://i0.wp.com/blogs.discovermagazine.com/gnxp/files/2010/04/resblog.png?resize=125%2C123)](http://researchblogging.org/news/?cat=8)[ResearchBlogCast](http://researchblogging.org/news/?cat=8) is now on iTunes. You can search for it under “ResearchBlogCast” in the iTunes store and subscribe, or, [just subscribe via this web page](http://itunes.apple.com/podcast/researchblogcast/id366632318). We’re talking about the [DASH diet](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/DASH_diet) next. Feel free to suggest ideas if you have anything clever, or want to hear our opinions on a specific topic. Just not something weird like Calvinist soteriology where there’s no chance that any of us are going to be able to follow the lingo.
