Open Thread – November 27th, 2010

Hope Thanksgiving went well for Americans. I didn’t gain weight at all, 142lbs as of Friday morning!

Out of curiosity, what fiction do you read?

Also, check out Dienekes [post on the ability to generate disjoint clusters in the DODECAD sample set](https://dienekes.blogspot.com/2010/11/clusters-galore-extremely-fine-scale.html). He asserts that one may now be able to generate extreme fine-scale assessments of likely population assignment from genetic material now. Check out this [table](https://1.bp.blogspot.com/_Ish7688voT0/TPF9i0f-qqI/AAAAAAAAC8c/heg9D1qd1fw/s1600/galore1.png); each column after the first two are the number of individuals in a given cluser. The rows are populations. The second column are the N individuals.

Last week’s [This American Life](http://www.thisamericanlife.org/radio-archives/episode/420/neighborhood-watch) had a segment on dog feces DNA fingerprinting. I think this is a good idea.
