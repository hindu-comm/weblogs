Denis Dutton, 1944-2010

[Professor, web entrepreneur Denis Dutton dies](http://www.stuff.co.nz/national/education/4498558/Professor-web-entrepreneur-Denis-Dutton-dies). Readers of this weblog probably know him for [Arts & Letters Daily](http://www.aldaily.com/) and [The Art Instinct](https://www.amazon.com/exec/obidos/ASIN/B004AYDB1M//geneexpressio-20). I never knew the man personally, but he made an impact on me through his website and articles. We shared friends, and I was proud when [Arts & Letters Daily](http://www.aldaily.com/) put this website on its blogroll, and sent us the occasional link. For me blogging is about ideas, not people, but when someone passes on those ideas are made flesh.
