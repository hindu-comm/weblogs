Humanity+ conference in Pasadena

[![humaplus](https://i0.wp.com/blogs.discovermagazine.com/gnxp/files/2010/12/humaplus.png?resize=279%2C108)![humaplus](https://i0.wp.com/blogs.discovermagazine.com/gnxp/files/2010/12/humaplus.png?resize=279%2C108)](http://humanityplus.org/conferences/program/)I was going to try and make it to the [Humanity+](http://humanityplus.org/conferences/) conference this year, but life intruded and the scheduling didn’t work out. Here’s the [program](http://humanityplus.org/conferences/program/). If you live in the LA area and this is your cup of tea, [registration still looks open](http://humanityplus10.eventbrite.com/). Also check out [H+ magazine](http://www.hplusmagazine.com/). I noticed that my friend [Michael Vassar](http://www.hplusmagazine.com/editors-blog/top-10-reasons-expect-next-10-years-be-more-exciting-last) seems more optimistic about the teens of the 21st century than he was a few years ago, [Top 10 Reasons to expect the next 10 years to be more exciting than the last](http://www.hplusmagazine.com/editors-blog/top-10-reasons-expect-next-10-years-be-more-exciting-last). I’m most excited by \#2 b the way, if it’s viable.
