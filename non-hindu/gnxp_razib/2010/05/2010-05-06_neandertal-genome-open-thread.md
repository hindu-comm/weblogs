Neandertal genome open thread

My thoughts on the topic are pretty disjointed, and I can’t come up with a post that adds anything to what others have already said. And there’s still the [primary documents](http://www.sciencemag.org/special/neandertal/) to digest in full. So I’m going to open this post up to stray thoughts/comments (though try to keep it at least at the level of a Neandertal cognitively). Question: what other human evolution story is of the same order of magnitude in terms of significance? I think the last one of this magnitude was the [Cann, et. al.](http://artsci.wustl.edu/~landc/html/cann/) “mitochondrial Eve” narrative of the mid-80s. Before that Lucy?
