Singularity Summit 2010

The [Singularity Summit](http://www.singularitysummit.com/) is going to happen in about a month in San Francisco (August 14th-15th). [Registration here](https://www.singularitysummit.com/registration/). Yes, [Ray Kurzweil](http://www.singularitysummit.com/program) will be there, but also [Irene Pepperberg](http://www.alexfoundation.org/dr_irene_pepperberg.html), [James Randi](http://www.randi.org/jr/) and [John Tooby](http://www.anth.ucsb.edu/faculty/tooby/). If you want to meet the ladies, probably *not* your scene (perhaps more accurately *the* lady, or two). But if you want to high five [Robin Hanson](http://www.nytimes.com/2010/07/11/magazine/11cryonics-t.html?_r=1&ref=magazine) at an after hours meet-up, get ready to party!

Here are my [reflections from last year](http://blogs.discovermagazine.com/gnxp/2009/10/singularity-summit-2009/).
