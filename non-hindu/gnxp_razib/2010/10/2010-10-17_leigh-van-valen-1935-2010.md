Leigh Van Valen, 1935-2010

[John Hawks](http://johnhawks.net/node/14930) is reporting that [Leigh Van Valen](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Leigh_Van_Valen) passed away this weekend. I had the pleasure and honor of Leigh Van Valen being an occasional reader of my musings. He would leave comments as “leigh.” Here’s [one](http://blogs.discovermagazine.com/gnxp/2008/02/the-structure-of-evolutionary-theory-blogging-chapter-5/#comment-8778) which he left in February 2008:

> Goldschmidt did a great deal more than hopeless monsters. I was with > Dobzhansky when he heard of Goldschmidt’s death. I’ve preserved his > comment: “A great geneticist has just died.”

Van Valen was the originator of the [Red Queen’s Hypothesis](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Red_Queen's_Hypothesis).
