DonorsChoose Challenge 2010 – It's On!

So to make a long story short last Saturday I was going to rev up this year’s [DonorsChoose](http://www.donorschoose.org/) but there were some technical difficulties which discouraged me, so I decided to point to other challenges. But my fellow [Discover blogger](http://blogs.discovermagazine.com/cosmicvariance) [Dr. Sean Carroll](http://blogs.discovermagazine.com/cosmicvariance/2010/10/12/donorschoose-challenge-2010/) has stepped up to the plate, so I decided to buck up and move forward. To the left under my headshot is the link to my [DonorsChoose Challenge 2010](http://www.donorschoose.org/donors/viewChallenge.html?id=78389&utm_source=BC08&utm_medium=widget&utm_content=GP&utm_campaign=78389&max=50). As usual I’ve selected mostly biology/gene related projects. **Let’s do this!**
