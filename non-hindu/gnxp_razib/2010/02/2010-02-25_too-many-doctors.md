Too many doctors!

[Mike the Mad Biologist](http://scienceblogs.com/mikethemadbiologist/2010/02/yes_we_have_a_phd_glut.php?utm_source=selectfeed&utm_medium=rss) has a post up, [Yes, We Have a PhD Glut…](http://scienceblogs.com/mikethemadbiologist/2010/02/yes_we_have_a_phd_glut.php?utm_source=selectfeed&utm_medium=rss) Which is interesting, because it isn’t has if [getting a doctorate is financially lucrative](http://wuphys.wustl.edu/~katz/scientist.html). Though [getting a medical doctorate is financially lucrative](http://www.payscale.com/research/US/People_with_Doctor_of_Medicine_(MD)_Degrees/Salary). Perhaps the medical profession has the right idea, [control labor supply](http://www.fsmb.org/)?\* Right idea for medical doctors at least….  
\* At least the medical profession hasn’t opened the floodgate and enabled the overproduction of those with MDs who will never practice the profession and so be saddled with a lot of debt, which is the case with the legal profession (and for those who do pass the bar and enter into the legal profession, there’s a [two-tiered pay scale](http://www.elsblog.org/the_empirical_legal_studi/2007/09/distribution-of.html)).
