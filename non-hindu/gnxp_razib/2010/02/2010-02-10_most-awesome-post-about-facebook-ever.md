Most awesome post about Facebook ever

At least in my book. [How to split up the US](http://petewarden.typepad.com/searchbrowser/2010/02/how-to-split-up-the-us.html). The author took social networking data and split up the United States into clusters. Here’s the map:  
![Ninenations.PNG](https://i0.wp.com/blogs.discovermagazine.com/gnxp/files/Ninenations.PNG?resize=464%2C231)![Ninenations.PNG](https://i0.wp.com/blogs.discovermagazine.com/gnxp/files/Ninenations.PNG?resize=464%2C231)  
Clicking through you can find the top fan pages of [various nations](http://fanpageanalytics.com/countryprofiles.html#countriestab). I noticed Megan Fox came up high on a list of many. She’s \#8 in Pakistan, and \#2 in India. \#7 in England. \#6 in Saudi Arabia! Isn’t that *haram*? \#1 in Canada, \#2 in Australia, \#6 in USA and \#4 in Mexico.
