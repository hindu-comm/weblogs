1000 Genomes data, 290,000 SNPs

To markthe release of the 1000 Genomes papers, here’s are [pedigree files](https://www.dropbox.com/s/nv3ckpm5hon12el/1000G290K.tar.gz?dl=0) with the 2,500 1000 Genomes samples. The 290,000 SNPs overlap with HGDP and other public SNP-chip data sets. The .fam has the population IDs. For what it’s work, I just used [plink 2](https://www.cog-genomics.org/plink2/input#vcf) to convert from VCF format.
