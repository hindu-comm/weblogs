New version of ADMIXTOOLS

Nick Patterson just emailed me to tell me that there’s a new version of ADMIXTOOLS available. Here’s the [page](https://github.com/DReichLab/AdmixTools). Of note, two new programs with minimal [documentation](https://github.com/DReichLab/AdmixTools/blob/master/pdoc.pdf), qWave and qpAdm. Combined with the data sets available it should be enough to allow motivated people to generate some results themselves….
