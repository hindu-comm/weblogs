Open Thread, 05/04/2020

[![](https://i0.wp.com/www.gnxp.com/WordPress/wp-content/uploads/2020/04/imperialtwilight.jpeg?resize=184%2C274&ssl=1)![](https://i0.wp.com/www.gnxp.com/WordPress/wp-content/uploads/2020/04/imperialtwilight.jpeg?resize=184%2C274&ssl=1)](https://www.amazon.com/exec/obidos/ASIN/B074YK9NHS/geneexpressio-20)[Imperial Twilight: The Opium War and the End of China’s Last Golden Age](https://www.amazon.com/exec/obidos/ASIN/B074YK9NHS/geneexpressio-20) is pretty good. It gives a blow-by-blow of the lead up to the Opium Wars. One gets a more visceral sense of why Chinese nationalists hate the West so much.

[Influenza: The Hundred-Year Hunt to Cure the 1918 Spanish Flu Pandemic](https://www.amazon.com/exec/obidos/ASIN/B078M56BNB/geneexpressio-20) and [Salt: A World History](https://www.amazon.com/exec/obidos/ASIN/B00BPDN33W/geneexpressio-20) are both Amazon Kindle deals. Recommended, especially the second.

[Understanding the Eneolithic steppe](https://eurogenes.blogspot.com/2020/05/understanding-eneolithic-steppe.html). If you are curious about ancient DNA and you aren’t reading David what’s wrong with you?

[The search for sexually antagonistic genes: Practical insights from studies of local adaptation and statistical genomics](https://www.biorxiv.org/content/10.1101/2020.04.30.071191v1?rss=1).

[Fast and accurate approximation of the joint site frequency spectrum of multiple populations](https://www.biorxiv.org/content/10.1101/2020.05.01.073213v1?rss=1).

[SARS-CoV-2 is well adapted for humans. What does this mean for re-emergence?](https://www.biorxiv.org/content/10.1101/2020.05.01.073262v1?rss=1)

[Multiethnic catalog of structural variants and their translational impact for disease phenotypes across 19,652 genomes](https://www.biorxiv.org/content/10.1101/2020.05.02.074096v1?rss=1).

[In a City Frozen in Fear, Time Freezes, Too](https://www.nytimes.com/2020/05/01/world/asia/india-coronavirus-delhi.html?action=click&module=News&pgtype=Homepage). Great images of Delhi during lockdown.

[Can Genetic Engineering Bring Back the American Chestnut?](https://www.nytimes.com/2020/04/30/magazine/american-chestnut.html?action=click&module=Top%20Stories&pgtype=Homepage)

[![](https://i0.wp.com/www.gnxp.com/WordPress/wp-content/uploads/2020/05/muhammadempires.jpeg?resize=185%2C273&ssl=1)![](https://i0.wp.com/www.gnxp.com/WordPress/wp-content/uploads/2020/05/muhammadempires.jpeg?resize=185%2C273&ssl=1)](https://www.amazon.com/exec/obidos/ASIN/B0844Q8CL2/geneexpressio-20)[Low-coverage sequencing cost-effectively detects known and novel variation in underrepresented populations](https://www.biorxiv.org/content/10.1101/2020.04.27.064832v1?rss=1). Low-coverage sequencing is getting competitive with arrays.

[China’s Factories Are Back. Its Consumers Aren’t.](https://www.nytimes.com/2020/04/28/business/china-coronavirus-economy.html) “Demand shock!”

[De novo mutation in ancestral generations evolves haplotypes contributing to disease](https://www.biorxiv.org/content/10.1101/2020.04.27.064824v1?rss=1).

[Cuomo’s N.Y. Reopening Plan: 10 Regions, 4 Phases, Many Caveats](https://www.wsj.com/articles/harvard-to-have-a-fall-semester-but-details-unclear-11588032333?mod=hp_lista_pos2).

Got [Muhammad and the Empires of Faith](https://www.amazon.com/exec/obidos/ASIN/B0844Q8CL2/geneexpressio-20).

On this weblog, I’ve expressed optimism about COVID-19. 45,000 deaths in the USA? Perhaps 85,000? Seems like I was too optimistic. It looks like we’ll go well over 100,000 thousand, and we don’t have a good roadmap for the future. Hopefully, something helps us.
