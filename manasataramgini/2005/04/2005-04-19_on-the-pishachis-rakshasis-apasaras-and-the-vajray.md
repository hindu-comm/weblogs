We had in our long wanderings encountered many a pishAchi, rAkSasi and apsarA. What we were fated to have we knew not. We were locked in the embrace of the vajrayoginI. We sunk into the ultimate intoxication of her ApIna-vakshAbyaH and gazed at her urUbhyaH with our third eye which was open in midst of the kR^iShNa rAtrI, even as mahAkAla danced the tANDava on our shikhA. She split herself into three forms and provided us pleasure in the midst of the kAlarAtrI and the yuddhachakra.
