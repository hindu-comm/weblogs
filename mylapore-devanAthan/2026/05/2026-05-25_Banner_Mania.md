# Banner Mania

### How can there be an intersection without Vijay's banner ?

Dear Chief Minister Vijay,

Vanakkam. Or it it Good Morning ? I am confused as you wear a trouser and coat in this sweltering Chennai heat.

Be that as it may, I recently came across an intersection in Mandaveli, Chennai that was incomplete. Yes, incomplete in the full sense of a four road intersection.

Yes. The intersection had 4 roads. Vehicles were plying on all the roads. And three sides of the intersection were complete. One was not.

Why this discrimination ?

Did you not promise that you would treat all as equal ? So, here is your first test of showing you would treat all as equal. Hence treat this intersection first.

Subscribe

While three sides of the intersection had your banners, the poor fourth one didn’t have. What wrong did that side do ? Is this the equality that Periyar, Ambedkar, Anna and Senkottaiyan Anna had taught us ?

So, please ensure that the poor side of the intersection, that has been discriminated for a long time since May 10, be taken care of and a banner of yours installed.

Earnestly awaiting your immediate action and redressal of this discrimination.

I remain

The Mylapore Inquirer.

Attachments : the three lucky sides with banners and the unlucky side without banner.
