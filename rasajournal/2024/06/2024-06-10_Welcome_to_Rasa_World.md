Hi and Namaste!

Rasa World is an attempt to find the rasa in culture around the world— be it tech, arts, history, popular culture, religion, philosophy, and more. It is a newsletter by Rasa Journal, where I will share my thoughts and insights.

We live in an incredibly fast-changing culture, owing to rapid advancements in tech that connects us not just across different cultures but also different areas of life. At the same time, we are also witnessing a decline of journalism and publishing as industries and hence, losing out on thoughtful commentary that might help us make sense of the world around us. With Rasa World, the idea is to look at the implications of a society which is both hyperconnected and isolated at the same time and what that might mean for culture around the world.   
  
As with the previous edition, Rasa World will be published only once or twice a month. Thank you for reading, as always, and please feel free to connect on [Twitter](https://twitter.com/rasajournal) or write at \[rasajournal@gmail.com\] about anything you’d like to share.
